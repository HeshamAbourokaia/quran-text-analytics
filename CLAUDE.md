## Writing style preferences

### Vivid comparison verbs

When rewriting or drafting text where comparisons matter, use this style:

> Rewrite this so the comparisons hit harder. Replace flat phrases like "is bigger than" or "is more important than" with one vivid physical verb (like dwarfs, eclipses, swallows, drowns out). Keep it plain English, no fancy words, one image per sentence.

Reference verbs: dwarfs, eclipses, overshadows, swallows, buries, drowns out, towers over, wipes out.

### Drafted messages (SMS, emails, listings replies)

- No em dashes or long dashes.
- No "AI breadcrumbs" (phrasing that reads as obviously AI-generated).
- Short paragraphs, plain Aussie-casual English.
