# Theme: Stories of the Prophets (قصص الأنبياء)

- **Scholarly color tag**: #F59E0B
- **Surah count**: 13

Surahs containing extended narratives of earlier prophets and their nations. Often used to comfort the Prophet Muhammad and warn his contemporaries by paralleling earlier patterns of rejection and salvation.
