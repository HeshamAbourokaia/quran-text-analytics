# Surah 53: An-Najm (النجم)

- **Mushaf number**: 53
- **Place of revelation**: Mecca (Makkiyah)
- **Verse count**: 62
- **Themes**: [Faith and Monotheism](../themes/faith-monotheism.md)
- **Named entities mentioned**: [Allah](../entities/divine_allah.md), [Ar-Rahim (the Most Merciful)](../entities/divine_ar-rahim.md), [Ar-Rahman (the Most Gracious)](../entities/divine_ar-rahman.md), [Abraham (Ibrahim)](../entities/prophet_ibrahim.md), [Moses (Musa)](../entities/prophet_musa.md), [Noah (Nuh)](../entities/prophet_nuh.md), [Rabb (the Lord)](../entities/divine_rabb.md)

- **Revelation order (chronological)**: #23 of 114
- **Revelation phase**: [Early Meccan](../timeline/phase_early-mecca.md) (610-615 CE, Year 1-5 of Prophethood)

## Verses

### Verse 53:0
**Arabic**: بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

**English**: In the name of God, the Gracious, the Merciful.

### Verse 53:1
**Arabic**: وَٱلنَّجْمِ إِذَا هَوَىٰ

**English**: By the star as it goes down.

### Verse 53:2
**Arabic**: مَا ضَلَّ صَاحِبُكُمْ وَمَا غَوَىٰ

**English**: Your friend has not gone astray, nor has he erred.

### Verse 53:3
**Arabic**: وَمَا يَنطِقُ عَنِ ٱلْهَوَىٰٓ

**English**: Nor does he speak out of desire.

### Verse 53:4
**Arabic**: إِنْ هُوَ إِلَّا وَحْىٌۭ يُوحَىٰ

**English**: It is but a revelation revealed.

### Verse 53:5
**Arabic**: عَلَّمَهُۥ شَدِيدُ ٱلْقُوَىٰ

**English**: Taught to him by the Extremely Powerful.

### Verse 53:6
**Arabic**: ذُو مِرَّةٍۢ فَٱسْتَوَىٰ

**English**: The one of vigor. He settled.

### Verse 53:7
**Arabic**: وَهُوَ بِٱلْأُفُقِ ٱلْأَعْلَىٰ

**English**: While he was at the highest horizon.

### Verse 53:8
**Arabic**: ثُمَّ دَنَا فَتَدَلَّىٰ

**English**: Then he came near, and hovered around.

### Verse 53:9
**Arabic**: فَكَانَ قَابَ قَوْسَيْنِ أَوْ أَدْنَىٰ

**English**: He was within two bows' length, or closer.

### Verse 53:10
**Arabic**: فَأَوْحَىٰٓ إِلَىٰ عَبْدِهِۦ مَآ أَوْحَىٰ

**English**: Then He revealed to His servant what He revealed.

### Verse 53:11
**Arabic**: مَا كَذَبَ ٱلْفُؤَادُ مَا رَأَىٰٓ

**English**: The heart did not lie about what it saw.

### Verse 53:12
**Arabic**: أَفَتُمَٰرُونَهُۥ عَلَىٰ مَا يَرَىٰ

**English**: Will you dispute with him concerning what he saw?

### Verse 53:13
**Arabic**: وَلَقَدْ رَءَاهُ نَزْلَةً أُخْرَىٰ

**English**: He saw him on another descent.

### Verse 53:14
**Arabic**: عِندَ سِدْرَةِ ٱلْمُنتَهَىٰ

**English**: At the Lotus Tree of the Extremity.

### Verse 53:15
**Arabic**: عِندَهَا جَنَّةُ ٱلْمَأْوَىٰٓ

**English**: Near which is the Garden of Repose.

### Verse 53:16
**Arabic**: إِذْ يَغْشَى ٱلسِّدْرَةَ مَا يَغْشَىٰ

**English**: As there covered the Lotus Tree what covered it.

### Verse 53:17
**Arabic**: مَا زَاغَ ٱلْبَصَرُ وَمَا طَغَىٰ

**English**: The sight did not waver, nor did it exceed.

### Verse 53:18
**Arabic**: لَقَدْ رَأَىٰ مِنْ ءَايَٰتِ رَبِّهِ ٱلْكُبْرَىٰٓ

**English**: He saw some of the Great Signs of his Lord.

### Verse 53:19
**Arabic**: أَفَرَءَيْتُمُ ٱللَّٰتَ وَٱلْعُزَّىٰ

**English**: Have you considered al-Lat and al-Uzza?

### Verse 53:20
**Arabic**: وَمَنَوٰةَ ٱلثَّالِثَةَ ٱلْأُخْرَىٰٓ

**English**: And Manat, the third one, the other?

### Verse 53:21
**Arabic**: أَلَكُمُ ٱلذَّكَرُ وَلَهُ ٱلْأُنثَىٰ

**English**: Are you to have the males, and He the females?

### Verse 53:22
**Arabic**: تِلْكَ إِذًۭا قِسْمَةٌۭ ضِيزَىٰٓ

**English**: What a bizarre distribution.

### Verse 53:23
**Arabic**: إِنْ هِىَ إِلَّآ أَسْمَآءٌۭ سَمَّيْتُمُوهَآ أَنتُمْ وَءَابَآؤُكُم مَّآ أَنزَلَ ٱللَّهُ بِهَا مِن سُلْطَٰنٍ ۚ إِن يَتَّبِعُونَ إِلَّا ٱلظَّنَّ وَمَا تَهْوَى ٱلْأَنفُسُ ۖ وَلَقَدْ جَآءَهُم مِّن رَّبِّهِمُ ٱلْهُدَىٰٓ

**English**: These are nothing but names, which you have devised, you and your ancestors, for which God sent down no authority. They follow nothing but assumptions, and what the ego desires, even though guidance has come to them from their Lord.

### Verse 53:24
**Arabic**: أَمْ لِلْإِنسَٰنِ مَا تَمَنَّىٰ

**English**: Or is the human being to have whatever he desires?

### Verse 53:25
**Arabic**: فَلِلَّهِ ٱلْءَاخِرَةُ وَٱلْأُولَىٰ

**English**: To God belong the Last and the First.

### Verse 53:26
**Arabic**: ۞ وَكَم مِّن مَّلَكٍۢ فِى ٱلسَّمَٰوَٰتِ لَا تُغْنِى شَفَٰعَتُهُمْ شَيْـًٔا إِلَّا مِنۢ بَعْدِ أَن يَأْذَنَ ٱللَّهُ لِمَن يَشَآءُ وَيَرْضَىٰٓ

**English**: How many an angel is there in the heavens whose intercession avails nothing, except after God gives permission to whomever He wills, and approves?

### Verse 53:27
**Arabic**: إِنَّ ٱلَّذِينَ لَا يُؤْمِنُونَ بِٱلْءَاخِرَةِ لَيُسَمُّونَ ٱلْمَلَٰٓئِكَةَ تَسْمِيَةَ ٱلْأُنثَىٰ

**English**: Those who do not believe in the Hereafter give the angels the names of females.

### Verse 53:28
**Arabic**: وَمَا لَهُم بِهِۦ مِنْ عِلْمٍ ۖ إِن يَتَّبِعُونَ إِلَّا ٱلظَّنَّ ۖ وَإِنَّ ٱلظَّنَّ لَا يُغْنِى مِنَ ٱلْحَقِّ شَيْـًۭٔا

**English**: They have no knowledge of that. They only follow assumptions, and assumptions are no substitute for the truth.

### Verse 53:29
**Arabic**: فَأَعْرِضْ عَن مَّن تَوَلَّىٰ عَن ذِكْرِنَا وَلَمْ يُرِدْ إِلَّا ٱلْحَيَوٰةَ ٱلدُّنْيَا

**English**: So avoid him who has turned away from Our remembrance, and desires nothing but the present life.

### Verse 53:30
**Arabic**: ذَٰلِكَ مَبْلَغُهُم مِّنَ ٱلْعِلْمِ ۚ إِنَّ رَبَّكَ هُوَ أَعْلَمُ بِمَن ضَلَّ عَن سَبِيلِهِۦ وَهُوَ أَعْلَمُ بِمَنِ ٱهْتَدَىٰ

**English**: That is the extent of their knowledge. Your Lord knows best who has strayed from His path, and He knows best who has accepted guidance.

### Verse 53:31
**Arabic**: وَلِلَّهِ مَا فِى ٱلسَّمَٰوَٰتِ وَمَا فِى ٱلْأَرْضِ لِيَجْزِىَ ٱلَّذِينَ أَسَٰٓـُٔوا۟ بِمَا عَمِلُوا۟ وَيَجْزِىَ ٱلَّذِينَ أَحْسَنُوا۟ بِٱلْحُسْنَى

**English**: To God belongs whatever is in the heavens and whatever is on earth. He will repay those who do evil according to their deeds, and recompense those who do good with the best.

### Verse 53:32
**Arabic**: ٱلَّذِينَ يَجْتَنِبُونَ كَبَٰٓئِرَ ٱلْإِثْمِ وَٱلْفَوَٰحِشَ إِلَّا ٱللَّمَمَ ۚ إِنَّ رَبَّكَ وَٰسِعُ ٱلْمَغْفِرَةِ ۚ هُوَ أَعْلَمُ بِكُمْ إِذْ أَنشَأَكُم مِّنَ ٱلْأَرْضِ وَإِذْ أَنتُمْ أَجِنَّةٌۭ فِى بُطُونِ أُمَّهَٰتِكُمْ ۖ فَلَا تُزَكُّوٓا۟ أَنفُسَكُمْ ۖ هُوَ أَعْلَمُ بِمَنِ ٱتَّقَىٰٓ

**English**: Those who avoid gross sins and indecencies-except for minor lapses-your Lord is of Vast Forgiveness. He knows you well, ever since He created you from the earth, and ever since you were embryos in your mothers' wombs. So do not acclaim your own virtue; He is fully aware of the righteous.

### Verse 53:33
**Arabic**: أَفَرَءَيْتَ ٱلَّذِى تَوَلَّىٰ

**English**: Have you considered him who turned away?

### Verse 53:34
**Arabic**: وَأَعْطَىٰ قَلِيلًۭا وَأَكْدَىٰٓ

**English**: And gave a little, and held back?

### Verse 53:35
**Arabic**: أَعِندَهُۥ عِلْمُ ٱلْغَيْبِ فَهُوَ يَرَىٰٓ

**English**: Does he possess knowledge of the unseen, and can therefore foresee?

### Verse 53:36
**Arabic**: أَمْ لَمْ يُنَبَّأْ بِمَا فِى صُحُفِ مُوسَىٰ

**English**: Or was he not informed of what is in the Scrolls of Moses?

### Verse 53:37
**Arabic**: وَإِبْرَٰهِيمَ ٱلَّذِى وَفَّىٰٓ

**English**: And of Abraham, who fulfilled?

### Verse 53:38
**Arabic**: أَلَّا تَزِرُ وَازِرَةٌۭ وِزْرَ أُخْرَىٰ

**English**: That no soul bears the burdens of another soul.

### Verse 53:39
**Arabic**: وَأَن لَّيْسَ لِلْإِنسَٰنِ إِلَّا مَا سَعَىٰ

**English**: And that the human being attains only what he strives for.

### Verse 53:40
**Arabic**: وَأَنَّ سَعْيَهُۥ سَوْفَ يُرَىٰ

**English**: And that his efforts will be witnessed.

### Verse 53:41
**Arabic**: ثُمَّ يُجْزَىٰهُ ٱلْجَزَآءَ ٱلْأَوْفَىٰ

**English**: Then he will be rewarded for it the fullest reward.

### Verse 53:42
**Arabic**: وَأَنَّ إِلَىٰ رَبِّكَ ٱلْمُنتَهَىٰ

**English**: And that to your Lord is the finality.

### Verse 53:43
**Arabic**: وَأَنَّهُۥ هُوَ أَضْحَكَ وَأَبْكَىٰ

**English**: And that it is He who causes laughter and weeping.

### Verse 53:44
**Arabic**: وَأَنَّهُۥ هُوَ أَمَاتَ وَأَحْيَا

**English**: And that it is He who gives death and life.

### Verse 53:45
**Arabic**: وَأَنَّهُۥ خَلَقَ ٱلزَّوْجَيْنِ ٱلذَّكَرَ وَٱلْأُنثَىٰ

**English**: And that it is He who created the two kinds-the male and the female.

### Verse 53:46
**Arabic**: مِن نُّطْفَةٍ إِذَا تُمْنَىٰ

**English**: From a sperm drop, when emitted.

### Verse 53:47
**Arabic**: وَأَنَّ عَلَيْهِ ٱلنَّشْأَةَ ٱلْأُخْرَىٰ

**English**: And that upon Him is the next existence.

### Verse 53:48
**Arabic**: وَأَنَّهُۥ هُوَ أَغْنَىٰ وَأَقْنَىٰ

**English**: And that it is He who enriches and impoverishes.

### Verse 53:49
**Arabic**: وَأَنَّهُۥ هُوَ رَبُّ ٱلشِّعْرَىٰ

**English**: And that it is He who is the Lord of Sirius.

### Verse 53:50
**Arabic**: وَأَنَّهُۥٓ أَهْلَكَ عَادًا ٱلْأُولَىٰ

**English**: And that it is He who destroyed the first Aad.

### Verse 53:51
**Arabic**: وَثَمُودَا۟ فَمَآ أَبْقَىٰ

**English**: And Thamood, sparing no one.

### Verse 53:52
**Arabic**: وَقَوْمَ نُوحٍۢ مِّن قَبْلُ ۖ إِنَّهُمْ كَانُوا۟ هُمْ أَظْلَمَ وَأَطْغَىٰ

**English**: And the people of Noah before that; for they were most unjust and most oppressive.

### Verse 53:53
**Arabic**: وَٱلْمُؤْتَفِكَةَ أَهْوَىٰ

**English**: And He toppled the ruined cities.

### Verse 53:54
**Arabic**: فَغَشَّىٰهَا مَا غَشَّىٰ

**English**: And covered them with whatever covered them.

### Verse 53:55
**Arabic**: فَبِأَىِّ ءَالَآءِ رَبِّكَ تَتَمَارَىٰ

**English**: So which of your Lord's marvels can you deny?

### Verse 53:56
**Arabic**: هَٰذَا نَذِيرٌۭ مِّنَ ٱلنُّذُرِ ٱلْأُولَىٰٓ

**English**: This is a warning, just like the first warnings.

### Verse 53:57
**Arabic**: أَزِفَتِ ٱلْءَازِفَةُ

**English**: The inevitable is imminent.

### Verse 53:58
**Arabic**: لَيْسَ لَهَا مِن دُونِ ٱللَّهِ كَاشِفَةٌ

**English**: None besides God can unveil it.

### Verse 53:59
**Arabic**: أَفَمِنْ هَٰذَا ٱلْحَدِيثِ تَعْجَبُونَ

**English**: Do you marvel at this discourse?

### Verse 53:60
**Arabic**: وَتَضْحَكُونَ وَلَا تَبْكُونَ

**English**: And laugh, and do not weep?

### Verse 53:61
**Arabic**: وَأَنتُمْ سَٰمِدُونَ

**English**: Lost in your frivolity?

### Verse 53:62
**Arabic**: فَٱسْجُدُوا۟ لِلَّهِ وَٱعْبُدُوا۟ ۩

**English**: So bow down to God, and worship!
