# Surah 55: Ar-Rahman (الرحمن)

- **Mushaf number**: 55
- **Place of revelation**: Medina (Madaniyah)
- **Verse count**: 78
- **Themes**: [Worship and Devotion](../themes/worship.md)
- **Named entities mentioned**: [Allah](../entities/divine_allah.md), [Ar-Rahim (the Most Merciful)](../entities/divine_ar-rahim.md), [Ar-Rahman (the Most Gracious)](../entities/divine_ar-rahman.md), [Rabb (the Lord)](../entities/divine_rabb.md)

**Arabic**: بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

**English**: The sun and the moon move according to plan.

**Arabic**: وَٱلنَّجْمُ وَٱلشَّجَرُ يَسْجُدَانِ

**English**: And the stars and the trees prostrate themselves.

**Arabic**: وَٱلسَّمَآءَ رَفَعَهَا وَوَضَعَ ٱلْمِيزَانَ

**English**: But maintain the weights with justice, and do not violate the balance.

**English**: And the earth; He set up for the creatures.
