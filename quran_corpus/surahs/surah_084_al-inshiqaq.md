# Surah 84: Al-Inshiqaq (الإنشقاق)

- **Mushaf number**: 84
- **Place of revelation**: Mecca (Makkiyah)
- **Verse count**: 25
- **Themes**: [Day of Judgment](../themes/day-of-judgment.md)
- **Named entities mentioned**: [Allah](../entities/divine_allah.md), [Ar-Rahim (the Most Merciful)](../entities/divine_ar-rahim.md), [Ar-Rahman (the Most Gracious)](../entities/divine_ar-rahman.md), [Rabb (the Lord)](../entities/divine_rabb.md)

## Verses

### Verse 84:0
**Arabic**: بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

**English**: In the name of God, the Gracious, the Merciful.

### Verse 84:1
**Arabic**: إِذَا ٱلسَّمَآءُ ٱنشَقَّتْ

**English**: When the sky is ruptured.

### Verse 84:2
**Arabic**: وَأَذِنَتْ لِرَبِّهَا وَحُقَّتْ

**English**: And hearkens to its Lord, as it must.

### Verse 84:3
**Arabic**: وَإِذَا ٱلْأَرْضُ مُدَّتْ

**English**: And when the earth is leveled out.

### Verse 84:4
**Arabic**: وَأَلْقَتْ مَا فِيهَا وَتَخَلَّتْ

**English**: And casts out what is in it, and becomes empty.

### Verse 84:5
**Arabic**: وَأَذِنَتْ لِرَبِّهَا وَحُقَّتْ

**English**: And hearkens to its Lord, as it must.

### Verse 84:6
**Arabic**: يَٰٓأَيُّهَا ٱلْإِنسَٰنُ إِنَّكَ كَادِحٌ إِلَىٰ رَبِّكَ كَدْحًۭا فَمُلَٰقِيهِ

**English**: O man! You are laboring towards your Lord, and you will meet Him.

### Verse 84:7
**Arabic**: فَأَمَّا مَنْ أُوتِىَ كِتَٰبَهُۥ بِيَمِينِهِۦ

**English**: As for him who is given his book in his right hand.

### Verse 84:8
**Arabic**: فَسَوْفَ يُحَاسَبُ حِسَابًۭا يَسِيرًۭا

**English**: He will have an easy settlement.

### Verse 84:9
**Arabic**: وَيَنقَلِبُ إِلَىٰٓ أَهْلِهِۦ مَسْرُورًۭا

**English**: And will return to his family delighted.

### Verse 84:10
**Arabic**: وَأَمَّا مَنْ أُوتِىَ كِتَٰبَهُۥ وَرَآءَ ظَهْرِهِۦ

**English**: But as for him who is given his book behind his back.

### Verse 84:11
**Arabic**: فَسَوْفَ يَدْعُوا۟ ثُبُورًۭا

**English**: He will call for death.

### Verse 84:12
**Arabic**: وَيَصْلَىٰ سَعِيرًا

**English**: And will enter the Blaze.

### Verse 84:13
**Arabic**: إِنَّهُۥ كَانَ فِىٓ أَهْلِهِۦ مَسْرُورًا

**English**: He used to be happy among his family.

### Verse 84:14
**Arabic**: إِنَّهُۥ ظَنَّ أَن لَّن يَحُورَ

**English**: He thought he would never return.

### Verse 84:15
**Arabic**: بَلَىٰٓ إِنَّ رَبَّهُۥ كَانَ بِهِۦ بَصِيرًۭا

**English**: In fact, his Lord was watching him.

### Verse 84:16
**Arabic**: فَلَآ أُقْسِمُ بِٱلشَّفَقِ

**English**: I swear by the twilight.

### Verse 84:17
**Arabic**: وَٱلَّيْلِ وَمَا وَسَقَ

**English**: And by the night, and what it covers.

### Verse 84:18
**Arabic**: وَٱلْقَمَرِ إِذَا ٱتَّسَقَ

**English**: And by the moon, as it grows full.

### Verse 84:19
**Arabic**: لَتَرْكَبُنَّ طَبَقًا عَن طَبَقٍۢ

**English**: You will mount stage by stage.

### Verse 84:20
**Arabic**: فَمَا لَهُمْ لَا يُؤْمِنُونَ

**English**: What is the matter with them that they do not believe?

### Verse 84:21
**Arabic**: وَإِذَا قُرِئَ عَلَيْهِمُ ٱلْقُرْءَانُ لَا يَسْجُدُونَ ۩

**English**: And when the Quran is read to them, they do not bow down?

### Verse 84:22
**Arabic**: بَلِ ٱلَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا۟ يُكَذِّبُونَ

**English**: In fact, those who disbelieve are in denial.

### Verse 84:23
**Arabic**: وَٱللَّهُ أَعْلَمُ بِمَا يُوعُونَ

**English**: But God knows what they hide inside.

### Verse 84:24
**Arabic**: فَبَشِّرْهُم بِعَذَابٍ أَلِيمٍ

**English**: So inform them of a painful punishment.

### Verse 84:25
**Arabic**: إِلَّا ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوا۟ وَعَمِلُوا۟ ٱلصَّٰلِحَٰتِ لَهُمْ أَجْرٌ غَيْرُ مَمْنُونٍۭ

**English**: Except those who believe and do good deeds; they will have an undiminished reward.
