# Surah 51: Az-Zariyat (الذاريات)

- **Mushaf number**: 51
- **Place of revelation**: Mecca (Makkiyah)
- **Verse count**: 60
- **Themes**: [Warning](../themes/warning.md)
- **Named entities mentioned**: [Allah](../entities/divine_allah.md), [Ar-Rahim (the Most Merciful)](../entities/divine_ar-rahim.md), [Ar-Rahman (the Most Gracious)](../entities/divine_ar-rahman.md), [Pharaoh (Firaun)](../entities/figure_firaun.md), [Abraham (Ibrahim)](../entities/prophet_ibrahim.md), [Moses (Musa)](../entities/prophet_musa.md), [Noah (Nuh)](../entities/prophet_nuh.md), [Rabb (the Lord)](../entities/divine_rabb.md)

## Verses

### Verse 51:0
**Arabic**: بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

**English**: In the name of God, the Gracious, the Merciful.

### Verse 51:1
**Arabic**: وَٱلذَّٰرِيَٰتِ ذَرْوًۭا

**English**: By the spreaders spreading.

### Verse 51:2
**Arabic**: فَٱلْحَٰمِلَٰتِ وِقْرًۭا

**English**: And those carrying loads.

### Verse 51:3
**Arabic**: فَٱلْجَٰرِيَٰتِ يُسْرًۭا

**English**: And those moving gently.

### Verse 51:4
**Arabic**: فَٱلْمُقَسِّمَٰتِ أَمْرًا

**English**: And those distributing as commanded.

### Verse 51:5
**Arabic**: إِنَّمَا تُوعَدُونَ لَصَادِقٌۭ

**English**: What you are promised is true.

### Verse 51:6
**Arabic**: وَإِنَّ ٱلدِّينَ لَوَٰقِعٌۭ

**English**: Judgment will take place.

### Verse 51:7
**Arabic**: وَٱلسَّمَآءِ ذَاتِ ٱلْحُبُكِ

**English**: By the sky that is woven.

### Verse 51:8
**Arabic**: إِنَّكُمْ لَفِى قَوْلٍۢ مُّخْتَلِفٍۢ

**English**: You differ in what you say.

### Verse 51:9
**Arabic**: يُؤْفَكُ عَنْهُ مَنْ أُفِكَ

**English**: Averted from it is he who is averted.

### Verse 51:10
**Arabic**: قُتِلَ ٱلْخَرَّٰصُونَ

**English**: Perish the imposters.

### Verse 51:11
**Arabic**: ٱلَّذِينَ هُمْ فِى غَمْرَةٍۢ سَاهُونَ

**English**: Those who are dazed in ignorance.

### Verse 51:12
**Arabic**: يَسْـَٔلُونَ أَيَّانَ يَوْمُ ٱلدِّينِ

**English**: They ask, "When is the Day of Judgment?"

### Verse 51:13
**Arabic**: يَوْمَ هُمْ عَلَى ٱلنَّارِ يُفْتَنُونَ

**English**: The Day they are presented to the Fire.

### Verse 51:14
**Arabic**: ذُوقُوا۟ فِتْنَتَكُمْ هَٰذَا ٱلَّذِى كُنتُم بِهِۦ تَسْتَعْجِلُونَ

**English**: "Taste your ordeal. This is what you used to challenge."

### Verse 51:15
**Arabic**: إِنَّ ٱلْمُتَّقِينَ فِى جَنَّٰتٍۢ وَعُيُونٍ

**English**: But the pious are amidst gardens and springs.

### Verse 51:16
**Arabic**: ءَاخِذِينَ مَآ ءَاتَىٰهُمْ رَبُّهُمْ ۚ إِنَّهُمْ كَانُوا۟ قَبْلَ ذَٰلِكَ مُحْسِنِينَ

**English**: Receiving what their Lord has given them. They were virtuous before that.

### Verse 51:17
**Arabic**: كَانُوا۟ قَلِيلًۭا مِّنَ ٱلَّيْلِ مَا يَهْجَعُونَ

**English**: They used to sleep a little at night.

### Verse 51:18
**Arabic**: وَبِٱلْأَسْحَارِ هُمْ يَسْتَغْفِرُونَ

**English**: And at dawn, they would pray for pardon.

### Verse 51:19
**Arabic**: وَفِىٓ أَمْوَٰلِهِمْ حَقٌّۭ لِّلسَّآئِلِ وَٱلْمَحْرُومِ

**English**: And in their wealth, there was a share for the beggar and the deprived.

### Verse 51:20
**Arabic**: وَفِى ٱلْأَرْضِ ءَايَٰتٌۭ لِّلْمُوقِنِينَ

**English**: And on earth are signs for the convinced.

### Verse 51:21
**Arabic**: وَفِىٓ أَنفُسِكُمْ ۚ أَفَلَا تُبْصِرُونَ

**English**: And within yourselves. Do you not see?

### Verse 51:22
**Arabic**: وَفِى ٱلسَّمَآءِ رِزْقُكُمْ وَمَا تُوعَدُونَ

**English**: And in the heaven is your livelihood, and what you are promised.

### Verse 51:23
**Arabic**: فَوَرَبِّ ٱلسَّمَآءِ وَٱلْأَرْضِ إِنَّهُۥ لَحَقٌّۭ مِّثْلَ مَآ أَنَّكُمْ تَنطِقُونَ

**English**: By the Lord of the heaven and the earth, it is as true as the fact that you speak.

### Verse 51:24
**Arabic**: هَلْ أَتَىٰكَ حَدِيثُ ضَيْفِ إِبْرَٰهِيمَ ٱلْمُكْرَمِينَ

**English**: Has the story of Abraham's honorable guests reached you?

### Verse 51:25
**Arabic**: إِذْ دَخَلُوا۟ عَلَيْهِ فَقَالُوا۟ سَلَٰمًۭا ۖ قَالَ سَلَٰمٌۭ قَوْمٌۭ مُّنكَرُونَ

**English**: When they entered upon him, they said, "Peace." He said, "Peace, strangers."

### Verse 51:26
**Arabic**: فَرَاغَ إِلَىٰٓ أَهْلِهِۦ فَجَآءَ بِعِجْلٍۢ سَمِينٍۢ

**English**: Then he slipped away to his family, and brought a fatted calf.

### Verse 51:27
**Arabic**: فَقَرَّبَهُۥٓ إِلَيْهِمْ قَالَ أَلَا تَأْكُلُونَ

**English**: He set it before them. He said, "Will you not eat?"

### Verse 51:28
**Arabic**: فَأَوْجَسَ مِنْهُمْ خِيفَةًۭ ۖ قَالُوا۟ لَا تَخَفْ ۖ وَبَشَّرُوهُ بِغُلَٰمٍ عَلِيمٍۢ

**English**: And he harbored fear of them. They said, "Do not fear," and they announced to him the good news of a knowledgeable boy.

### Verse 51:29
**Arabic**: فَأَقْبَلَتِ ٱمْرَأَتُهُۥ فِى صَرَّةٍۢ فَصَكَّتْ وَجْهَهَا وَقَالَتْ عَجُوزٌ عَقِيمٌۭ

**English**: His wife came forward crying. She clasped her face, and said, "A barren old woman?"

### Verse 51:30
**Arabic**: قَالُوا۟ كَذَٰلِكِ قَالَ رَبُّكِ ۖ إِنَّهُۥ هُوَ ٱلْحَكِيمُ ٱلْعَلِيمُ

**English**: They said, "Thus spoke your Lord. He is the Wise, the Knowing."

### Verse 51:31
**Arabic**: ۞ قَالَ فَمَا خَطْبُكُمْ أَيُّهَا ٱلْمُرْسَلُونَ

**English**: He said, "What is your business, O envoys?"

### Verse 51:32
**Arabic**: قَالُوٓا۟ إِنَّآ أُرْسِلْنَآ إِلَىٰ قَوْمٍۢ مُّجْرِمِينَ

**English**: They said, "We are sent to a people guilty of sin."

### Verse 51:33
**Arabic**: لِنُرْسِلَ عَلَيْهِمْ حِجَارَةًۭ مِّن طِينٍۢ

**English**: "To unleash upon them rocks of clay."

### Verse 51:34
**Arabic**: مُّسَوَّمَةً عِندَ رَبِّكَ لِلْمُسْرِفِينَ

**English**: "Marked by your Lord for the excessive."

### Verse 51:35
**Arabic**: فَأَخْرَجْنَا مَن كَانَ فِيهَا مِنَ ٱلْمُؤْمِنِينَ

**English**: We evacuated all the believers who were in it.

### Verse 51:36
**Arabic**: فَمَا وَجَدْنَا فِيهَا غَيْرَ بَيْتٍۢ مِّنَ ٱلْمُسْلِمِينَ

**English**: But found in it only one household of Muslims.

### Verse 51:37
**Arabic**: وَتَرَكْنَا فِيهَآ ءَايَةًۭ لِّلَّذِينَ يَخَافُونَ ٱلْعَذَابَ ٱلْأَلِيمَ

**English**: And We left in it a sign for those who fear the painful punishment.

### Verse 51:38
**Arabic**: وَفِى مُوسَىٰٓ إِذْ أَرْسَلْنَٰهُ إِلَىٰ فِرْعَوْنَ بِسُلْطَٰنٍۢ مُّبِينٍۢ

**English**: And in Moses. We sent him to Pharaoh with a clear authority.

### Verse 51:39
**Arabic**: فَتَوَلَّىٰ بِرُكْنِهِۦ وَقَالَ سَٰحِرٌ أَوْ مَجْنُونٌۭ

**English**: But he turned away with his warlords, and said, "A sorcerer or a madman."

### Verse 51:40
**Arabic**: فَأَخَذْنَٰهُ وَجُنُودَهُۥ فَنَبَذْنَٰهُمْ فِى ٱلْيَمِّ وَهُوَ مُلِيمٌۭ

**English**: So We seized him and his troops, and threw them into the sea, and He was to blame.

### Verse 51:41
**Arabic**: وَفِى عَادٍ إِذْ أَرْسَلْنَا عَلَيْهِمُ ٱلرِّيحَ ٱلْعَقِيمَ

**English**: And in Aad. We unleashed against them the devastating wind.

### Verse 51:42
**Arabic**: مَا تَذَرُ مِن شَىْءٍ أَتَتْ عَلَيْهِ إِلَّا جَعَلَتْهُ كَٱلرَّمِيمِ

**English**: It spared nothing it came upon, but rendered it like decayed ruins.

### Verse 51:43
**Arabic**: وَفِى ثَمُودَ إِذْ قِيلَ لَهُمْ تَمَتَّعُوا۟ حَتَّىٰ حِينٍۢ

**English**: And in Thamood. They were told, "Enjoy yourselves for a while."

### Verse 51:44
**Arabic**: فَعَتَوْا۟ عَنْ أَمْرِ رَبِّهِمْ فَأَخَذَتْهُمُ ٱلصَّٰعِقَةُ وَهُمْ يَنظُرُونَ

**English**: But they defied the command of their Lord, so the lightning struck them as they looked on.

### Verse 51:45
**Arabic**: فَمَا ٱسْتَطَٰعُوا۟ مِن قِيَامٍۢ وَمَا كَانُوا۟ مُنتَصِرِينَ

**English**: They could not rise up, nor could they find help.

### Verse 51:46
**Arabic**: وَقَوْمَ نُوحٍۢ مِّن قَبْلُ ۖ إِنَّهُمْ كَانُوا۟ قَوْمًۭا فَٰسِقِينَ

**English**: And before that, the people of Noah. They were immoral people.

### Verse 51:47
**Arabic**: وَٱلسَّمَآءَ بَنَيْنَٰهَا بِأَيْي۟دٍۢ وَإِنَّا لَمُوسِعُونَ

**English**: We constructed the universe with power, and We are expanding it.

### Verse 51:48
**Arabic**: وَٱلْأَرْضَ فَرَشْنَٰهَا فَنِعْمَ ٱلْمَٰهِدُونَ

**English**: And the earth-We spread it out-How well We prepared it!

### Verse 51:49
**Arabic**: وَمِن كُلِّ شَىْءٍ خَلَقْنَا زَوْجَيْنِ لَعَلَّكُمْ تَذَكَّرُونَ

**English**: We created all things in pairs, so that you may reflect and ponder.

### Verse 51:50
**Arabic**: فَفِرُّوٓا۟ إِلَى ٱللَّهِ ۖ إِنِّى لَكُم مِّنْهُ نَذِيرٌۭ مُّبِينٌۭ

**English**: "So flee towards God. I am to you from Him a clear warner."

### Verse 51:51
**Arabic**: وَلَا تَجْعَلُوا۟ مَعَ ٱللَّهِ إِلَٰهًا ءَاخَرَ ۖ إِنِّى لَكُم مِّنْهُ نَذِيرٌۭ مُّبِينٌۭ

**English**: "And do not set up any other god with God. I am to you from Him a clear warner."

### Verse 51:52
**Arabic**: كَذَٰلِكَ مَآ أَتَى ٱلَّذِينَ مِن قَبْلِهِم مِّن رَّسُولٍ إِلَّا قَالُوا۟ سَاحِرٌ أَوْ مَجْنُونٌ

**English**: Likewise, no messenger came to those before them, but they said, "A sorcerer or a madman."

### Verse 51:53
**Arabic**: أَتَوَاصَوْا۟ بِهِۦ ۚ بَلْ هُمْ قَوْمٌۭ طَاغُونَ

**English**: Did they recommend it to one another? In fact, they are rebellious people.

### Verse 51:54
**Arabic**: فَتَوَلَّ عَنْهُمْ فَمَآ أَنتَ بِمَلُومٍۢ

**English**: So turn away from them; you are not to blame.

### Verse 51:55
**Arabic**: وَذَكِّرْ فَإِنَّ ٱلذِّكْرَىٰ تَنفَعُ ٱلْمُؤْمِنِينَ

**English**: And remind, for the reminder benefits the believers.

### Verse 51:56
**Arabic**: وَمَا خَلَقْتُ ٱلْجِنَّ وَٱلْإِنسَ إِلَّا لِيَعْبُدُونِ

**English**: I did not create the jinn and the humans except to worship Me.

### Verse 51:57
**Arabic**: مَآ أُرِيدُ مِنْهُم مِّن رِّزْقٍۢ وَمَآ أُرِيدُ أَن يُطْعِمُونِ

**English**: I need no livelihood from them, nor do I need them to feed Me.

### Verse 51:58
**Arabic**: إِنَّ ٱللَّهَ هُوَ ٱلرَّزَّاقُ ذُو ٱلْقُوَّةِ ٱلْمَتِينُ

**English**: God is the Provider, the One with Power, the Strong.

### Verse 51:59
**Arabic**: فَإِنَّ لِلَّذِينَ ظَلَمُوا۟ ذَنُوبًۭا مِّثْلَ ذَنُوبِ أَصْحَٰبِهِمْ فَلَا يَسْتَعْجِلُونِ

**English**: Those who do wrong will have their turn, like the turn of their counterparts, so let them not rush Me.

### Verse 51:60
**Arabic**: فَوَيْلٌۭ لِّلَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا۟ مِن يَوْمِهِمُ ٱلَّذِى يُوعَدُونَ

**English**: So woe to those who disbelieve because of that Day of theirs which they are promised.
