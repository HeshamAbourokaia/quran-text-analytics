# Surah 110: An-Nasr (النصر)

- **Mushaf number**: 110
- **Place of revelation**: Medina (Madaniyah)
- **Verse count**: 3
- **Themes**: [Worship and Devotion](../themes/worship.md)
- **Named entities mentioned**: [Allah](../entities/divine_allah.md), [Ar-Rahim (the Most Merciful)](../entities/divine_ar-rahim.md), [Ar-Rahman (the Most Gracious)](../entities/divine_ar-rahman.md), [Rabb (the Lord)](../entities/divine_rabb.md)

- **Revelation order (chronological)**: #114 of 114
- **Revelation phase**: [Late Medinan](../timeline/phase_late-medina.md) (628-632 CE, Year 6-10 AH)

## Verses

### Verse 110:0
**Arabic**: بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

**English**: In the name of God, the Gracious, the Merciful.

### Verse 110:1
**Arabic**: إِذَا جَآءَ نَصْرُ ٱللَّهِ وَٱلْفَتْحُ

**English**: When there comes God's victory, and conquest.

### Verse 110:2
**Arabic**: وَرَأَيْتَ ٱلنَّاسَ يَدْخُلُونَ فِى دِينِ ٱللَّهِ أَفْوَاجًۭا

**English**: And you see the people entering God's religion in multitudes.

### Verse 110:3
**Arabic**: فَسَبِّحْ بِحَمْدِ رَبِّكَ وَٱسْتَغْفِرْهُ ۚ إِنَّهُۥ كَانَ تَوَّابًۢا

**English**: Then celebrate the praise of your Lord, and seek His forgiveness. He is the Accepter of Repentance.
