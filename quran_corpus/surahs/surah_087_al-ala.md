# Surah 87: Al-Ala (الأعلى)

- **Mushaf number**: 87
- **Place of revelation**: Mecca (Makkiyah)
- **Verse count**: 19
- **Themes**: [Faith and Monotheism](../themes/faith-monotheism.md)
- **Named entities mentioned**: [Allah](../entities/divine_allah.md), [Ar-Rahim (the Most Merciful)](../entities/divine_ar-rahim.md), [Ar-Rahman (the Most Gracious)](../entities/divine_ar-rahman.md), [Abraham (Ibrahim)](../entities/prophet_ibrahim.md), [Moses (Musa)](../entities/prophet_musa.md), [Rabb (the Lord)](../entities/divine_rabb.md), [John the Baptist (Yahya)](../entities/prophet_yahya.md)

## Verses

### Verse 87:0
**Arabic**: بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

**English**: In the name of God, the Gracious, the Merciful.

### Verse 87:1
**Arabic**: سَبِّحِ ٱسْمَ رَبِّكَ ٱلْأَعْلَى

**English**: Praise the Name of your Lord, the Most High.

### Verse 87:2
**Arabic**: ٱلَّذِى خَلَقَ فَسَوَّىٰ

**English**: He who creates and regulates.

### Verse 87:3
**Arabic**: وَٱلَّذِى قَدَّرَ فَهَدَىٰ

**English**: He who measures and guides.

### Verse 87:4
**Arabic**: وَٱلَّذِىٓ أَخْرَجَ ٱلْمَرْعَىٰ

**English**: He who produces the pasture.

### Verse 87:5
**Arabic**: فَجَعَلَهُۥ غُثَآءً أَحْوَىٰ

**English**: And then turns it into light debris.

### Verse 87:6
**Arabic**: سَنُقْرِئُكَ فَلَا تَنسَىٰٓ

**English**: We will make you read, so do not forget.

### Verse 87:7
**Arabic**: إِلَّا مَا شَآءَ ٱللَّهُ ۚ إِنَّهُۥ يَعْلَمُ ٱلْجَهْرَ وَمَا يَخْفَىٰ

**English**: Except what God wills. He knows what is declared, and what is hidden.

### Verse 87:8
**Arabic**: وَنُيَسِّرُكَ لِلْيُسْرَىٰ

**English**: We will ease you into the Easy Way.

### Verse 87:9
**Arabic**: فَذَكِّرْ إِن نَّفَعَتِ ٱلذِّكْرَىٰ

**English**: So remind, if reminding helps.

### Verse 87:10
**Arabic**: سَيَذَّكَّرُ مَن يَخْشَىٰ

**English**: The reverent will remember.

### Verse 87:11
**Arabic**: وَيَتَجَنَّبُهَا ٱلْأَشْقَى

**English**: But the wretched will avoid it.

### Verse 87:12
**Arabic**: ٱلَّذِى يَصْلَى ٱلنَّارَ ٱلْكُبْرَىٰ

**English**: He who will enter the Gigantic Fire.

### Verse 87:13
**Arabic**: ثُمَّ لَا يَمُوتُ فِيهَا وَلَا يَحْيَىٰ

**English**: Where he will neither die, nor live.

### Verse 87:14
**Arabic**: قَدْ أَفْلَحَ مَن تَزَكَّىٰ

**English**: Successful is he who purifies himself.

### Verse 87:15
**Arabic**: وَذَكَرَ ٱسْمَ رَبِّهِۦ فَصَلَّىٰ

**English**: And mentions the name of his Lord, and prays.

### Verse 87:16
**Arabic**: بَلْ تُؤْثِرُونَ ٱلْحَيَوٰةَ ٱلدُّنْيَا

**English**: But you prefer the present life.

### Verse 87:17
**Arabic**: وَٱلْءَاخِرَةُ خَيْرٌۭ وَأَبْقَىٰٓ

**English**: Though the Hereafter is better, and more lasting.

### Verse 87:18
**Arabic**: إِنَّ هَٰذَا لَفِى ٱلصُّحُفِ ٱلْأُولَىٰ

**English**: This is in the former scriptures.

### Verse 87:19
**Arabic**: صُحُفِ إِبْرَٰهِيمَ وَمُوسَىٰ

**English**: The Scriptures of Abraham and Moses.
