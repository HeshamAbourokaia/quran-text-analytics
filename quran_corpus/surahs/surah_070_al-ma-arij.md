# Surah 70: Al-Ma'arij (المعارج)

- **Mushaf number**: 70
- **Place of revelation**: Mecca (Makkiyah)
- **Verse count**: 44
- **Themes**: [Day of Judgment](../themes/day-of-judgment.md)
- **Named entities mentioned**: [Allah](../entities/divine_allah.md), [Ar-Rahim (the Most Merciful)](../entities/divine_ar-rahim.md), [Ar-Rahman (the Most Gracious)](../entities/divine_ar-rahman.md), [Rabb (the Lord)](../entities/divine_rabb.md)

## Verses

### Verse 70:0
**Arabic**: بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

**English**: In the name of God, the Gracious, the Merciful.

### Verse 70:1
**Arabic**: سَأَلَ سَآئِلٌۢ بِعَذَابٍۢ وَاقِعٍۢ

**English**: A questioner questioned the imminent torment.

### Verse 70:2
**Arabic**: لِّلْكَٰفِرِينَ لَيْسَ لَهُۥ دَافِعٌۭ

**English**: For the disbelievers; none can repel it.

### Verse 70:3
**Arabic**: مِّنَ ٱللَّهِ ذِى ٱلْمَعَارِجِ

**English**: From God, Lord of the Ways of Ascent.

### Verse 70:4
**Arabic**: تَعْرُجُ ٱلْمَلَٰٓئِكَةُ وَٱلرُّوحُ إِلَيْهِ فِى يَوْمٍۢ كَانَ مِقْدَارُهُۥ خَمْسِينَ أَلْفَ سَنَةٍۢ

**English**: Unto Him the angels and the Spirit ascend on a Day the duration of which is fifty thousand years.

### Verse 70:5
**Arabic**: فَٱصْبِرْ صَبْرًۭا جَمِيلًا

**English**: So be patient, with sweet patience.

### Verse 70:6
**Arabic**: إِنَّهُمْ يَرَوْنَهُۥ بَعِيدًۭا

**English**: They see it distant.

### Verse 70:7
**Arabic**: وَنَرَىٰهُ قَرِيبًۭا

**English**: But We see it near.

### Verse 70:8
**Arabic**: يَوْمَ تَكُونُ ٱلسَّمَآءُ كَٱلْمُهْلِ

**English**: On the Day when the sky will be like molten brass.

### Verse 70:9
**Arabic**: وَتَكُونُ ٱلْجِبَالُ كَٱلْعِهْنِ

**English**: And the mountains will be like tufted wool.

### Verse 70:10
**Arabic**: وَلَا يَسْـَٔلُ حَمِيمٌ حَمِيمًۭا

**English**: No friend will care about his friend.

### Verse 70:11
**Arabic**: يُبَصَّرُونَهُمْ ۚ يَوَدُّ ٱلْمُجْرِمُ لَوْ يَفْتَدِى مِنْ عَذَابِ يَوْمِئِذٍۭ بِبَنِيهِ

**English**: They will be shown each other. The criminal wishes he would be redeemed from the punishment of that Day by his children.

### Verse 70:12
**Arabic**: وَصَٰحِبَتِهِۦ وَأَخِيهِ

**English**: And his spouse, and his brother.

### Verse 70:13
**Arabic**: وَفَصِيلَتِهِ ٱلَّتِى تُـْٔوِيهِ

**English**: And his family that sheltered him.

### Verse 70:14
**Arabic**: وَمَن فِى ٱلْأَرْضِ جَمِيعًۭا ثُمَّ يُنجِيهِ

**English**: And everyone on earth, in order to save him.

### Verse 70:15
**Arabic**: كَلَّآ ۖ إِنَّهَا لَظَىٰ

**English**: By no means! It is a Raging Fire.

### Verse 70:16
**Arabic**: نَزَّاعَةًۭ لِّلشَّوَىٰ

**English**: It strips away the scalps.

### Verse 70:17
**Arabic**: تَدْعُوا۟ مَنْ أَدْبَرَ وَتَوَلَّىٰ

**English**: It invites him who once turned his back and fled.

### Verse 70:18
**Arabic**: وَجَمَعَ فَأَوْعَىٰٓ

**English**: And accumulated and hoarded.

### Verse 70:19
**Arabic**: ۞ إِنَّ ٱلْإِنسَٰنَ خُلِقَ هَلُوعًا

**English**: Man was created restless.

### Verse 70:20
**Arabic**: إِذَا مَسَّهُ ٱلشَّرُّ جَزُوعًۭا

**English**: Touched by adversity, he is fretful.

### Verse 70:21
**Arabic**: وَإِذَا مَسَّهُ ٱلْخَيْرُ مَنُوعًا

**English**: Touched by good, he is ungenerous.

### Verse 70:22
**Arabic**: إِلَّا ٱلْمُصَلِّينَ

**English**: Except the prayerful.

### Verse 70:23
**Arabic**: ٱلَّذِينَ هُمْ عَلَىٰ صَلَاتِهِمْ دَآئِمُونَ

**English**: Those who are constant at their prayers.

### Verse 70:24
**Arabic**: وَٱلَّذِينَ فِىٓ أَمْوَٰلِهِمْ حَقٌّۭ مَّعْلُومٌۭ

**English**: And those in whose wealth is a rightful share.

### Verse 70:25
**Arabic**: لِّلسَّآئِلِ وَٱلْمَحْرُومِ

**English**: For the beggar and the deprived.

### Verse 70:26
**Arabic**: وَٱلَّذِينَ يُصَدِّقُونَ بِيَوْمِ ٱلدِّينِ

**English**: And those who affirm the Day of Judgment.

### Verse 70:27
**Arabic**: وَٱلَّذِينَ هُم مِّنْ عَذَابِ رَبِّهِم مُّشْفِقُونَ

**English**: And those who fear the punishment of their Lord.

### Verse 70:28
**Arabic**: إِنَّ عَذَابَ رَبِّهِمْ غَيْرُ مَأْمُونٍۢ

**English**: Their Lord's punishment is not to be taken for granted.

### Verse 70:29
**Arabic**: وَٱلَّذِينَ هُمْ لِفُرُوجِهِمْ حَٰفِظُونَ

**English**: And those who guard their chastity.

### Verse 70:30
**Arabic**: إِلَّا عَلَىٰٓ أَزْوَٰجِهِمْ أَوْ مَا مَلَكَتْ أَيْمَٰنُهُمْ فَإِنَّهُمْ غَيْرُ مَلُومِينَ

**English**: Except from their spouses or those living under their control, for then they are free of blame.

### Verse 70:31
**Arabic**: فَمَنِ ٱبْتَغَىٰ وَرَآءَ ذَٰلِكَ فَأُو۟لَٰٓئِكَ هُمُ ٱلْعَادُونَ

**English**: But whoever seeks to go beyond that-these are the transgressors.

### Verse 70:32
**Arabic**: وَٱلَّذِينَ هُمْ لِأَمَٰنَٰتِهِمْ وَعَهْدِهِمْ رَٰعُونَ

**English**: And those who honor their trusts and their pledges.

### Verse 70:33
**Arabic**: وَٱلَّذِينَ هُم بِشَهَٰدَٰتِهِمْ قَآئِمُونَ

**English**: And those who stand by their testimonies.

### Verse 70:34
**Arabic**: وَٱلَّذِينَ هُمْ عَلَىٰ صَلَاتِهِمْ يُحَافِظُونَ

**English**: And those who are dedicated to their prayers.

### Verse 70:35
**Arabic**: أُو۟لَٰٓئِكَ فِى جَنَّٰتٍۢ مُّكْرَمُونَ

**English**: These will be honored in Gardens.

### Verse 70:36
**Arabic**: فَمَالِ ٱلَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا۟ قِبَلَكَ مُهْطِعِينَ

**English**: What is with those who disbelieve, stretching their necks towards you.

### Verse 70:37
**Arabic**: عَنِ ٱلْيَمِينِ وَعَنِ ٱلشِّمَالِ عِزِينَ

**English**: From the right, and from the left, banding together?

### Verse 70:38
**Arabic**: أَيَطْمَعُ كُلُّ ٱمْرِئٍۢ مِّنْهُمْ أَن يُدْخَلَ جَنَّةَ نَعِيمٍۢ

**English**: Is every one of them aspiring to be admitted into a Garden of Bliss?

### Verse 70:39
**Arabic**: كَلَّآ ۖ إِنَّا خَلَقْنَٰهُم مِّمَّا يَعْلَمُونَ

**English**: No indeed! We created them from what they know.

### Verse 70:40
**Arabic**: فَلَآ أُقْسِمُ بِرَبِّ ٱلْمَشَٰرِقِ وَٱلْمَغَٰرِبِ إِنَّا لَقَٰدِرُونَ

**English**: I swear by the Lord of the Easts and the Wests, that We are Able.

### Verse 70:41
**Arabic**: عَلَىٰٓ أَن نُّبَدِّلَ خَيْرًۭا مِّنْهُمْ وَمَا نَحْنُ بِمَسْبُوقِينَ

**English**: To replace them with better than they, and We are not to be outdone.

### Verse 70:42
**Arabic**: فَذَرْهُمْ يَخُوضُوا۟ وَيَلْعَبُوا۟ حَتَّىٰ يُلَٰقُوا۟ يَوْمَهُمُ ٱلَّذِى يُوعَدُونَ

**English**: So leave them to blunder and play, until they meet their Day which they are promised.

### Verse 70:43
**Arabic**: يَوْمَ يَخْرُجُونَ مِنَ ٱلْأَجْدَاثِ سِرَاعًۭا كَأَنَّهُمْ إِلَىٰ نُصُبٍۢ يُوفِضُونَ

**English**: The Day when they will emerge from the tombs in a rush, as though they were hurrying towards a target.

### Verse 70:44
**Arabic**: خَٰشِعَةً أَبْصَٰرُهُمْ تَرْهَقُهُمْ ذِلَّةٌۭ ۚ ذَٰلِكَ ٱلْيَوْمُ ٱلَّذِى كَانُوا۟ يُوعَدُونَ

**English**: Their eyes cast down; overwhelmed by humiliation. This is the Day which they were promised.
