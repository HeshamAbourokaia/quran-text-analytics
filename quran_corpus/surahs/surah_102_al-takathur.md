# Surah 102: Al-Takathur (التكاثر)

- **Mushaf number**: 102
- **Place of revelation**: Mecca (Makkiyah)
- **Verse count**: 8
- **Named entities mentioned**: [Allah](../entities/divine_allah.md), [Ar-Rahim (the Most Merciful)](../entities/divine_ar-rahim.md), [Ar-Rahman (the Most Gracious)](../entities/divine_ar-rahman.md)

## Verses

### Verse 102:0
**Arabic**: بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

**English**: In the name of God, the Gracious, the Merciful.

### Verse 102:1
**Arabic**: أَلْهَىٰكُمُ ٱلتَّكَاثُرُ

**English**: Abundance distracts you.

### Verse 102:2
**Arabic**: حَتَّىٰ زُرْتُمُ ٱلْمَقَابِرَ

**English**: Until you visit the graveyards.

### Verse 102:3
**Arabic**: كَلَّا سَوْفَ تَعْلَمُونَ

**English**: Indeed, you will know.

### Verse 102:4
**Arabic**: ثُمَّ كَلَّا سَوْفَ تَعْلَمُونَ

**English**: Certainly, you will know.

### Verse 102:5
**Arabic**: كَلَّا لَوْ تَعْلَمُونَ عِلْمَ ٱلْيَقِينِ

**English**: If you knew with knowledge of certainty.

### Verse 102:6
**Arabic**: لَتَرَوُنَّ ٱلْجَحِيمَ

**English**: You would see the Inferno.

### Verse 102:7
**Arabic**: ثُمَّ لَتَرَوُنَّهَا عَيْنَ ٱلْيَقِينِ

**English**: Then you will see it with the eye of certainty.

### Verse 102:8
**Arabic**: ثُمَّ لَتُسْـَٔلُنَّ يَوْمَئِذٍ عَنِ ٱلنَّعِيمِ

**English**: Then, on that Day, you will be questioned about the Bliss.
