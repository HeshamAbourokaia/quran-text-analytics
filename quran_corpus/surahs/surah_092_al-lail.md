# Surah 92: Al-Lail (الليل)

- **Mushaf number**: 92
- **Place of revelation**: Mecca (Makkiyah)
- **Verse count**: 21
- **Themes**: [Warning](../themes/warning.md)
- **Named entities mentioned**: [Allah](../entities/divine_allah.md), [Ar-Rahim (the Most Merciful)](../entities/divine_ar-rahim.md), [Ar-Rahman (the Most Gracious)](../entities/divine_ar-rahman.md), [Rabb (the Lord)](../entities/divine_rabb.md)

- **Revelation order (chronological)**: #9 of 114
- **Revelation phase**: [Early Meccan](../timeline/phase_early-mecca.md) (610-615 CE, Year 1-5 of Prophethood)

## Verses

### Verse 92:0
**Arabic**: بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

**English**: In the name of God, the Gracious, the Merciful.

### Verse 92:1
**Arabic**: وَٱلَّيْلِ إِذَا يَغْشَىٰ

**English**: By the night as it covers.

### Verse 92:2
**Arabic**: وَٱلنَّهَارِ إِذَا تَجَلَّىٰ

**English**: And the day as it reveals.

### Verse 92:3
**Arabic**: وَمَا خَلَقَ ٱلذَّكَرَ وَٱلْأُنثَىٰٓ

**English**: And He who created the male and the female.

### Verse 92:4
**Arabic**: إِنَّ سَعْيَكُمْ لَشَتَّىٰ

**English**: Your endeavors are indeed diverse.

### Verse 92:5
**Arabic**: فَأَمَّا مَنْ أَعْطَىٰ وَٱتَّقَىٰ

**English**: As for him who gives and is righteous.

### Verse 92:6
**Arabic**: وَصَدَّقَ بِٱلْحُسْنَىٰ

**English**: And confirms goodness.

### Verse 92:7
**Arabic**: فَسَنُيَسِّرُهُۥ لِلْيُسْرَىٰ

**English**: We will ease his way towards ease.

### Verse 92:8
**Arabic**: وَأَمَّا مَنۢ بَخِلَ وَٱسْتَغْنَىٰ

**English**: But as for him who is stingy and complacent.

### Verse 92:9
**Arabic**: وَكَذَّبَ بِٱلْحُسْنَىٰ

**English**: And denies goodness.

### Verse 92:10
**Arabic**: فَسَنُيَسِّرُهُۥ لِلْعُسْرَىٰ

**English**: We will ease his way towards difficulty.

### Verse 92:11
**Arabic**: وَمَا يُغْنِى عَنْهُ مَالُهُۥٓ إِذَا تَرَدَّىٰٓ

**English**: And his money will not avail him when he plummets.

### Verse 92:12
**Arabic**: إِنَّ عَلَيْنَا لَلْهُدَىٰ

**English**: It is upon Us to guide.

### Verse 92:13
**Arabic**: وَإِنَّ لَنَا لَلْءَاخِرَةَ وَٱلْأُولَىٰ

**English**: And to Us belong the Last and the First.

### Verse 92:14
**Arabic**: فَأَنذَرْتُكُمْ نَارًۭا تَلَظَّىٰ

**English**: I have warned you of a Fierce Blaze.

### Verse 92:15
**Arabic**: لَا يَصْلَىٰهَآ إِلَّا ٱلْأَشْقَى

**English**: None will burn in it except the very wicked.

### Verse 92:16
**Arabic**: ٱلَّذِى كَذَّبَ وَتَوَلَّىٰ

**English**: He who denies and turns away.

### Verse 92:17
**Arabic**: وَسَيُجَنَّبُهَا ٱلْأَتْقَى

**English**: But the devout will avoid it.

### Verse 92:18
**Arabic**: ٱلَّذِى يُؤْتِى مَالَهُۥ يَتَزَكَّىٰ

**English**: He who gives his money to become pure.

### Verse 92:19
**Arabic**: وَمَا لِأَحَدٍ عِندَهُۥ مِن نِّعْمَةٍۢ تُجْزَىٰٓ

**English**: Seeking no favor in return.

### Verse 92:20
**Arabic**: إِلَّا ٱبْتِغَآءَ وَجْهِ رَبِّهِ ٱلْأَعْلَىٰ

**English**: Only seeking the acceptance of his Lord, the Most High.

### Verse 92:21
**Arabic**: وَلَسَوْفَ يَرْضَىٰ

**English**: And he will be satisfied.
