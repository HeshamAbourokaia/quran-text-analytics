# Surah 80: Abasa (عبس)

- **Mushaf number**: 80
- **Place of revelation**: Mecca (Makkiyah)
- **Verse count**: 42
- **Themes**: [Nature and Signs](../themes/nature.md)
- **Named entities mentioned**: [Allah](../entities/divine_allah.md), [Ar-Rahim (the Most Merciful)](../entities/divine_ar-rahim.md), [Ar-Rahman (the Most Gracious)](../entities/divine_ar-rahman.md)

## Verses

### Verse 80:0
**Arabic**: بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

**English**: In the name of God, the Gracious, the Merciful.

### Verse 80:1
**Arabic**: عَبَسَ وَتَوَلَّىٰٓ

**English**: He frowned and turned away.

### Verse 80:2
**Arabic**: أَن جَآءَهُ ٱلْأَعْمَىٰ

**English**: When the blind man approached him.

### Verse 80:3
**Arabic**: وَمَا يُدْرِيكَ لَعَلَّهُۥ يَزَّكَّىٰٓ

**English**: But how do you know? Perhaps he was seeking to purify himself.

### Verse 80:4
**Arabic**: أَوْ يَذَّكَّرُ فَتَنفَعَهُ ٱلذِّكْرَىٰٓ

**English**: Or be reminded, and the message would benefit him.

### Verse 80:5
**Arabic**: أَمَّا مَنِ ٱسْتَغْنَىٰ

**English**: But as for him who was indifferent.

### Verse 80:6
**Arabic**: فَأَنتَ لَهُۥ تَصَدَّىٰ

**English**: You gave him your attention.

### Verse 80:7
**Arabic**: وَمَا عَلَيْكَ أَلَّا يَزَّكَّىٰ

**English**: Though you are not liable if he does not purify himself.

### Verse 80:8
**Arabic**: وَأَمَّا مَن جَآءَكَ يَسْعَىٰ

**English**: But as for him who came to you seeking.

### Verse 80:9
**Arabic**: وَهُوَ يَخْشَىٰ

**English**: In awe.

### Verse 80:10
**Arabic**: فَأَنتَ عَنْهُ تَلَهَّىٰ

**English**: To him you were inattentive.

### Verse 80:11
**Arabic**: كَلَّآ إِنَّهَا تَذْكِرَةٌۭ

**English**: Do not. This is a Lesson.

### Verse 80:12
**Arabic**: فَمَن شَآءَ ذَكَرَهُۥ

**English**: Whoever wills, shall remember it.

### Verse 80:13
**Arabic**: فِى صُحُفٍۢ مُّكَرَّمَةٍۢ

**English**: On honorable pages.

### Verse 80:14
**Arabic**: مَّرْفُوعَةٍۢ مُّطَهَّرَةٍۭ

**English**: Exalted and purified.

### Verse 80:15
**Arabic**: بِأَيْدِى سَفَرَةٍۢ

**English**: By the hands of scribes.

### Verse 80:16
**Arabic**: كِرَامٍۭ بَرَرَةٍۢ

**English**: Noble and devoted.

### Verse 80:17
**Arabic**: قُتِلَ ٱلْإِنسَٰنُ مَآ أَكْفَرَهُۥ

**English**: Perish man! How thankless he is!

### Verse 80:18
**Arabic**: مِنْ أَىِّ شَىْءٍ خَلَقَهُۥ

**English**: From what did He create him?

### Verse 80:19
**Arabic**: مِن نُّطْفَةٍ خَلَقَهُۥ فَقَدَّرَهُۥ

**English**: From a sperm drop He created him, and enabled him.

### Verse 80:20
**Arabic**: ثُمَّ ٱلسَّبِيلَ يَسَّرَهُۥ

**English**: Then He eased the way for him.

### Verse 80:21
**Arabic**: ثُمَّ أَمَاتَهُۥ فَأَقْبَرَهُۥ

**English**: Then He puts him to death, and buries him.

### Verse 80:22
**Arabic**: ثُمَّ إِذَا شَآءَ أَنشَرَهُۥ

**English**: Then, when He wills, He will resurrect him.

### Verse 80:23
**Arabic**: كَلَّا لَمَّا يَقْضِ مَآ أَمَرَهُۥ

**English**: But no, he did not fulfill what He has commanded him.

### Verse 80:24
**Arabic**: فَلْيَنظُرِ ٱلْإِنسَٰنُ إِلَىٰ طَعَامِهِۦٓ

**English**: Let man consider his food.

### Verse 80:25
**Arabic**: أَنَّا صَبَبْنَا ٱلْمَآءَ صَبًّۭا

**English**: We pour down water in abundance.

### Verse 80:26
**Arabic**: ثُمَّ شَقَقْنَا ٱلْأَرْضَ شَقًّۭا

**English**: Then crack the soil open.

### Verse 80:27
**Arabic**: فَأَنۢبَتْنَا فِيهَا حَبًّۭا

**English**: And grow in it grains.

### Verse 80:28
**Arabic**: وَعِنَبًۭا وَقَضْبًۭا

**English**: And grapes and herbs.

### Verse 80:29
**Arabic**: وَزَيْتُونًۭا وَنَخْلًۭا

**English**: And olives and dates.

### Verse 80:30
**Arabic**: وَحَدَآئِقَ غُلْبًۭا

**English**: And luscious gardens.

### Verse 80:31
**Arabic**: وَفَٰكِهَةًۭ وَأَبًّۭا

**English**: And fruits and vegetables.

### Verse 80:32
**Arabic**: مَّتَٰعًۭا لَّكُمْ وَلِأَنْعَٰمِكُمْ

**English**: Enjoyment for you, and for your livestock.

### Verse 80:33
**Arabic**: فَإِذَا جَآءَتِ ٱلصَّآخَّةُ

**English**: But when the Deafening Noise comes to pass.

### Verse 80:34
**Arabic**: يَوْمَ يَفِرُّ ٱلْمَرْءُ مِنْ أَخِيهِ

**English**: The Day when a person will flee from his brother.

### Verse 80:35
**Arabic**: وَأُمِّهِۦ وَأَبِيهِ

**English**: And his mother and his father.

### Verse 80:36
**Arabic**: وَصَٰحِبَتِهِۦ وَبَنِيهِ

**English**: And his consort and his children.

### Verse 80:37
**Arabic**: لِكُلِّ ٱمْرِئٍۢ مِّنْهُمْ يَوْمَئِذٍۢ شَأْنٌۭ يُغْنِيهِ

**English**: Every one of them, on that Day, will have enough to preoccupy him.

### Verse 80:38
**Arabic**: وُجُوهٌۭ يَوْمَئِذٍۢ مُّسْفِرَةٌۭ

**English**: Faces on that Day will be radiant.

### Verse 80:39
**Arabic**: ضَاحِكَةٌۭ مُّسْتَبْشِرَةٌۭ

**English**: Laughing and rejoicing.

### Verse 80:40
**Arabic**: وَوُجُوهٌۭ يَوْمَئِذٍ عَلَيْهَا غَبَرَةٌۭ

**English**: And Faces on that Day will be covered with misery.

### Verse 80:41
**Arabic**: تَرْهَقُهَا قَتَرَةٌ

**English**: Overwhelmed by remorse.

### Verse 80:42
**Arabic**: أُو۟لَٰٓئِكَ هُمُ ٱلْكَفَرَةُ ٱلْفَجَرَةُ

**English**: These are the faithless, the vicious.
