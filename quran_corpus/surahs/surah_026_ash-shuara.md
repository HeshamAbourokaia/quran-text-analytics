- **Mushaf number**: 26
- **Place of revelation**: Mecca (Makkiyah)
- **Verse count**: 227
- **Themes**: [Stories of the Prophets](../themes/prophets-stories.md)
- **Named entities mentioned**: [Allah](../entities/divine_allah.md), [Ar-Rahim (the Most Merciful)](../entities/divine_ar-rahim.md), [Ar-Rahman (the Most Gracious)](../entities/divine_ar-rahman.md), [Pharaoh (Firaun)](../entities/figure_firaun.md), [Hud](../entities/prophet_hud.md), [Iblis (Satan)](../entities/figure_iblis.md), [Abraham (Ibrahim)](../entities/prophet_ibrahim.md), [Lot (Lut)](../entities/prophet_lut.md), [Moses (Musa)](../entities/prophet_musa.md), [Noah (Nuh)](../entities/prophet_nuh.md), [Rabb (the Lord)](../entities/divine_rabb.md), [Shuayb](../entities/prophet_shuayb.md), [John the Baptist (Yahya)](../entities/prophet_yahya.md)

## Verses

### Verse 26:0
**Arabic**: بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

**English**: In the name of God, the Gracious, the Merciful.

### Verse 26:1
**Arabic**: طسٓمٓ

**English**: Ta, Seen, Meem.

### Verse 26:2
**Arabic**: تِلْكَ ءَايَٰتُ ٱلْكِتَٰبِ ٱلْمُبِينِ

**English**: These are the Verses of the Clarifying Book.

### Verse 26:3
**Arabic**: لَعَلَّكَ بَٰخِعٌۭ نَّفْسَكَ أَلَّا يَكُونُوا۟ مُؤْمِنِينَ

**English**: Perhaps you will destroy yourself with grief, because they do not become believers.

### Verse 26:4
**Arabic**: إِن نَّشَأْ نُنَزِّلْ عَلَيْهِم مِّنَ ٱلسَّمَآءِ ءَايَةًۭ فَظَلَّتْ أَعْنَٰقُهُمْ لَهَا خَٰضِعِينَ

**English**: If We will, We can send down upon them a sign from heaven, at which their necks will stay bent in humility.

### Verse 26:5
**Arabic**: وَمَا يَأْتِيهِم مِّن ذِكْرٍۢ مِّنَ ٱلرَّحْمَٰنِ مُحْدَثٍ إِلَّا كَانُوا۟ عَنْهُ مُعْرِضِينَ

**English**: No fresh reminder comes to them from the Most Merciful, but they turn their backs at it.

### Verse 26:6
**Arabic**: فَقَدْ كَذَّبُوا۟ فَسَيَأْتِيهِمْ أَنۢبَٰٓؤُا۟ مَا كَانُوا۟ بِهِۦ يَسْتَهْزِءُونَ

**English**: They have denied the truth, but soon will come to them the news of what they ridiculed.

### Verse 26:7
**Arabic**: أَوَلَمْ يَرَوْا۟ إِلَى ٱلْأَرْضِ كَمْ أَنۢبَتْنَا فِيهَا مِن كُلِّ زَوْجٍۢ كَرِيمٍ

**English**: Have they not seen the earth, and how many beautiful pairs We produced therein?

### Verse 26:8
**Arabic**: إِنَّ فِى ذَٰلِكَ لَءَايَةًۭ ۖ وَمَا كَانَ أَكْثَرُهُم مُّؤْمِنِينَ

**English**: Surely in this is a sign, but most of them are not believers.

### Verse 26:9
**Arabic**: وَإِنَّ رَبَّكَ لَهُوَ ٱلْعَزِيزُ ٱلرَّحِيمُ

**English**: Most surely, your Lord is the Almighty, the Merciful.

### Verse 26:10
**Arabic**: وَإِذْ نَادَىٰ رَبُّكَ مُوسَىٰٓ أَنِ ٱئْتِ ٱلْقَوْمَ ٱلظَّٰلِمِينَ

**English**: Your Lord called to Moses, "Go to the tyrannical people.

### Verse 26:11
**Arabic**: قَوْمَ فِرْعَوْنَ ۚ أَلَا يَتَّقُونَ

**English**: The people of Pharaoh. Will they not fear?"

**Arabic**: قَالَ رَبِّ إِنِّىٓ أَخَافُ أَن يُكَذِّبُونِ

**English**: He said, "My Lord, I fear they will reject me.

**English**: And I become stressed, and my tongue is not fluent, so send Aaron too.

### Verse 26:14
