# Surah 54: Al-Qamar (القمر)

- **Mushaf number**: 54
- **Place of revelation**: Mecca (Makkiyah)
- **Verse count**: 55
- **Themes**: [Warning](../themes/warning.md)
- **Named entities mentioned**: [Allah](../entities/divine_allah.md), [Ar-Rahim (the Most Merciful)](../entities/divine_ar-rahim.md), [Ar-Rahman (the Most Gracious)](../entities/divine_ar-rahman.md), [Pharaoh (Firaun)](../entities/figure_firaun.md), [Lot (Lut)](../entities/prophet_lut.md), [Noah (Nuh)](../entities/prophet_nuh.md), [Rabb (the Lord)](../entities/divine_rabb.md)

## Verses

### Verse 54:0
**Arabic**: بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

**English**: In the name of God, the Gracious, the Merciful.

### Verse 54:1
**Arabic**: ٱقْتَرَبَتِ ٱلسَّاعَةُ وَٱنشَقَّ ٱلْقَمَرُ

**English**: The Hour has drawn near, and the moon has split.

### Verse 54:2
**Arabic**: وَإِن يَرَوْا۟ ءَايَةًۭ يُعْرِضُوا۟ وَيَقُولُوا۟ سِحْرٌۭ مُّسْتَمِرٌّۭ

**English**: Yet whenever they see a miracle, they turn away, and say, "Continuous magic."

### Verse 54:3
**Arabic**: وَكَذَّبُوا۟ وَٱتَّبَعُوٓا۟ أَهْوَآءَهُمْ ۚ وَكُلُّ أَمْرٍۢ مُّسْتَقِرٌّۭ

**English**: They lied, and followed their opinions, but everything has its time.

### Verse 54:4
**Arabic**: وَلَقَدْ جَآءَهُم مِّنَ ٱلْأَنۢبَآءِ مَا فِيهِ مُزْدَجَرٌ

**English**: And there came to them news containing a deterrent.

### Verse 54:5
**Arabic**: حِكْمَةٌۢ بَٰلِغَةٌۭ ۖ فَمَا تُغْنِ ٱلنُّذُرُ

**English**: Profound wisdom-but warnings are of no avail.

### Verse 54:6
**Arabic**: فَتَوَلَّ عَنْهُمْ ۘ يَوْمَ يَدْعُ ٱلدَّاعِ إِلَىٰ شَىْءٍۢ نُّكُرٍ

**English**: So turn away from them. On the Day when the Caller calls to something terrible.

### Verse 54:7
**Arabic**: خُشَّعًا أَبْصَٰرُهُمْ يَخْرُجُونَ مِنَ ٱلْأَجْدَاثِ كَأَنَّهُمْ جَرَادٌۭ مُّنتَشِرٌۭ

**English**: Their eyes humiliated, they will emerge from the graves, as if they were swarming locusts.

### Verse 54:8
**Arabic**: مُّهْطِعِينَ إِلَى ٱلدَّاعِ ۖ يَقُولُ ٱلْكَٰفِرُونَ هَٰذَا يَوْمٌ عَسِرٌۭ

**English**: Scrambling towards the Caller, the disbelievers will say, "This is a difficult Day."

### Verse 54:9
**Arabic**: ۞ كَذَّبَتْ قَبْلَهُمْ قَوْمُ نُوحٍۢ فَكَذَّبُوا۟ عَبْدَنَا وَقَالُوا۟ مَجْنُونٌۭ وَٱزْدُجِرَ

**English**: Before them the people of Noah disbelieved. They rejected Our servant, and said, "Crazy," and he was rebuked.

### Verse 54:10
**Arabic**: فَدَعَا رَبَّهُۥٓ أَنِّى مَغْلُوبٌۭ فَٱنتَصِرْ

**English**: So he appealed to his Lord, "I am overwhelmed, so help me."

### Verse 54:11
**Arabic**: فَفَتَحْنَآ أَبْوَٰبَ ٱلسَّمَآءِ بِمَآءٍۢ مُّنْهَمِرٍۢ

**English**: So We opened the floodgates of heaven with water pouring down.

### Verse 54:12
**Arabic**: وَفَجَّرْنَا ٱلْأَرْضَ عُيُونًۭا فَٱلْتَقَى ٱلْمَآءُ عَلَىٰٓ أَمْرٍۢ قَدْ قُدِرَ

**English**: And We made the earth burst with springs, and the waters met for a purpose already destined.

### Verse 54:13
**Arabic**: وَحَمَلْنَٰهُ عَلَىٰ ذَاتِ أَلْوَٰحٍۢ وَدُسُرٍۢ

**English**: And We carried him on a craft of planks and nails.

### Verse 54:14
**Arabic**: تَجْرِى بِأَعْيُنِنَا جَزَآءًۭ لِّمَن كَانَ كُفِرَ

**English**: Sailing before Our eyes; a reward for him who was rejected.

### Verse 54:15
**Arabic**: وَلَقَد تَّرَكْنَٰهَآ ءَايَةًۭ فَهَلْ مِن مُّدَّكِرٍۢ

**English**: And We left it as a sign. Is there anyone who would take heed?

### Verse 54:16
**Arabic**: فَكَيْفَ كَانَ عَذَابِى وَنُذُرِ

**English**: So how were My punishment and My warnings?

### Verse 54:17
**Arabic**: وَلَقَدْ يَسَّرْنَا ٱلْقُرْءَانَ لِلذِّكْرِ فَهَلْ مِن مُّدَّكِرٍۢ

**English**: We made the Quran easy to learn. Is there anyone who would learn?

### Verse 54:18
**Arabic**: كَذَّبَتْ عَادٌۭ فَكَيْفَ كَانَ عَذَابِى وَنُذُرِ

**English**: Aad denied the truth. So how were My punishment and My warnings?

### Verse 54:19
**Arabic**: إِنَّآ أَرْسَلْنَا عَلَيْهِمْ رِيحًۭا صَرْصَرًۭا فِى يَوْمِ نَحْسٍۢ مُّسْتَمِرٍّۢ

**English**: We unleashed upon them a screaming wind, on a day of unrelenting misery.

### Verse 54:20
**Arabic**: تَنزِعُ ٱلنَّاسَ كَأَنَّهُمْ أَعْجَازُ نَخْلٍۢ مُّنقَعِرٍۢ

**English**: Plucking the people away, as though they were trunks of uprooted palm-trees.

### Verse 54:21
**Arabic**: فَكَيْفَ كَانَ عَذَابِى وَنُذُرِ

**English**: So how were My punishment and My warnings?

### Verse 54:22
**Arabic**: وَلَقَدْ يَسَّرْنَا ٱلْقُرْءَانَ لِلذِّكْرِ فَهَلْ مِن مُّدَّكِرٍۢ

**English**: We made the Quran easy to remember. Is there anyone who would remember?

### Verse 54:23
**Arabic**: كَذَّبَتْ ثَمُودُ بِٱلنُّذُرِ

**English**: Thamood rejected the warnings.

### Verse 54:24
**Arabic**: فَقَالُوٓا۟ أَبَشَرًۭا مِّنَّا وَٰحِدًۭا نَّتَّبِعُهُۥٓ إِنَّآ إِذًۭا لَّفِى ضَلَٰلٍۢ وَسُعُرٍ

**English**: They said, "Are we to follow one of us, a human being? We would then go astray, and end up in Hell.

### Verse 54:25
**Arabic**: أَءُلْقِىَ ٱلذِّكْرُ عَلَيْهِ مِنۢ بَيْنِنَا بَلْ هُوَ كَذَّابٌ أَشِرٌۭ

**English**: Was the message given to him, out of all of us? In fact, he is a wicked liar."

### Verse 54:26
**Arabic**: سَيَعْلَمُونَ غَدًۭا مَّنِ ٱلْكَذَّابُ ٱلْأَشِرُ

**English**: They will know tomorrow who the wicked liar is.

### Verse 54:27
**Arabic**: إِنَّا مُرْسِلُوا۟ ٱلنَّاقَةِ فِتْنَةًۭ لَّهُمْ فَٱرْتَقِبْهُمْ وَٱصْطَبِرْ

**English**: We are sending the she-camel as a test for them; so watch them and be patient.

### Verse 54:28
**Arabic**: وَنَبِّئْهُمْ أَنَّ ٱلْمَآءَ قِسْمَةٌۢ بَيْنَهُمْ ۖ كُلُّ شِرْبٍۢ مُّحْتَضَرٌۭ

**English**: And inform them that the water is to be shared between them; each share of drink made available.

### Verse 54:29
**Arabic**: فَنَادَوْا۟ صَاحِبَهُمْ فَتَعَاطَىٰ فَعَقَرَ

**English**: But they called their friend, and he dared, and he slaughtered.

### Verse 54:30
**Arabic**: فَكَيْفَ كَانَ عَذَابِى وَنُذُرِ

**English**: So how were My punishment and My warnings?

### Verse 54:31
**Arabic**: إِنَّآ أَرْسَلْنَا عَلَيْهِمْ صَيْحَةًۭ وَٰحِدَةًۭ فَكَانُوا۟ كَهَشِيمِ ٱلْمُحْتَظِرِ

**English**: We sent against them a single Scream, and they became like crushed hay.

### Verse 54:32
**Arabic**: وَلَقَدْ يَسَّرْنَا ٱلْقُرْءَانَ لِلذِّكْرِ فَهَلْ مِن مُّدَّكِرٍۢ

**English**: We made the Quran easy to understand. Is there anyone who would understand?

### Verse 54:33
**Arabic**: كَذَّبَتْ قَوْمُ لُوطٍۭ بِٱلنُّذُرِ

**English**: The people of Lot rejected the warnings.

### Verse 54:34
**Arabic**: إِنَّآ أَرْسَلْنَا عَلَيْهِمْ حَاصِبًا إِلَّآ ءَالَ لُوطٍۢ ۖ نَّجَّيْنَٰهُم بِسَحَرٍۢ

**English**: We unleashed upon them a shower of stones, except for the family of Lot; We rescued them at dawn.

### Verse 54:35
**Arabic**: نِّعْمَةًۭ مِّنْ عِندِنَا ۚ كَذَٰلِكَ نَجْزِى مَن شَكَرَ

**English**: A blessing from Us. Thus We reward the thankful.

### Verse 54:36
**Arabic**: وَلَقَدْ أَنذَرَهُم بَطْشَتَنَا فَتَمَارَوْا۟ بِٱلنُّذُرِ

**English**: He had warned them of Our onslaught, but they dismissed the warnings.

### Verse 54:37
**Arabic**: وَلَقَدْ رَٰوَدُوهُ عَن ضَيْفِهِۦ فَطَمَسْنَآ أَعْيُنَهُمْ فَذُوقُوا۟ عَذَابِى وَنُذُرِ

**English**: They even lusted for his guest, so We obliterated their eyes. "So taste My punishment and My warnings."

### Verse 54:38
**Arabic**: وَلَقَدْ صَبَّحَهُم بُكْرَةً عَذَابٌۭ مُّسْتَقِرٌّۭ

**English**: Early morning brought upon them enduring punishment.

### Verse 54:39
**Arabic**: فَذُوقُوا۟ عَذَابِى وَنُذُرِ

**English**: So taste My punishment and My warnings.

### Verse 54:40
**Arabic**: وَلَقَدْ يَسَّرْنَا ٱلْقُرْءَانَ لِلذِّكْرِ فَهَلْ مِن مُّدَّكِرٍۢ

**English**: We made the Quran easy to memorize. Is there anyone who would memorize?

### Verse 54:41
**Arabic**: وَلَقَدْ جَآءَ ءَالَ فِرْعَوْنَ ٱلنُّذُرُ

**English**: The warnings also came to the people of Pharaoh.

### Verse 54:42
**Arabic**: كَذَّبُوا۟ بِـَٔايَٰتِنَا كُلِّهَا فَأَخَذْنَٰهُمْ أَخْذَ عَزِيزٍۢ مُّقْتَدِرٍ

**English**: They rejected Our signs, all of them, so We seized them-the seizure of an Almighty Omnipotent.

### Verse 54:43
**Arabic**: أَكُفَّارُكُمْ خَيْرٌۭ مِّنْ أُو۟لَٰٓئِكُمْ أَمْ لَكُم بَرَآءَةٌۭ فِى ٱلزُّبُرِ

**English**: Are your unbelievers better than all those? Or do you have immunity in the scriptures?

### Verse 54:44
**Arabic**: أَمْ يَقُولُونَ نَحْنُ جَمِيعٌۭ مُّنتَصِرٌۭ

**English**: Or do they say, "We are united, and we will be victorious"?

### Verse 54:45
**Arabic**: سَيُهْزَمُ ٱلْجَمْعُ وَيُوَلُّونَ ٱلدُّبُرَ

**English**: The multitude will be defeated, and they will turn their backs.

### Verse 54:46
**Arabic**: بَلِ ٱلسَّاعَةُ مَوْعِدُهُمْ وَٱلسَّاعَةُ أَدْهَىٰ وَأَمَرُّ

**English**: The Hour is their appointed time-the Hour is more disastrous, and most bitter.

### Verse 54:47
**Arabic**: إِنَّ ٱلْمُجْرِمِينَ فِى ضَلَٰلٍۢ وَسُعُرٍۢ

**English**: The wicked are in confusion and madness.

### Verse 54:48
**Arabic**: يَوْمَ يُسْحَبُونَ فِى ٱلنَّارِ عَلَىٰ وُجُوهِهِمْ ذُوقُوا۟ مَسَّ سَقَرَ

**English**: The Day when they are dragged upon their faces into the Fire: "Taste the touch of Saqar."

### Verse 54:49
**Arabic**: إِنَّا كُلَّ شَىْءٍ خَلَقْنَٰهُ بِقَدَرٍۢ

**English**: Everything We created is precisely measured.

### Verse 54:50
**Arabic**: وَمَآ أَمْرُنَآ إِلَّا وَٰحِدَةٌۭ كَلَمْحٍۭ بِٱلْبَصَرِ

**English**: And Our command is but once, like the twinkling of an eye.

### Verse 54:51
**Arabic**: وَلَقَدْ أَهْلَكْنَآ أَشْيَاعَكُمْ فَهَلْ مِن مُّدَّكِرٍۢ

**English**: We have destroyed your likes. Is there anyone who would ponder?

### Verse 54:52
**Arabic**: وَكُلُّ شَىْءٍۢ فَعَلُوهُ فِى ٱلزُّبُرِ

**English**: Everything they have done is in the Books.

### Verse 54:53
**Arabic**: وَكُلُّ صَغِيرٍۢ وَكَبِيرٍۢ مُّسْتَطَرٌ

**English**: Everything, small or large, is written down.

### Verse 54:54
**Arabic**: إِنَّ ٱلْمُتَّقِينَ فِى جَنَّٰتٍۢ وَنَهَرٍۢ

**English**: The righteous will be amidst gardens and rivers.

### Verse 54:55
**Arabic**: فِى مَقْعَدِ صِدْقٍ عِندَ مَلِيكٍۢ مُّقْتَدِرٍۭ

**English**: In an assembly of virtue, in the presence of an Omnipotent King.
