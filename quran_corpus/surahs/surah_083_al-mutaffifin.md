# Surah 83: Al-Mutaffifin (المطففين)

- **Mushaf number**: 83
- **Place of revelation**: Mecca (Makkiyah)
- **Verse count**: 36
- **Themes**: [Ethics and Character](../themes/ethics.md)
- **Named entities mentioned**: [Allah](../entities/divine_allah.md), [Ar-Rahim (the Most Merciful)](../entities/divine_ar-rahim.md), [Ar-Rahman (the Most Gracious)](../entities/divine_ar-rahman.md), [Rabb (the Lord)](../entities/divine_rabb.md)

## Verses

### Verse 83:0
**Arabic**: بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

**English**: In the name of God, the Gracious, the Merciful.

### Verse 83:1
**Arabic**: وَيْلٌۭ لِّلْمُطَفِّفِينَ

**English**: Woe to the defrauders.

### Verse 83:2
**Arabic**: ٱلَّذِينَ إِذَا ٱكْتَالُوا۟ عَلَى ٱلنَّاسِ يَسْتَوْفُونَ

**English**: Those who, when they take a measure from people, they take in full.

### Verse 83:3
**Arabic**: وَإِذَا كَالُوهُمْ أَو وَّزَنُوهُمْ يُخْسِرُونَ

**English**: But when they measure or weigh to others, they cheat.

### Verse 83:4
**Arabic**: أَلَا يَظُنُّ أُو۟لَٰٓئِكَ أَنَّهُم مَّبْعُوثُونَ

**English**: Do these not know that they will be resurrected?

### Verse 83:5
**Arabic**: لِيَوْمٍ عَظِيمٍۢ

**English**: For a Great Day?

### Verse 83:6
**Arabic**: يَوْمَ يَقُومُ ٱلنَّاسُ لِرَبِّ ٱلْعَٰلَمِينَ

**English**: The Day when mankind will stand before the Lord of the Worlds?

### Verse 83:7
**Arabic**: كَلَّآ إِنَّ كِتَٰبَ ٱلْفُجَّارِ لَفِى سِجِّينٍۢ

**English**: Not at all. The record of the wicked is in Sijjeen.

### Verse 83:8
**Arabic**: وَمَآ أَدْرَىٰكَ مَا سِجِّينٌۭ

**English**: But how can you know what Sijjeen is?

### Verse 83:9
**Arabic**: كِتَٰبٌۭ مَّرْقُومٌۭ

**English**: A numerical book.

### Verse 83:10
**Arabic**: وَيْلٌۭ يَوْمَئِذٍۢ لِّلْمُكَذِّبِينَ

**English**: Woe on that Day to the deniers.

### Verse 83:11
**Arabic**: ٱلَّذِينَ يُكَذِّبُونَ بِيَوْمِ ٱلدِّينِ

**English**: Those who deny the Day of Reckoning.

### Verse 83:12
**Arabic**: وَمَا يُكَذِّبُ بِهِۦٓ إِلَّا كُلُّ مُعْتَدٍ أَثِيمٍ

**English**: But none denies it except the sinful aggressor.

### Verse 83:13
**Arabic**: إِذَا تُتْلَىٰ عَلَيْهِ ءَايَٰتُنَا قَالَ أَسَٰطِيرُ ٱلْأَوَّلِينَ

**English**: When Our revelations are recited to him, he says, "Legends of the ancients."

### Verse 83:14
**Arabic**: كَلَّا ۖ بَلْ ۜ رَانَ عَلَىٰ قُلُوبِهِم مَّا كَانُوا۟ يَكْسِبُونَ

**English**: Not at all. Their hearts have become corroded by what they used to earn.

### Verse 83:15
**Arabic**: كَلَّآ إِنَّهُمْ عَن رَّبِّهِمْ يَوْمَئِذٍۢ لَّمَحْجُوبُونَ

**English**: Not at all. On that Day, they will be screened from their Lord.

### Verse 83:16
**Arabic**: ثُمَّ إِنَّهُمْ لَصَالُوا۟ ٱلْجَحِيمِ

**English**: Then they will roast in Hell.

### Verse 83:17
**Arabic**: ثُمَّ يُقَالُ هَٰذَا ٱلَّذِى كُنتُم بِهِۦ تُكَذِّبُونَ

**English**: Then it will be said, "This is what you used to deny."

### Verse 83:18
**Arabic**: كَلَّآ إِنَّ كِتَٰبَ ٱلْأَبْرَارِ لَفِى عِلِّيِّينَ

**English**: No indeed; the record of the righteous is in Elliyyeen.

### Verse 83:19
**Arabic**: وَمَآ أَدْرَىٰكَ مَا عِلِّيُّونَ

**English**: But how can you know what Elliyyoon is?

### Verse 83:20
**Arabic**: كِتَٰبٌۭ مَّرْقُومٌۭ

**English**: A numerical book.

### Verse 83:21
**Arabic**: يَشْهَدُهُ ٱلْمُقَرَّبُونَ

**English**: Witnessed by those brought near.

### Verse 83:22
**Arabic**: إِنَّ ٱلْأَبْرَارَ لَفِى نَعِيمٍ

**English**: Indeed, the righteous will be amid bliss.

### Verse 83:23
**Arabic**: عَلَى ٱلْأَرَآئِكِ يَنظُرُونَ

**English**: On thrones, looking on.

### Verse 83:24
**Arabic**: تَعْرِفُ فِى وُجُوهِهِمْ نَضْرَةَ ٱلنَّعِيمِ

**English**: You will recognize on their faces the radiance of bliss.

### Verse 83:25
**Arabic**: يُسْقَوْنَ مِن رَّحِيقٍۢ مَّخْتُومٍ

**English**: They will be given to drink a sealed wine.

### Verse 83:26
**Arabic**: خِتَٰمُهُۥ مِسْكٌۭ ۚ وَفِى ذَٰلِكَ فَلْيَتَنَافَسِ ٱلْمُتَنَٰفِسُونَ

**English**: Whose seal is musk-this is what competitors should compete for.

### Verse 83:27
**Arabic**: وَمِزَاجُهُۥ مِن تَسْنِيمٍ

**English**: Its mixture is of Tasneem.

### Verse 83:28
**Arabic**: عَيْنًۭا يَشْرَبُ بِهَا ٱلْمُقَرَّبُونَ

**English**: A spring from which those brought near drink.

### Verse 83:29
**Arabic**: إِنَّ ٱلَّذِينَ أَجْرَمُوا۟ كَانُوا۟ مِنَ ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوا۟ يَضْحَكُونَ

**English**: Those who committed crimes used to laugh at those who believed.

### Verse 83:30
**Arabic**: وَإِذَا مَرُّوا۟ بِهِمْ يَتَغَامَزُونَ

**English**: And when they passed by them, they would wink at one another.

### Verse 83:31
**Arabic**: وَإِذَا ٱنقَلَبُوٓا۟ إِلَىٰٓ أَهْلِهِمُ ٱنقَلَبُوا۟ فَكِهِينَ

**English**: And when they went back to their families, they would go back exulting.

### Verse 83:32
**Arabic**: وَإِذَا رَأَوْهُمْ قَالُوٓا۟ إِنَّ هَٰٓؤُلَآءِ لَضَآلُّونَ

**English**: And if they saw them, they would say, "These people are lost."

### Verse 83:33
**Arabic**: وَمَآ أُرْسِلُوا۟ عَلَيْهِمْ حَٰفِظِينَ

**English**: Yet they were not sent as guardians over them.

### Verse 83:34
**Arabic**: فَٱلْيَوْمَ ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوا۟ مِنَ ٱلْكُفَّارِ يَضْحَكُونَ

**English**: But on that Day, those who believed will laugh at the unbelievers.

### Verse 83:35
**Arabic**: عَلَى ٱلْأَرَآئِكِ يَنظُرُونَ

**English**: On luxurious furnishings, looking on.

### Verse 83:36
**Arabic**: هَلْ ثُوِّبَ ٱلْكُفَّارُ مَا كَانُوا۟ يَفْعَلُونَ

**English**: Have the unbelievers been repaid for what they used to do?
