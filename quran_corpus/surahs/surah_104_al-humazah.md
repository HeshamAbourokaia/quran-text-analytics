# Surah 104: Al-Humazah (الهمزة)

- **Mushaf number**: 104
- **Place of revelation**: Mecca (Makkiyah)
- **Verse count**: 9
- **Themes**: [Warning](../themes/warning.md)
- **Named entities mentioned**: [Allah](../entities/divine_allah.md), [Ar-Rahim (the Most Merciful)](../entities/divine_ar-rahim.md), [Ar-Rahman (the Most Gracious)](../entities/divine_ar-rahman.md)

- **Revelation order (chronological)**: #32 of 114
- **Revelation phase**: [Early Meccan](../timeline/phase_early-mecca.md) (610-615 CE, Year 1-5 of Prophethood)

## Verses

### Verse 104:0
**Arabic**: بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

**English**: In the name of God, the Gracious, the Merciful.

### Verse 104:1
**Arabic**: وَيْلٌۭ لِّكُلِّ هُمَزَةٍۢ لُّمَزَةٍ

**English**: Woe to every slanderer backbiter.

### Verse 104:2
**Arabic**: ٱلَّذِى جَمَعَ مَالًۭا وَعَدَّدَهُۥ

**English**: Who gathers wealth and counts it over.

### Verse 104:3
**Arabic**: يَحْسَبُ أَنَّ مَالَهُۥٓ أَخْلَدَهُۥ

**English**: Thinking that his wealth has made him immortal.

### Verse 104:4
**Arabic**: كَلَّا ۖ لَيُنۢبَذَنَّ فِى ٱلْحُطَمَةِ

**English**: By no means. He will be thrown into the Crusher.

### Verse 104:5
**Arabic**: وَمَآ أَدْرَىٰكَ مَا ٱلْحُطَمَةُ

**English**: And what will make you realize what the Crusher is?

### Verse 104:6
**Arabic**: نَارُ ٱللَّهِ ٱلْمُوقَدَةُ

**English**: God's kindled Fire.

### Verse 104:7
**Arabic**: ٱلَّتِى تَطَّلِعُ عَلَى ٱلْأَفْـِٔدَةِ

**English**: That laps to the hearts.

### Verse 104:8
**Arabic**: إِنَّهَا عَلَيْهِم مُّؤْصَدَةٌۭ

**English**: It closes in on them.

### Verse 104:9
**Arabic**: فِى عَمَدٍۢ مُّمَدَّدَةٍۭ

**English**: In extended columns.
