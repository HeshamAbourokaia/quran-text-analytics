# Surah 52: At-Tur (الطور)

- **Mushaf number**: 52
- **Place of revelation**: Mecca (Makkiyah)
- **Verse count**: 49
- **Themes**: [Warning](../themes/warning.md)
- **Named entities mentioned**: [Allah](../entities/divine_allah.md), [Ar-Rahim (the Most Merciful)](../entities/divine_ar-rahim.md), [Ar-Rahman (the Most Gracious)](../entities/divine_ar-rahman.md), [Rabb (the Lord)](../entities/divine_rabb.md), [Mount Sinai (Tur)](../entities/place_sinai.md)

## Verses

### Verse 52:0
**Arabic**: بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

**English**: In the name of God, the Gracious, the Merciful.

### Verse 52:1
**Arabic**: وَٱلطُّورِ

**English**: By the Mount.

### Verse 52:2
**Arabic**: وَكِتَٰبٍۢ مَّسْطُورٍۢ

**English**: And a Book inscribed.

### Verse 52:3
**Arabic**: فِى رَقٍّۢ مَّنشُورٍۢ

**English**: In a published scroll.

### Verse 52:4
**Arabic**: وَٱلْبَيْتِ ٱلْمَعْمُورِ

**English**: And the frequented House.

### Verse 52:5
**Arabic**: وَٱلسَّقْفِ ٱلْمَرْفُوعِ

**English**: And the elevated roof.

### Verse 52:6
**Arabic**: وَٱلْبَحْرِ ٱلْمَسْجُورِ

**English**: And the seething sea.

### Verse 52:7
**Arabic**: إِنَّ عَذَابَ رَبِّكَ لَوَٰقِعٌۭ

**English**: The punishment of your Lord is coming.

### Verse 52:8
**Arabic**: مَّا لَهُۥ مِن دَافِعٍۢ

**English**: There is nothing to avert it.

### Verse 52:9
**Arabic**: يَوْمَ تَمُورُ ٱلسَّمَآءُ مَوْرًۭا

**English**: On the Day when the heaven sways in agitation.

### Verse 52:10
**Arabic**: وَتَسِيرُ ٱلْجِبَالُ سَيْرًۭا

**English**: And the mountains go into motion.

### Verse 52:11
**Arabic**: فَوَيْلٌۭ يَوْمَئِذٍۢ لِّلْمُكَذِّبِينَ

**English**: Woe on that Day to the deniers.

### Verse 52:12
**Arabic**: ٱلَّذِينَ هُمْ فِى خَوْضٍۢ يَلْعَبُونَ

**English**: Those who play with speculation.

### Verse 52:13
**Arabic**: يَوْمَ يُدَعُّونَ إِلَىٰ نَارِ جَهَنَّمَ دَعًّا

**English**: The Day when they are shoved into the Fire of Hell forcefully.

### Verse 52:14
**Arabic**: هَٰذِهِ ٱلنَّارُ ٱلَّتِى كُنتُم بِهَا تُكَذِّبُونَ

**English**: "This is the Fire which you used to deny.

### Verse 52:15
**Arabic**: أَفَسِحْرٌ هَٰذَآ أَمْ أَنتُمْ لَا تُبْصِرُونَ

**English**: Is this magic, or do you not see?

### Verse 52:16
**Arabic**: ٱصْلَوْهَا فَٱصْبِرُوٓا۟ أَوْ لَا تَصْبِرُوا۟ سَوَآءٌ عَلَيْكُمْ ۖ إِنَّمَا تُجْزَوْنَ مَا كُنتُمْ تَعْمَلُونَ

**English**: Burn in it. Whether you are patient, or impatient, it is the same for you. You are only being repaid for what you used to do."

### Verse 52:17
**Arabic**: إِنَّ ٱلْمُتَّقِينَ فِى جَنَّٰتٍۢ وَنَعِيمٍۢ

**English**: But the righteous will be amid gardens and bliss.

### Verse 52:18
**Arabic**: فَٰكِهِينَ بِمَآ ءَاتَىٰهُمْ رَبُّهُمْ وَوَقَىٰهُمْ رَبُّهُمْ عَذَابَ ٱلْجَحِيمِ

**English**: Enjoying what their Lord has given them, and their Lord has spared them the suffering of Hell.

### Verse 52:19
**Arabic**: كُلُوا۟ وَٱشْرَبُوا۟ هَنِيٓـًٔۢا بِمَا كُنتُمْ تَعْمَلُونَ

**English**: Eat and drink happily, for what you used to do.

### Verse 52:20
**Arabic**: مُتَّكِـِٔينَ عَلَىٰ سُرُرٍۢ مَّصْفُوفَةٍۢ ۖ وَزَوَّجْنَٰهُم بِحُورٍ عِينٍۢ

**English**: Relaxing on luxurious furnishings; and We will couple them with gorgeous spouses.

### Verse 52:21
**Arabic**: وَٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوا۟ وَٱتَّبَعَتْهُمْ ذُرِّيَّتُهُم بِإِيمَٰنٍ أَلْحَقْنَا بِهِمْ ذُرِّيَّتَهُمْ وَمَآ أَلَتْنَٰهُم مِّنْ عَمَلِهِم مِّن شَىْءٍۢ ۚ كُلُّ ٱمْرِئٍۭ بِمَا كَسَبَ رَهِينٌۭ

**English**: Those who believed and their offspring followed them in faith-We will unite them with their offspring, and We will not deprive them of any of their works. Every person is hostage to what he has earned.

### Verse 52:22
**Arabic**: وَأَمْدَدْنَٰهُم بِفَٰكِهَةٍۢ وَلَحْمٍۢ مِّمَّا يَشْتَهُونَ

**English**: And We will supply them with fruit, and meat; such as they desire.

### Verse 52:23
**Arabic**: يَتَنَٰزَعُونَ فِيهَا كَأْسًۭا لَّا لَغْوٌۭ فِيهَا وَلَا تَأْثِيمٌۭ

**English**: They will exchange therein a cup; wherein is neither harm, nor sin.

### Verse 52:24
**Arabic**: ۞ وَيَطُوفُ عَلَيْهِمْ غِلْمَانٌۭ لَّهُمْ كَأَنَّهُمْ لُؤْلُؤٌۭ مَّكْنُونٌۭ

**English**: Serving them will be youths like hidden pearls.

### Verse 52:25
**Arabic**: وَأَقْبَلَ بَعْضُهُمْ عَلَىٰ بَعْضٍۢ يَتَسَآءَلُونَ

**English**: And they will approach one another, inquiring.

### Verse 52:26
**Arabic**: قَالُوٓا۟ إِنَّا كُنَّا قَبْلُ فِىٓ أَهْلِنَا مُشْفِقِينَ

**English**: They will say, "Before this, we were fearful for our families.

### Verse 52:27
**Arabic**: فَمَنَّ ٱللَّهُ عَلَيْنَا وَوَقَىٰنَا عَذَابَ ٱلسَّمُومِ

**English**: But God blessed us, and spared us the agony of the Fiery Winds.

### Verse 52:28
**Arabic**: إِنَّا كُنَّا مِن قَبْلُ نَدْعُوهُ ۖ إِنَّهُۥ هُوَ ٱلْبَرُّ ٱلرَّحِيمُ

**English**: Before this, we used to pray to Him. He is the Good, the Compassionate."

### Verse 52:29
**Arabic**: فَذَكِّرْ فَمَآ أَنتَ بِنِعْمَتِ رَبِّكَ بِكَاهِنٍۢ وَلَا مَجْنُونٍ

**English**: So remind. By the grace of your Lord, you are neither a soothsayer, nor a madman.

### Verse 52:30
**Arabic**: أَمْ يَقُولُونَ شَاعِرٌۭ نَّتَرَبَّصُ بِهِۦ رَيْبَ ٱلْمَنُونِ

**English**: Or do they say, "A poet-we await for him a calamity of time"?

### Verse 52:31
**Arabic**: قُلْ تَرَبَّصُوا۟ فَإِنِّى مَعَكُم مِّنَ ٱلْمُتَرَبِّصِينَ

**English**: Say, "Go on waiting; I will be waiting with you."

### Verse 52:32
**Arabic**: أَمْ تَأْمُرُهُمْ أَحْلَٰمُهُم بِهَٰذَآ ۚ أَمْ هُمْ قَوْمٌۭ طَاغُونَ

**English**: Or is it that their dreams compel them to this? Or are they aggressive people?

### Verse 52:33
**Arabic**: أَمْ يَقُولُونَ تَقَوَّلَهُۥ ۚ بَل لَّا يُؤْمِنُونَ

**English**: Or do they say, "He made it up"? Rather, they do not believe.

### Verse 52:34
**Arabic**: فَلْيَأْتُوا۟ بِحَدِيثٍۢ مِّثْلِهِۦٓ إِن كَانُوا۟ صَٰدِقِينَ

**English**: So let them produce a discourse like it, if they are truthful.

### Verse 52:35
**Arabic**: أَمْ خُلِقُوا۟ مِنْ غَيْرِ شَىْءٍ أَمْ هُمُ ٱلْخَٰلِقُونَ

**English**: Or were they created out of nothing? Or are they the creators?

### Verse 52:36
**Arabic**: أَمْ خَلَقُوا۟ ٱلسَّمَٰوَٰتِ وَٱلْأَرْضَ ۚ بَل لَّا يُوقِنُونَ

**English**: Or did they create the heavens and the earth? In fact, they are not certain.

### Verse 52:37
**Arabic**: أَمْ عِندَهُمْ خَزَآئِنُ رَبِّكَ أَمْ هُمُ ٱلْمُصَۣيْطِرُونَ

**English**: Or do they possess the treasuries of your Lord? Or are they the controllers?

### Verse 52:38
**Arabic**: أَمْ لَهُمْ سُلَّمٌۭ يَسْتَمِعُونَ فِيهِ ۖ فَلْيَأْتِ مُسْتَمِعُهُم بِسُلْطَٰنٍۢ مُّبِينٍ

**English**: Or do they have a stairway by means of which they listen? Then let their listener produce a clear proof.

### Verse 52:39
**Arabic**: أَمْ لَهُ ٱلْبَنَٰتُ وَلَكُمُ ٱلْبَنُونَ

**English**: Or for Him the daughters, and for you the sons?

### Verse 52:40
**Arabic**: أَمْ تَسْـَٔلُهُمْ أَجْرًۭا فَهُم مِّن مَّغْرَمٍۢ مُّثْقَلُونَ

**English**: Or do you demand a payment from them, and they are burdened by debt?

### Verse 52:41
**Arabic**: أَمْ عِندَهُمُ ٱلْغَيْبُ فَهُمْ يَكْتُبُونَ

**English**: Or do they know the future, and they are writing it down?

### Verse 52:42
**Arabic**: أَمْ يُرِيدُونَ كَيْدًۭا ۖ فَٱلَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا۟ هُمُ ٱلْمَكِيدُونَ

**English**: Or are they planning a conspiracy? The conspiracy will befall the disbelievers.

### Verse 52:43
**Arabic**: أَمْ لَهُمْ إِلَٰهٌ غَيْرُ ٱللَّهِ ۚ سُبْحَٰنَ ٱللَّهِ عَمَّا يُشْرِكُونَ

**English**: Or do they have a god besides God? God transcends what they associate.

### Verse 52:44
**Arabic**: وَإِن يَرَوْا۟ كِسْفًۭا مِّنَ ٱلسَّمَآءِ سَاقِطًۭا يَقُولُوا۟ سَحَابٌۭ مَّرْكُومٌۭ

**English**: Even if they were to see lumps of the sky falling down, they would say, "A mass of clouds."

### Verse 52:45
**Arabic**: فَذَرْهُمْ حَتَّىٰ يُلَٰقُوا۟ يَوْمَهُمُ ٱلَّذِى فِيهِ يُصْعَقُونَ

**English**: So leave them until they meet their Day in which they will be stunned.

### Verse 52:46
**Arabic**: يَوْمَ لَا يُغْنِى عَنْهُمْ كَيْدُهُمْ شَيْـًۭٔا وَلَا هُمْ يُنصَرُونَ

**English**: The Day when their ploys will avail them nothing; and they will not be helped.

### Verse 52:47
**Arabic**: وَإِنَّ لِلَّذِينَ ظَلَمُوا۟ عَذَابًۭا دُونَ ذَٰلِكَ وَلَٰكِنَّ أَكْثَرَهُمْ لَا يَعْلَمُونَ

**English**: For those who do wrong, there is a punishment besides that; but most of them do not know.

### Verse 52:48
**Arabic**: وَٱصْبِرْ لِحُكْمِ رَبِّكَ فَإِنَّكَ بِأَعْيُنِنَا ۖ وَسَبِّحْ بِحَمْدِ رَبِّكَ حِينَ تَقُومُ

**English**: So patiently await the decision of your Lord, for you are before Our Eyes; and proclaim the praises of your Lord when you arise.

### Verse 52:49
**Arabic**: وَمِنَ ٱلَّيْلِ فَسَبِّحْهُ وَإِدْبَٰرَ ٱلنُّجُومِ

**English**: And glorify Him during the night, and at the receding of the stars.
