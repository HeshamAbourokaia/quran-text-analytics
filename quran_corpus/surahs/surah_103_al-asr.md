# Surah 103: Al-Asr (العصر)

- **Mushaf number**: 103
- **Place of revelation**: Mecca (Makkiyah)
- **Verse count**: 3
- **Themes**: [Faith and Monotheism](../themes/faith-monotheism.md)
- **Named entities mentioned**: [Allah](../entities/divine_allah.md), [Ar-Rahim (the Most Merciful)](../entities/divine_ar-rahim.md), [Ar-Rahman (the Most Gracious)](../entities/divine_ar-rahman.md)

- **Revelation order (chronological)**: #13 of 114
- **Revelation phase**: [Early Meccan](../timeline/phase_early-mecca.md) (610-615 CE, Year 1-5 of Prophethood)

## Verses

### Verse 103:0
**Arabic**: بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

**English**: In the name of God, the Gracious, the Merciful.

### Verse 103:1
**Arabic**: وَٱلْعَصْرِ

**English**: By time.

### Verse 103:2
**Arabic**: إِنَّ ٱلْإِنسَٰنَ لَفِى خُسْرٍ

**English**: The human being is in loss.

### Verse 103:3
**Arabic**: إِلَّا ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوا۟ وَعَمِلُوا۟ ٱلصَّٰلِحَٰتِ وَتَوَاصَوْا۟ بِٱلْحَقِّ وَتَوَاصَوْا۟ بِٱلصَّبْرِ

**English**: Except those who believe, and do good works, and encourage truth, and recommend patience.
