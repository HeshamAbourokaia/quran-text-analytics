# Surah 97: Al-Qadr (القدر)

- **Mushaf number**: 97
- **Place of revelation**: Mecca (Makkiyah)
- **Verse count**: 5
- **Themes**: [Worship and Devotion](../themes/worship.md)
- **Named entities mentioned**: [Allah](../entities/divine_allah.md), [Ar-Rahim (the Most Merciful)](../entities/divine_ar-rahim.md), [Ar-Rahman (the Most Gracious)](../entities/divine_ar-rahman.md), [Rabb (the Lord)](../entities/divine_rabb.md)

- **Revelation order (chronological)**: #25 of 114
- **Revelation phase**: [Early Meccan](../timeline/phase_early-mecca.md) (610-615 CE, Year 1-5 of Prophethood)

## Verses

### Verse 97:0
**Arabic**: بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

**English**: In the name of God, the Gracious, the Merciful.

### Verse 97:1
**Arabic**: إِنَّآ أَنزَلْنَٰهُ فِى لَيْلَةِ ٱلْقَدْرِ

**English**: We sent it down on the Night of Decree.

### Verse 97:2
**Arabic**: وَمَآ أَدْرَىٰكَ مَا لَيْلَةُ ٱلْقَدْرِ

**English**: But what will convey to you what the Night of Decree is?

### Verse 97:3
**Arabic**: لَيْلَةُ ٱلْقَدْرِ خَيْرٌۭ مِّنْ أَلْفِ شَهْرٍۢ

**English**: The Night of Decree is better than a thousand months.

### Verse 97:4
**Arabic**: تَنَزَّلُ ٱلْمَلَٰٓئِكَةُ وَٱلرُّوحُ فِيهَا بِإِذْنِ رَبِّهِم مِّن كُلِّ أَمْرٍۢ

**English**: In it descend the angels and the Spirit, by the leave of their Lord, with every command.

### Verse 97:5
**Arabic**: سَلَٰمٌ هِىَ حَتَّىٰ مَطْلَعِ ٱلْفَجْرِ

**English**: Peace it is; until the rise of dawn.
