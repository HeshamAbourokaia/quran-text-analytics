# Surah 77: Al-Mursalat (المرسلات)

- **Mushaf number**: 77
- **Place of revelation**: Mecca (Makkiyah)
- **Verse count**: 50
- **Themes**: [Day of Judgment](../themes/day-of-judgment.md)
- **Named entities mentioned**: [Allah](../entities/divine_allah.md), [Ar-Rahim (the Most Merciful)](../entities/divine_ar-rahim.md), [Ar-Rahman (the Most Gracious)](../entities/divine_ar-rahman.md), [Rabb (the Lord)](../entities/divine_rabb.md)

## Verses

### Verse 77:0
**Arabic**: بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

**English**: In the name of God, the Gracious, the Merciful.

### Verse 77:1
**Arabic**: وَٱلْمُرْسَلَٰتِ عُرْفًۭا

**English**: By those unleashed in succession.

### Verse 77:2
**Arabic**: فَٱلْعَٰصِفَٰتِ عَصْفًۭا

**English**: Storming turbulently.

### Verse 77:3
**Arabic**: وَٱلنَّٰشِرَٰتِ نَشْرًۭا

**English**: Scattering far and wide.

### Verse 77:4
**Arabic**: فَٱلْفَٰرِقَٰتِ فَرْقًۭا

**English**: Separating decisively.

### Verse 77:5
**Arabic**: فَٱلْمُلْقِيَٰتِ ذِكْرًا

**English**: Delivering a message.

### Verse 77:6
**Arabic**: عُذْرًا أَوْ نُذْرًا

**English**: Excusing or warning.

### Verse 77:7
**Arabic**: إِنَّمَا تُوعَدُونَ لَوَٰقِعٌۭ

**English**: Surely what you are promised will happen.

### Verse 77:8
**Arabic**: فَإِذَا ٱلنُّجُومُ طُمِسَتْ

**English**: When the stars are obliterated.

### Verse 77:9
**Arabic**: وَإِذَا ٱلسَّمَآءُ فُرِجَتْ

**English**: And the sky is fractured.

### Verse 77:10
**Arabic**: وَإِذَا ٱلْجِبَالُ نُسِفَتْ

**English**: And the mountains are blown away.

### Verse 77:11
**Arabic**: وَإِذَا ٱلرُّسُلُ أُقِّتَتْ

**English**: And the messengers are alerted.

### Verse 77:12
**Arabic**: لِأَىِّ يَوْمٍ أُجِّلَتْ

**English**: Until which day is it deferred?

### Verse 77:13
**Arabic**: لِيَوْمِ ٱلْفَصْلِ

**English**: Until the Day of Decision.

### Verse 77:14
**Arabic**: وَمَآ أَدْرَىٰكَ مَا يَوْمُ ٱلْفَصْلِ

**English**: And what will teach you what the Day of Decision is?

### Verse 77:15
**Arabic**: وَيْلٌۭ يَوْمَئِذٍۢ لِّلْمُكَذِّبِينَ

**English**: Woe on that Day to the liars.

### Verse 77:16
**Arabic**: أَلَمْ نُهْلِكِ ٱلْأَوَّلِينَ

**English**: Did We not destroy the ancients?

### Verse 77:17
**Arabic**: ثُمَّ نُتْبِعُهُمُ ٱلْءَاخِرِينَ

**English**: Then succeeded them with the others?

### Verse 77:18
**Arabic**: كَذَٰلِكَ نَفْعَلُ بِٱلْمُجْرِمِينَ

**English**: This is how We deal with the guilty.

### Verse 77:19
**Arabic**: وَيْلٌۭ يَوْمَئِذٍۢ لِّلْمُكَذِّبِينَ

**English**: Woe on that Day to the rejecters.

### Verse 77:20
**Arabic**: أَلَمْ نَخْلُقكُّم مِّن مَّآءٍۢ مَّهِينٍۢ

**English**: Did We not create you from an insignificant fluid?

### Verse 77:21
**Arabic**: فَجَعَلْنَٰهُ فِى قَرَارٍۢ مَّكِينٍ

**English**: Then lodged it in a secure place?

### Verse 77:22
**Arabic**: إِلَىٰ قَدَرٍۢ مَّعْلُومٍۢ

**English**: For a known term?

### Verse 77:23
**Arabic**: فَقَدَرْنَا فَنِعْمَ ٱلْقَٰدِرُونَ

**English**: We measured precisely. We are the best to measure.

### Verse 77:24
**Arabic**: وَيْلٌۭ يَوْمَئِذٍۢ لِّلْمُكَذِّبِينَ

**English**: Woe on that Day to the falsifiers.

### Verse 77:25
**Arabic**: أَلَمْ نَجْعَلِ ٱلْأَرْضَ كِفَاتًا

**English**: Did We not make the earth a homestead?

### Verse 77:26
**Arabic**: أَحْيَآءًۭ وَأَمْوَٰتًۭا

**English**: For the living and the dead?

### Verse 77:27
**Arabic**: وَجَعَلْنَا فِيهَا رَوَٰسِىَ شَٰمِخَٰتٍۢ وَأَسْقَيْنَٰكُم مَّآءًۭ فُرَاتًۭا

**English**: And set on it lofty mountains, and given you pure water to drink?

### Verse 77:28
**Arabic**: وَيْلٌۭ يَوْمَئِذٍۢ لِّلْمُكَذِّبِينَ

**English**: Woe on that Day to the deniers.

### Verse 77:29
**Arabic**: ٱنطَلِقُوٓا۟ إِلَىٰ مَا كُنتُم بِهِۦ تُكَذِّبُونَ

**English**: "Proceed to what you used to deny."

### Verse 77:30
**Arabic**: ٱنطَلِقُوٓا۟ إِلَىٰ ظِلٍّۢ ذِى ثَلَٰثِ شُعَبٍۢ

**English**: "Proceed to a shadow of three different masses."

### Verse 77:31
**Arabic**: لَّا ظَلِيلٍۢ وَلَا يُغْنِى مِنَ ٱللَّهَبِ

**English**: Offering no shade, and unavailing against the flames.

### Verse 77:32
**Arabic**: إِنَّهَا تَرْمِى بِشَرَرٍۢ كَٱلْقَصْرِ

**English**: It shoots sparks as castles.

### Verse 77:33
**Arabic**: كَأَنَّهُۥ جِمَٰلَتٌۭ صُفْرٌۭ

**English**: As if they were yellow camels.

### Verse 77:34
**Arabic**: وَيْلٌۭ يَوْمَئِذٍۢ لِّلْمُكَذِّبِينَ

**English**: Woe on that Day to the liars.

### Verse 77:35
**Arabic**: هَٰذَا يَوْمُ لَا يَنطِقُونَ

**English**: This is a Day when they will not speak.

### Verse 77:36
**Arabic**: وَلَا يُؤْذَنُ لَهُمْ فَيَعْتَذِرُونَ

**English**: And they will not be allowed to apologize.

### Verse 77:37
**Arabic**: وَيْلٌۭ يَوْمَئِذٍۢ لِّلْمُكَذِّبِينَ

**English**: Woe on that Day to the rejecters.

### Verse 77:38
**Arabic**: هَٰذَا يَوْمُ ٱلْفَصْلِ ۖ جَمَعْنَٰكُمْ وَٱلْأَوَّلِينَ

**English**: This is the Day of Separation; We have gathered you, together with the ancients.

### Verse 77:39
**Arabic**: فَإِن كَانَ لَكُمْ كَيْدٌۭ فَكِيدُونِ

**English**: So if you have a strategy, use it against Me.

### Verse 77:40
**Arabic**: وَيْلٌۭ يَوْمَئِذٍۢ لِّلْمُكَذِّبِينَ

**English**: Woe on that Day to the falsifiers.

### Verse 77:41
**Arabic**: إِنَّ ٱلْمُتَّقِينَ فِى ظِلَٰلٍۢ وَعُيُونٍۢ

**English**: The righteous will be amidst shades and fountains.

### Verse 77:42
**Arabic**: وَفَوَٰكِهَ مِمَّا يَشْتَهُونَ

**English**: And fruits as they desire.

### Verse 77:43
**Arabic**: كُلُوا۟ وَٱشْرَبُوا۟ هَنِيٓـًٔۢا بِمَا كُنتُمْ تَعْمَلُونَ

**English**: "Eat and drink pleasantly, for what you used to do."

### Verse 77:44
**Arabic**: إِنَّا كَذَٰلِكَ نَجْزِى ٱلْمُحْسِنِينَ

**English**: This is how We reward the doers of good.

### Verse 77:45
**Arabic**: وَيْلٌۭ يَوْمَئِذٍۢ لِّلْمُكَذِّبِينَ

**English**: Woe on that Day to the deniers.

### Verse 77:46
**Arabic**: كُلُوا۟ وَتَمَتَّعُوا۟ قَلِيلًا إِنَّكُم مُّجْرِمُونَ

**English**: "Eat and enjoy yourselves a little; you are indeed criminals."

### Verse 77:47
**Arabic**: وَيْلٌۭ يَوْمَئِذٍۢ لِّلْمُكَذِّبِينَ

**English**: Woe on that Day to the liars.

### Verse 77:48
**Arabic**: وَإِذَا قِيلَ لَهُمُ ٱرْكَعُوا۟ لَا يَرْكَعُونَ

**English**: And when it is said to them, "Kneel", they do not kneel.

### Verse 77:49
**Arabic**: وَيْلٌۭ يَوْمَئِذٍۢ لِّلْمُكَذِّبِينَ

**English**: Woe on that Day to the rejecters.

### Verse 77:50
**Arabic**: فَبِأَىِّ حَدِيثٍۭ بَعْدَهُۥ يُؤْمِنُونَ

**English**: In what message, beyond this, will they believe?
