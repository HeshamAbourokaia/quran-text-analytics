# Surah 112: Al-Ikhlas (الإخلاص)

- **Mushaf number**: 112
- **Place of revelation**: Mecca (Makkiyah)
- **Verse count**: 4
- **Themes**: [Faith and Monotheism](../themes/faith-monotheism.md)
- **Named entities mentioned**: [Allah](../entities/divine_allah.md), [Ar-Rahim (the Most Merciful)](../entities/divine_ar-rahim.md), [Ar-Rahman (the Most Gracious)](../entities/divine_ar-rahman.md)

## Verses

### Verse 112:0
**Arabic**: بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

**English**: In the name of God, the Gracious, the Merciful.

### Verse 112:1
**Arabic**: قُلْ هُوَ ٱللَّهُ أَحَدٌ

**English**: Say, "He is God, the One.

### Verse 112:2
**Arabic**: ٱللَّهُ ٱلصَّمَدُ

**English**: God, the Absolute.

### Verse 112:3
**Arabic**: لَمْ يَلِدْ وَلَمْ يُولَدْ

**English**: He begets not, nor was He begotten.

### Verse 112:4
**Arabic**: وَلَمْ يَكُن لَّهُۥ كُفُوًا أَحَدٌۢ

**English**: And there is nothing comparable to Him."
