# Surah 79: An-Naziat (النازعات)

- **Mushaf number**: 79
- **Place of revelation**: Mecca (Makkiyah)
- **Verse count**: 46
- **Themes**: [Day of Judgment](../themes/day-of-judgment.md)
- **Named entities mentioned**: [Allah](../entities/divine_allah.md), [Ar-Rahim (the Most Merciful)](../entities/divine_ar-rahim.md), [Ar-Rahman (the Most Gracious)](../entities/divine_ar-rahman.md), [Pharaoh (Firaun)](../entities/figure_firaun.md), [Moses (Musa)](../entities/prophet_musa.md), [Rabb (the Lord)](../entities/divine_rabb.md)

- **Revelation order (chronological)**: #81 of 114
- **Revelation phase**: [Late Meccan](../timeline/phase_late-mecca.md) (619-622 CE, Year 9-13 of Prophethood)

## Verses

### Verse 79:0
**Arabic**: بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

**English**: In the name of God, the Gracious, the Merciful.

### Verse 79:1
**Arabic**: وَٱلنَّٰزِعَٰتِ غَرْقًۭا

**English**: By those who snatch violently.

### Verse 79:2
**Arabic**: وَٱلنَّٰشِطَٰتِ نَشْطًۭا

**English**: And those who remove gently.

### Verse 79:3
**Arabic**: وَٱلسَّٰبِحَٰتِ سَبْحًۭا

**English**: And those who glide smoothly.

### Verse 79:4
**Arabic**: فَٱلسَّٰبِقَٰتِ سَبْقًۭا

**English**: And those who race swiftly.

### Verse 79:5
**Arabic**: فَٱلْمُدَبِّرَٰتِ أَمْرًۭا

**English**: And those who regulate events.

### Verse 79:6
**Arabic**: يَوْمَ تَرْجُفُ ٱلرَّاجِفَةُ

**English**: On the Day when the Quake quakes.

### Verse 79:7
**Arabic**: تَتْبَعُهَا ٱلرَّادِفَةُ

**English**: And is followed by the Successor.

### Verse 79:8
**Arabic**: قُلُوبٌۭ يَوْمَئِذٍۢ وَاجِفَةٌ

**English**: Hearts on that Day will be pounding.

### Verse 79:9
**Arabic**: أَبْصَٰرُهَا خَٰشِعَةٌۭ

**English**: Their sights downcast.

### Verse 79:10
**Arabic**: يَقُولُونَ أَءِنَّا لَمَرْدُودُونَ فِى ٱلْحَافِرَةِ

**English**: They say, "Are we to be restored to the original condition?

### Verse 79:11
**Arabic**: أَءِذَا كُنَّا عِظَٰمًۭا نَّخِرَةًۭ

**English**: When we have become hollow bones?"

### Verse 79:12
**Arabic**: قَالُوا۟ تِلْكَ إِذًۭا كَرَّةٌ خَاسِرَةٌۭ

**English**: They say, "This is a losing proposition."

### Verse 79:13
**Arabic**: فَإِنَّمَا هِىَ زَجْرَةٌۭ وَٰحِدَةٌۭ

**English**: But it will be only a single nudge.

### Verse 79:14
**Arabic**: فَإِذَا هُم بِٱلسَّاهِرَةِ

**English**: And they will be awake.

### Verse 79:15
**Arabic**: هَلْ أَتَىٰكَ حَدِيثُ مُوسَىٰٓ

**English**: Has the story of Moses reached you?

### Verse 79:16
**Arabic**: إِذْ نَادَىٰهُ رَبُّهُۥ بِٱلْوَادِ ٱلْمُقَدَّسِ طُوًى

**English**: When His Lord called out to him in the sacred valley of Tuwa.

### Verse 79:17
**Arabic**: ٱذْهَبْ إِلَىٰ فِرْعَوْنَ إِنَّهُۥ طَغَىٰ

**English**: "Go to Pharaoh-he has transgressed."

### Verse 79:18
**Arabic**: فَقُلْ هَل لَّكَ إِلَىٰٓ أَن تَزَكَّىٰ

**English**: And say, "Do you care to be cleansed?

### Verse 79:19
**Arabic**: وَأَهْدِيَكَ إِلَىٰ رَبِّكَ فَتَخْشَىٰ

**English**: And I will guide you to your Lord, and you will turn reverent."

### Verse 79:20
**Arabic**: فَأَرَىٰهُ ٱلْءَايَةَ ٱلْكُبْرَىٰ

**English**: He showed him the Greatest Miracle.

### Verse 79:21
**Arabic**: فَكَذَّبَ وَعَصَىٰ

**English**: But he denied and defied.

### Verse 79:22
**Arabic**: ثُمَّ أَدْبَرَ يَسْعَىٰ

**English**: Then turned his back, and tried.

### Verse 79:23
**Arabic**: فَحَشَرَ فَنَادَىٰ

**English**: And gathered and proclaimed.

### Verse 79:24
**Arabic**: فَقَالَ أَنَا۠ رَبُّكُمُ ٱلْأَعْلَىٰ

**English**: He said, "I am your Lord, the most high."

### Verse 79:25
**Arabic**: فَأَخَذَهُ ٱللَّهُ نَكَالَ ٱلْءَاخِرَةِ وَٱلْأُولَىٰٓ

**English**: So God seized him with an exemplary punishment, in the last and in the first.

### Verse 79:26
**Arabic**: إِنَّ فِى ذَٰلِكَ لَعِبْرَةًۭ لِّمَن يَخْشَىٰٓ

**English**: In this is a lesson for whoever fears.

### Verse 79:27
**Arabic**: ءَأَنتُمْ أَشَدُّ خَلْقًا أَمِ ٱلسَّمَآءُ ۚ بَنَىٰهَا

**English**: Are you more difficult to create, or the heaven? He constructed it.

### Verse 79:28
**Arabic**: رَفَعَ سَمْكَهَا فَسَوَّىٰهَا

**English**: He raised its masses, and proportioned it.

### Verse 79:29
**Arabic**: وَأَغْطَشَ لَيْلَهَا وَأَخْرَجَ ضُحَىٰهَا

**English**: And He dimmed its night, and brought out its daylight.

### Verse 79:30
**Arabic**: وَٱلْأَرْضَ بَعْدَ ذَٰلِكَ دَحَىٰهَآ

**English**: And the earth after that He spread.

### Verse 79:31
**Arabic**: أَخْرَجَ مِنْهَا مَآءَهَا وَمَرْعَىٰهَا

**English**: And from it, He produced its water and its pasture.

### Verse 79:32
**Arabic**: وَٱلْجِبَالَ أَرْسَىٰهَا

**English**: And the mountains, He anchored.

### Verse 79:33
**Arabic**: مَتَٰعًۭا لَّكُمْ وَلِأَنْعَٰمِكُمْ

**English**: A source of enjoyment for you and for your animals.

### Verse 79:34
**Arabic**: فَإِذَا جَآءَتِ ٱلطَّآمَّةُ ٱلْكُبْرَىٰ

**English**: But when the Great Cataclysm arrives.

### Verse 79:35
**Arabic**: يَوْمَ يَتَذَكَّرُ ٱلْإِنسَٰنُ مَا سَعَىٰ

**English**: A Day when man will remember what he has endeavored.

### Verse 79:36
**Arabic**: وَبُرِّزَتِ ٱلْجَحِيمُ لِمَن يَرَىٰ

**English**: And Hell will be displayed to whoever sees.

### Verse 79:37
**Arabic**: فَأَمَّا مَن طَغَىٰ

**English**: As for him who was defiant.

### Verse 79:38
**Arabic**: وَءَاثَرَ ٱلْحَيَوٰةَ ٱلدُّنْيَا

**English**: And preferred the life of this world.

### Verse 79:39
**Arabic**: فَإِنَّ ٱلْجَحِيمَ هِىَ ٱلْمَأْوَىٰ

**English**: Then Hell is the shelter.

### Verse 79:40
**Arabic**: وَأَمَّا مَنْ خَافَ مَقَامَ رَبِّهِۦ وَنَهَى ٱلنَّفْسَ عَنِ ٱلْهَوَىٰ

**English**: But as for him who feared the Standing of his Lord, and restrained the self from desires.

### Verse 79:41
**Arabic**: فَإِنَّ ٱلْجَنَّةَ هِىَ ٱلْمَأْوَىٰ

**English**: Then Paradise is the shelter.

### Verse 79:42
**Arabic**: يَسْـَٔلُونَكَ عَنِ ٱلسَّاعَةِ أَيَّانَ مُرْسَىٰهَا

**English**: They ask you about the Hour, "When will it take place?"

### Verse 79:43
**Arabic**: فِيمَ أَنتَ مِن ذِكْرَىٰهَآ

**English**: You have no knowledge of it.

### Verse 79:44
**Arabic**: إِلَىٰ رَبِّكَ مُنتَهَىٰهَآ

**English**: To your Lord is its finality.

### Verse 79:45
**Arabic**: إِنَّمَآ أَنتَ مُنذِرُ مَن يَخْشَىٰهَا

**English**: You are just a warner for whoever dreads it.

### Verse 79:46
**Arabic**: كَأَنَّهُمْ يَوْمَ يَرَوْنَهَا لَمْ يَلْبَثُوٓا۟ إِلَّا عَشِيَّةً أَوْ ضُحَىٰهَا

**English**: On the Day when they witness it-as though they only stayed an evening, or its morning.
