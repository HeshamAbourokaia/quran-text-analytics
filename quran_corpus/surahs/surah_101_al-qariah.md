# Surah 101: Al-Qariah (القارعة)

- **Mushaf number**: 101
- **Place of revelation**: Mecca (Makkiyah)
- **Verse count**: 11
- **Themes**: [Day of Judgment](../themes/day-of-judgment.md)
- **Named entities mentioned**: [Allah](../entities/divine_allah.md), [Ar-Rahim (the Most Merciful)](../entities/divine_ar-rahim.md), [Ar-Rahman (the Most Gracious)](../entities/divine_ar-rahman.md)

## Verses

### Verse 101:0
**Arabic**: بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

**English**: In the name of God, the Gracious, the Merciful.

### Verse 101:1
**Arabic**: ٱلْقَارِعَةُ

**English**: The Shocker.

### Verse 101:2
**Arabic**: مَا ٱلْقَارِعَةُ

**English**: What is the Shocker?

### Verse 101:3
**Arabic**: وَمَآ أَدْرَىٰكَ مَا ٱلْقَارِعَةُ

**English**: What will explain to you what the Shocker is?

### Verse 101:4
**Arabic**: يَوْمَ يَكُونُ ٱلنَّاسُ كَٱلْفَرَاشِ ٱلْمَبْثُوثِ

**English**: The Day when the people will be like scattered moths.

### Verse 101:5
**Arabic**: وَتَكُونُ ٱلْجِبَالُ كَٱلْعِهْنِ ٱلْمَنفُوشِ

**English**: And the mountains will be like tufted wool.

### Verse 101:6
**Arabic**: فَأَمَّا مَن ثَقُلَتْ مَوَٰزِينُهُۥ

**English**: As for he whose scales are heavy.

### Verse 101:7
**Arabic**: فَهُوَ فِى عِيشَةٍۢ رَّاضِيَةٍۢ

**English**: He will be in a pleasant life.

### Verse 101:8
**Arabic**: وَأَمَّا مَنْ خَفَّتْ مَوَٰزِينُهُۥ

**English**: But as for he whose scales are light.

### Verse 101:9
**Arabic**: فَأُمُّهُۥ هَاوِيَةٌۭ

**English**: His home is the Abyss.

### Verse 101:10
**Arabic**: وَمَآ أَدْرَىٰكَ مَا هِيَهْ

**English**: Do you know what it is?

### Verse 101:11
**Arabic**: نَارٌ حَامِيَةٌۢ

**English**: A Raging Fire.
