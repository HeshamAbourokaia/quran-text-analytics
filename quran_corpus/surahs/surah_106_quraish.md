# Surah 106: Quraish (قريش)

- **Mushaf number**: 106
- **Place of revelation**: Mecca (Makkiyah)
- **Verse count**: 4
- **Themes**: [Patience and Endurance](../themes/patience.md)
- **Named entities mentioned**: [Allah](../entities/divine_allah.md), [Ar-Rahim (the Most Merciful)](../entities/divine_ar-rahim.md), [Ar-Rahman (the Most Gracious)](../entities/divine_ar-rahman.md), [Rabb (the Lord)](../entities/divine_rabb.md)

## Verses

### Verse 106:0
**Arabic**: بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

**English**: In the name of God, the Gracious, the Merciful.

### Verse 106:1
**Arabic**: لِإِيلَٰفِ قُرَيْشٍ

**English**: For the security of Quraish.

### Verse 106:2
**Arabic**: إِۦلَٰفِهِمْ رِحْلَةَ ٱلشِّتَآءِ وَٱلصَّيْفِ

**English**: Their security during winter and summer journeys.

### Verse 106:3
**Arabic**: فَلْيَعْبُدُوا۟ رَبَّ هَٰذَا ٱلْبَيْتِ

**English**: Let them worship the Lord of this House.

### Verse 106:4
**Arabic**: ٱلَّذِىٓ أَطْعَمَهُم مِّن جُوعٍۢ وَءَامَنَهُم مِّنْ خَوْفٍۭ

**English**: Who has fed them against hunger, and has secured them against fear.
