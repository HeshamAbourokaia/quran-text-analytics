# Surah 99: Az-Zalzalah (الزلزلة)

- **Mushaf number**: 99
- **Place of revelation**: Medina (Madaniyah)
- **Verse count**: 8
- **Themes**: [Day of Judgment](../themes/day-of-judgment.md)
- **Named entities mentioned**: [Allah](../entities/divine_allah.md), [Ar-Rahim (the Most Merciful)](../entities/divine_ar-rahim.md), [Ar-Rahman (the Most Gracious)](../entities/divine_ar-rahman.md), [Rabb (the Lord)](../entities/divine_rabb.md)

- **Revelation order (chronological)**: #93 of 114
- **Revelation phase**: [Early Medinan](../timeline/phase_early-medina.md) (622-628 CE, Year 1-6 AH)

## Verses

### Verse 99:0
**Arabic**: بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

**English**: In the name of God, the Gracious, the Merciful.

### Verse 99:1
**Arabic**: إِذَا زُلْزِلَتِ ٱلْأَرْضُ زِلْزَالَهَا

**English**: When the earth is shaken with its quake.

### Verse 99:2
**Arabic**: وَأَخْرَجَتِ ٱلْأَرْضُ أَثْقَالَهَا

**English**: And the earth brings out its loads.

### Verse 99:3
**Arabic**: وَقَالَ ٱلْإِنسَٰنُ مَا لَهَا

**English**: And man says, "What is the matter with it?"

### Verse 99:4
**Arabic**: يَوْمَئِذٍۢ تُحَدِّثُ أَخْبَارَهَا

**English**: On that Day, it will tell its tales.

### Verse 99:5
**Arabic**: بِأَنَّ رَبَّكَ أَوْحَىٰ لَهَا

**English**: For your Lord will have inspired it.

### Verse 99:6
**Arabic**: يَوْمَئِذٍۢ يَصْدُرُ ٱلنَّاسُ أَشْتَاتًۭا لِّيُرَوْا۟ أَعْمَٰلَهُمْ

**English**: On that Day, the people will emerge in droves, to be shown their works.

### Verse 99:7
**Arabic**: فَمَن يَعْمَلْ مِثْقَالَ ذَرَّةٍ خَيْرًۭا يَرَهُۥ

**English**: Whoever has done an atom's weight of good will see it.

### Verse 99:8
**Arabic**: وَمَن يَعْمَلْ مِثْقَالَ ذَرَّةٍۢ شَرًّۭا يَرَهُۥ

**English**: And whoever has done an atom's weight of evil will see it.
