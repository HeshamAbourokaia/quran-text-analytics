# Surah 73: Al-Muzzammil (المزمل)

- **Mushaf number**: 73
- **Place of revelation**: Mecca (Makkiyah)
- **Verse count**: 20
- **Themes**: [Worship and Devotion](../themes/worship.md)
- **Named entities mentioned**: [Allah](../entities/divine_allah.md), [Ar-Rahim (the Most Merciful)](../entities/divine_ar-rahim.md), [Ar-Rahman (the Most Gracious)](../entities/divine_ar-rahman.md), [Pharaoh (Firaun)](../entities/figure_firaun.md), [Rabb (the Lord)](../entities/divine_rabb.md)

- **Revelation order (chronological)**: #3 of 114
- **Revelation phase**: [Early Meccan](../timeline/phase_early-mecca.md) (610-615 CE, Year 1-5 of Prophethood)

## Verses

### Verse 73:0
**Arabic**: بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

**English**: In the name of God, the Gracious, the Merciful.

### Verse 73:1
**Arabic**: يَٰٓأَيُّهَا ٱلْمُزَّمِّلُ

**English**: O you Enwrapped one.

### Verse 73:2
**Arabic**: قُمِ ٱلَّيْلَ إِلَّا قَلِيلًۭا

**English**: Stay up during the night, except a little.

### Verse 73:3
**Arabic**: نِّصْفَهُۥٓ أَوِ ٱنقُصْ مِنْهُ قَلِيلًا

**English**: For half of it, or reduce it a little.

### Verse 73:4
**Arabic**: أَوْ زِدْ عَلَيْهِ وَرَتِّلِ ٱلْقُرْءَانَ تَرْتِيلًا

**English**: Or add to it; and chant the Quran rhythmically.

### Verse 73:5
**Arabic**: إِنَّا سَنُلْقِى عَلَيْكَ قَوْلًۭا ثَقِيلًا

**English**: We are about to give you a heavy message.

### Verse 73:6
**Arabic**: إِنَّ نَاشِئَةَ ٱلَّيْلِ هِىَ أَشَدُّ وَطْـًۭٔا وَأَقْوَمُ قِيلًا

**English**: The vigil of night is more effective, and better suited for recitation.

### Verse 73:7
**Arabic**: إِنَّ لَكَ فِى ٱلنَّهَارِ سَبْحًۭا طَوِيلًۭا

**English**: In the daytime, you have lengthy work to do.

### Verse 73:8
**Arabic**: وَٱذْكُرِ ٱسْمَ رَبِّكَ وَتَبَتَّلْ إِلَيْهِ تَبْتِيلًۭا

**English**: So remember the Name of your Lord, and devote yourself to Him wholeheartedly.

### Verse 73:9
**Arabic**: رَّبُّ ٱلْمَشْرِقِ وَٱلْمَغْرِبِ لَآ إِلَٰهَ إِلَّا هُوَ فَٱتَّخِذْهُ وَكِيلًۭا

**English**: Lord of the East and the West. There is no god but He, so take Him as a Trustee.

### Verse 73:10
**Arabic**: وَٱصْبِرْ عَلَىٰ مَا يَقُولُونَ وَٱهْجُرْهُمْ هَجْرًۭا جَمِيلًۭا

**English**: And endure patiently what they say, and withdraw from them politely.

### Verse 73:11
**Arabic**: وَذَرْنِى وَٱلْمُكَذِّبِينَ أُو۟لِى ٱلنَّعْمَةِ وَمَهِّلْهُمْ قَلِيلًا

**English**: And leave Me to those who deny the truth, those of luxury, and give them a brief respite.

### Verse 73:12
**Arabic**: إِنَّ لَدَيْنَآ أَنكَالًۭا وَجَحِيمًۭا

**English**: With Us are shackles, and a Fierce Fire.

### Verse 73:13
**Arabic**: وَطَعَامًۭا ذَا غُصَّةٍۢ وَعَذَابًا أَلِيمًۭا

**English**: And food that chokes, and a painful punishment.

### Verse 73:14
**Arabic**: يَوْمَ تَرْجُفُ ٱلْأَرْضُ وَٱلْجِبَالُ وَكَانَتِ ٱلْجِبَالُ كَثِيبًۭا مَّهِيلًا

**English**: On the Day when the earth and the mountains tremble, and the mountains become heaps of sand.

### Verse 73:15
**Arabic**: إِنَّآ أَرْسَلْنَآ إِلَيْكُمْ رَسُولًۭا شَٰهِدًا عَلَيْكُمْ كَمَآ أَرْسَلْنَآ إِلَىٰ فِرْعَوْنَ رَسُولًۭا

**English**: We have sent to you a messenger, a witness over you, as We sent to Pharaoh a messenger.

### Verse 73:16
**Arabic**: فَعَصَىٰ فِرْعَوْنُ ٱلرَّسُولَ فَأَخَذْنَٰهُ أَخْذًۭا وَبِيلًۭا

**English**: But Pharaoh defied the Messenger, so We seized him with a terrible seizing.

### Verse 73:17
**Arabic**: فَكَيْفَ تَتَّقُونَ إِن كَفَرْتُمْ يَوْمًۭا يَجْعَلُ ٱلْوِلْدَٰنَ شِيبًا

**English**: So how will you, if you persist in unbelief, save yourself from a Day which will turn the children gray-haired?

### Verse 73:18
**Arabic**: ٱلسَّمَآءُ مُنفَطِرٌۢ بِهِۦ ۚ كَانَ وَعْدُهُۥ مَفْعُولًا

**English**: The heaven will shatter thereby. His promise is always fulfilled.

### Verse 73:19
**Arabic**: إِنَّ هَٰذِهِۦ تَذْكِرَةٌۭ ۖ فَمَن شَآءَ ٱتَّخَذَ إِلَىٰ رَبِّهِۦ سَبِيلًا

**English**: This is a reminder. So whoever wills, let him take a path to his Lord.

### Verse 73:20
**Arabic**: ۞ إِنَّ رَبَّكَ يَعْلَمُ أَنَّكَ تَقُومُ أَدْنَىٰ مِن ثُلُثَىِ ٱلَّيْلِ وَنِصْفَهُۥ وَثُلُثَهُۥ وَطَآئِفَةٌۭ مِّنَ ٱلَّذِينَ مَعَكَ ۚ وَٱللَّهُ يُقَدِّرُ ٱلَّيْلَ وَٱلنَّهَارَ ۚ عَلِمَ أَن لَّن تُحْصُوهُ فَتَابَ عَلَيْكُمْ ۖ فَٱقْرَءُوا۟ مَا تَيَسَّرَ مِنَ ٱلْقُرْءَانِ ۚ عَلِمَ أَن سَيَكُونُ مِنكُم مَّرْضَىٰ ۙ وَءَاخَرُونَ يَضْرِبُونَ فِى ٱلْأَرْضِ يَبْتَغُونَ مِن فَضْلِ ٱللَّهِ ۙ وَءَاخَرُونَ يُقَٰتِلُونَ فِى سَبِيلِ ٱللَّهِ ۖ فَٱقْرَءُوا۟ مَا تَيَسَّرَ مِنْهُ ۚ وَأَقِيمُوا۟ ٱلصَّلَوٰةَ وَءَاتُوا۟ ٱلزَّكَوٰةَ وَأَقْرِضُوا۟ ٱللَّهَ قَرْضًا حَسَنًۭا ۚ وَمَا تُقَدِّمُوا۟ لِأَنفُسِكُم مِّنْ خَيْرٍۢ تَجِدُوهُ عِندَ ٱللَّهِ هُوَ خَيْرًۭا وَأَعْظَمَ أَجْرًۭا ۚ وَٱسْتَغْفِرُوا۟ ٱللَّهَ ۖ إِنَّ ٱللَّهَ غَفُورٌۭ رَّحِيمٌۢ

**English**: Your Lord knows that you stay up nearly two-thirds of the night, or half of it, or one-third of it, along with a group of those with you. God designed the night and the day. He knows that you are unable to sustain it, so He has pardoned you. So read of the Quran what is possible for you. He knows that some of you may be ill; and others travelling through the land, seeking God's bounty; and others fighting in God's cause. So read of it what is possible for you, and observe the prayers, and give regular charity, and lend God a generous loan. Whatever good you advance for yourselves, you will find it with God, better and generously rewarded. And seek God's forgiveness, for God is Forgiving and Merciful.
