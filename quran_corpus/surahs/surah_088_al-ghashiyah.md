# Surah 88: Al-Ghashiyah (الغاشية)

- **Mushaf number**: 88
- **Place of revelation**: Mecca (Makkiyah)
- **Verse count**: 26
- **Themes**: [Day of Judgment](../themes/day-of-judgment.md)
- **Named entities mentioned**: [Allah](../entities/divine_allah.md), [Ar-Rahim (the Most Merciful)](../entities/divine_ar-rahim.md), [Ar-Rahman (the Most Gracious)](../entities/divine_ar-rahman.md)

- **Revelation order (chronological)**: #68 of 114
- **Revelation phase**: [Middle Meccan](../timeline/phase_middle-mecca.md) (615-619 CE, Year 5-9 of Prophethood)

## Verses

### Verse 88:0
**Arabic**: بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

**English**: In the name of God, the Gracious, the Merciful.

### Verse 88:1
**Arabic**: هَلْ أَتَىٰكَ حَدِيثُ ٱلْغَٰشِيَةِ

**English**: Has there come to you the news of the overwhelming?

### Verse 88:2
**Arabic**: وُجُوهٌۭ يَوْمَئِذٍ خَٰشِعَةٌ

**English**: Faces on that Day will be shamed.

### Verse 88:3
**Arabic**: عَامِلَةٌۭ نَّاصِبَةٌۭ

**English**: Laboring and exhausted.

### Verse 88:4
**Arabic**: تَصْلَىٰ نَارًا حَامِيَةًۭ

**English**: Roasting in a scorching Fire.

### Verse 88:5
**Arabic**: تُسْقَىٰ مِنْ عَيْنٍ ءَانِيَةٍۢ

**English**: Given to drink from a flaming spring.

### Verse 88:6
**Arabic**: لَّيْسَ لَهُمْ طَعَامٌ إِلَّا مِن ضَرِيعٍۢ

**English**: They will have no food except thorns.

### Verse 88:7
**Arabic**: لَّا يُسْمِنُ وَلَا يُغْنِى مِن جُوعٍۢ

**English**: That neither nourishes, nor satisfies hunger.

### Verse 88:8
**Arabic**: وُجُوهٌۭ يَوْمَئِذٍۢ نَّاعِمَةٌۭ

**English**: Faces on that Day will be joyful.

### Verse 88:9
**Arabic**: لِّسَعْيِهَا رَاضِيَةٌۭ

**English**: Satisfied with their endeavor.

### Verse 88:10
**Arabic**: فِى جَنَّةٍ عَالِيَةٍۢ

**English**: In a lofty Garden.

### Verse 88:11
**Arabic**: لَّا تَسْمَعُ فِيهَا لَٰغِيَةًۭ

**English**: In it you will hear no nonsense.

### Verse 88:12
**Arabic**: فِيهَا عَيْنٌۭ جَارِيَةٌۭ

**English**: In it is a flowing spring.

### Verse 88:13
**Arabic**: فِيهَا سُرُرٌۭ مَّرْفُوعَةٌۭ

**English**: In it are raised beds.

### Verse 88:14
**Arabic**: وَأَكْوَابٌۭ مَّوْضُوعَةٌۭ

**English**: And cups set in place.

### Verse 88:15
**Arabic**: وَنَمَارِقُ مَصْفُوفَةٌۭ

**English**: And cushions set in rows.

### Verse 88:16
**Arabic**: وَزَرَابِىُّ مَبْثُوثَةٌ

**English**: And carpets spread around.

### Verse 88:17
**Arabic**: أَفَلَا يَنظُرُونَ إِلَى ٱلْإِبِلِ كَيْفَ خُلِقَتْ

**English**: Do they not look at the camels-how they are created?

### Verse 88:18
**Arabic**: وَإِلَى ٱلسَّمَآءِ كَيْفَ رُفِعَتْ

**English**: And at the sky-how it is raised?

### Verse 88:19
**Arabic**: وَإِلَى ٱلْجِبَالِ كَيْفَ نُصِبَتْ

**English**: And at the mountains-how they are installed?

### Verse 88:20
**Arabic**: وَإِلَى ٱلْأَرْضِ كَيْفَ سُطِحَتْ

**English**: And at the earth-how it is spread out?

### Verse 88:21
**Arabic**: فَذَكِّرْ إِنَّمَآ أَنتَ مُذَكِّرٌۭ

**English**: So remind. You are only a reminder.

### Verse 88:22
**Arabic**: لَّسْتَ عَلَيْهِم بِمُصَيْطِرٍ

**English**: You have no control over them.

### Verse 88:23
**Arabic**: إِلَّا مَن تَوَلَّىٰ وَكَفَرَ

**English**: But whoever turns away and disbelieves.

### Verse 88:24
**Arabic**: فَيُعَذِّبُهُ ٱللَّهُ ٱلْعَذَابَ ٱلْأَكْبَرَ

**English**: God will punish him with the greatest punishment.

### Verse 88:25
**Arabic**: إِنَّ إِلَيْنَآ إِيَابَهُمْ

**English**: To Us is their return.

### Verse 88:26
**Arabic**: ثُمَّ إِنَّ عَلَيْنَا حِسَابَهُم

**English**: Then upon Us rests their reckoning.
