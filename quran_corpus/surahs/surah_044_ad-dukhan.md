# Surah 44: Ad-Dukhan (الدخان)

- **Mushaf number**: 44
- **Place of revelation**: Mecca (Makkiyah)
- **Verse count**: 59
- **Themes**: [Day of Judgment](../themes/day-of-judgment.md)
- **Named entities mentioned**: [Allah](../entities/divine_allah.md), [Ar-Rahim (the Most Merciful)](../entities/divine_ar-rahim.md), [Ar-Rahman (the Most Gracious)](../entities/divine_ar-rahman.md), [Pharaoh (Firaun)](../entities/figure_firaun.md), [Rabb (the Lord)](../entities/divine_rabb.md)

## Verses

### Verse 44:0
**Arabic**: بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

**English**: In the name of God, the Gracious, the Merciful.

### Verse 44:1
**Arabic**: حمٓ

**English**: Ha, Meem.

### Verse 44:2
**Arabic**: وَٱلْكِتَٰبِ ٱلْمُبِينِ

**English**: By the Enlightening Scripture.

### Verse 44:3
**Arabic**: إِنَّآ أَنزَلْنَٰهُ فِى لَيْلَةٍۢ مُّبَٰرَكَةٍ ۚ إِنَّا كُنَّا مُنذِرِينَ

**English**: We have revealed it on a Blessed Night-We have warned.

### Verse 44:4
**Arabic**: فِيهَا يُفْرَقُ كُلُّ أَمْرٍ حَكِيمٍ

**English**: In it is distinguished every wise command.

### Verse 44:5
**Arabic**: أَمْرًۭا مِّنْ عِندِنَآ ۚ إِنَّا كُنَّا مُرْسِلِينَ

**English**: A decree from Us. We have been sending messages.

### Verse 44:6
**Arabic**: رَحْمَةًۭ مِّن رَّبِّكَ ۚ إِنَّهُۥ هُوَ ٱلسَّمِيعُ ٱلْعَلِيمُ

**English**: As mercy from your Lord. He is the Hearer, the Knower.

### Verse 44:7
**Arabic**: رَبِّ ٱلسَّمَٰوَٰتِ وَٱلْأَرْضِ وَمَا بَيْنَهُمَآ ۖ إِن كُنتُم مُّوقِنِينَ

**English**: Lord of the heavens and the earth and what is between them, if you know for sure.

### Verse 44:8
**Arabic**: لَآ إِلَٰهَ إِلَّا هُوَ يُحْىِۦ وَيُمِيتُ ۖ رَبُّكُمْ وَرَبُّ ءَابَآئِكُمُ ٱلْأَوَّلِينَ

**English**: There is no god but He. He gives life and causes death-your Lord and Lord of your ancestors of old.

### Verse 44:9
**Arabic**: بَلْ هُمْ فِى شَكٍّۢ يَلْعَبُونَ

**English**: Yet they play around in doubt.

### Verse 44:10
**Arabic**: فَٱرْتَقِبْ يَوْمَ تَأْتِى ٱلسَّمَآءُ بِدُخَانٍۢ مُّبِينٍۢ

**English**: So watch out for the Day when the sky produces a visible smoke.

### Verse 44:11
**Arabic**: يَغْشَى ٱلنَّاسَ ۖ هَٰذَا عَذَابٌ أَلِيمٌۭ

**English**: Enveloping mankind; this is a painful punishment.

### Verse 44:12
**Arabic**: رَّبَّنَا ٱكْشِفْ عَنَّا ٱلْعَذَابَ إِنَّا مُؤْمِنُونَ

**English**: "Our Lord, lift the torment from us, we are believers."

### Verse 44:13
**Arabic**: أَنَّىٰ لَهُمُ ٱلذِّكْرَىٰ وَقَدْ جَآءَهُمْ رَسُولٌۭ مُّبِينٌۭ

**English**: But how can they be reminded? An enlightening messenger has already come to them.

### Verse 44:14
**Arabic**: ثُمَّ تَوَلَّوْا۟ عَنْهُ وَقَالُوا۟ مُعَلَّمٌۭ مَّجْنُونٌ

**English**: But they turned away from him, and said, "Educated, but crazy!"

### Verse 44:15
**Arabic**: إِنَّا كَاشِفُوا۟ ٱلْعَذَابِ قَلِيلًا ۚ إِنَّكُمْ عَآئِدُونَ

**English**: We will ease the punishment a little, but you will revert.

### Verse 44:16
**Arabic**: يَوْمَ نَبْطِشُ ٱلْبَطْشَةَ ٱلْكُبْرَىٰٓ إِنَّا مُنتَقِمُونَ

**English**: The Day when We launch the Great Assault-We will avenge.

### Verse 44:17
**Arabic**: ۞ وَلَقَدْ فَتَنَّا قَبْلَهُمْ قَوْمَ فِرْعَوْنَ وَجَآءَهُمْ رَسُولٌۭ كَرِيمٌ

**English**: Before them We tested the people of Pharaoh; a noble messenger came to them.

### Verse 44:18
**Arabic**: أَنْ أَدُّوٓا۟ إِلَىَّ عِبَادَ ٱللَّهِ ۖ إِنِّى لَكُمْ رَسُولٌ أَمِينٌۭ

**English**: Saying, "Hand over God's servants to me. I am an honest messenger to you."

### Verse 44:19
**Arabic**: وَأَن لَّا تَعْلُوا۟ عَلَى ٱللَّهِ ۖ إِنِّىٓ ءَاتِيكُم بِسُلْطَٰنٍۢ مُّبِينٍۢ

**English**: And, "Do not exalt yourselves above God. I come to you with clear authority.

### Verse 44:20
**Arabic**: وَإِنِّى عُذْتُ بِرَبِّى وَرَبِّكُمْ أَن تَرْجُمُونِ

**English**: I have taken refuge in my Lord and your Lord, lest you stone me.

### Verse 44:21
**Arabic**: وَإِن لَّمْ تُؤْمِنُوا۟ لِى فَٱعْتَزِلُونِ

**English**: But if you do not believe in me, keep away from me."

### Verse 44:22
**Arabic**: فَدَعَا رَبَّهُۥٓ أَنَّ هَٰٓؤُلَآءِ قَوْمٌۭ مُّجْرِمُونَ

**English**: He appealed to his Lord: "These are a sinful people."

### Verse 44:23
**Arabic**: فَأَسْرِ بِعِبَادِى لَيْلًا إِنَّكُم مُّتَّبَعُونَ

**English**: "Set out with My servants by night-you will be followed.

### Verse 44:24
**Arabic**: وَٱتْرُكِ ٱلْبَحْرَ رَهْوًا ۖ إِنَّهُمْ جُندٌۭ مُّغْرَقُونَ

**English**: And cross the sea quickly; they are an army to be drowned."

### Verse 44:25
**Arabic**: كَمْ تَرَكُوا۟ مِن جَنَّٰتٍۢ وَعُيُونٍۢ

**English**: How many gardens and fountains did they leave behind?

### Verse 44:26
**Arabic**: وَزُرُوعٍۢ وَمَقَامٍۢ كَرِيمٍۢ

**English**: And plantations, and splendid buildings.

### Verse 44:27
**Arabic**: وَنَعْمَةٍۢ كَانُوا۟ فِيهَا فَٰكِهِينَ

**English**: And comforts they used to enjoy.

### Verse 44:28
**Arabic**: كَذَٰلِكَ ۖ وَأَوْرَثْنَٰهَا قَوْمًا ءَاخَرِينَ

**English**: So it was; and We passed it on to another people.

### Verse 44:29
**Arabic**: فَمَا بَكَتْ عَلَيْهِمُ ٱلسَّمَآءُ وَٱلْأَرْضُ وَمَا كَانُوا۟ مُنظَرِينَ

**English**: Neither heaven nor earth wept over them, nor were they reprieved.

### Verse 44:30
**Arabic**: وَلَقَدْ نَجَّيْنَا بَنِىٓ إِسْرَٰٓءِيلَ مِنَ ٱلْعَذَابِ ٱلْمُهِينِ

**English**: And We delivered the Children of Israel from the humiliating persecution.

### Verse 44:31
**Arabic**: مِن فِرْعَوْنَ ۚ إِنَّهُۥ كَانَ عَالِيًۭا مِّنَ ٱلْمُسْرِفِينَ

**English**: From Pharaoh. He was a transgressing tyrant.

### Verse 44:32
**Arabic**: وَلَقَدِ ٱخْتَرْنَٰهُمْ عَلَىٰ عِلْمٍ عَلَى ٱلْعَٰلَمِينَ

**English**: And We chose them knowingly over all other people.

### Verse 44:33
**Arabic**: وَءَاتَيْنَٰهُم مِّنَ ٱلْءَايَٰتِ مَا فِيهِ بَلَٰٓؤٌۭا۟ مُّبِينٌ

**English**: And We gave them many signs, in which was an obvious test.

### Verse 44:34
**Arabic**: إِنَّ هَٰٓؤُلَآءِ لَيَقُولُونَ

**English**: These people say.

### Verse 44:35
**Arabic**: إِنْ هِىَ إِلَّا مَوْتَتُنَا ٱلْأُولَىٰ وَمَا نَحْنُ بِمُنشَرِينَ

**English**: "There is nothing but our first death, and we will not be resurrected.

### Verse 44:36
**Arabic**: فَأْتُوا۟ بِـَٔابَآئِنَآ إِن كُنتُمْ صَٰدِقِينَ

**English**: Bring back our ancestors, if you are truthful."

### Verse 44:37
**Arabic**: أَهُمْ خَيْرٌ أَمْ قَوْمُ تُبَّعٍۢ وَٱلَّذِينَ مِن قَبْلِهِمْ ۚ أَهْلَكْنَٰهُمْ ۖ إِنَّهُمْ كَانُوا۟ مُجْرِمِينَ

**English**: Are they better, or the people of Tubba and those before them? We annihilated them. They were evildoers.

### Verse 44:38
**Arabic**: وَمَا خَلَقْنَا ٱلسَّمَٰوَٰتِ وَٱلْأَرْضَ وَمَا بَيْنَهُمَا لَٰعِبِينَ

**English**: We did not create the heavens and the earth and what is between them to play.

### Verse 44:39
**Arabic**: مَا خَلَقْنَٰهُمَآ إِلَّا بِٱلْحَقِّ وَلَٰكِنَّ أَكْثَرَهُمْ لَا يَعْلَمُونَ

**English**: We created them only for a specific purpose, but most of them do not know.

### Verse 44:40
**Arabic**: إِنَّ يَوْمَ ٱلْفَصْلِ مِيقَٰتُهُمْ أَجْمَعِينَ

**English**: The Day of Sorting Out is the appointed time for them all.

### Verse 44:41
**Arabic**: يَوْمَ لَا يُغْنِى مَوْلًى عَن مَّوْلًۭى شَيْـًۭٔا وَلَا هُمْ يُنصَرُونَ

**English**: The Day when no friend will avail a friend in any way, and they will not be helped.

### Verse 44:42
**Arabic**: إِلَّا مَن رَّحِمَ ٱللَّهُ ۚ إِنَّهُۥ هُوَ ٱلْعَزِيزُ ٱلرَّحِيمُ

**English**: Except for him upon whom God has mercy. He is the Mighty, the Merciful.

### Verse 44:43
**Arabic**: إِنَّ شَجَرَتَ ٱلزَّقُّومِ

**English**: The Tree of Bitterness.

### Verse 44:44
**Arabic**: طَعَامُ ٱلْأَثِيمِ

**English**: The food of the sinner.

### Verse 44:45
**Arabic**: كَٱلْمُهْلِ يَغْلِى فِى ٱلْبُطُونِ

**English**: Like molten lead; boiling inside the bellies.

### Verse 44:46
**Arabic**: كَغَلْىِ ٱلْحَمِيمِ

**English**: Like the boiling of seething water.

### Verse 44:47
**Arabic**: خُذُوهُ فَٱعْتِلُوهُ إِلَىٰ سَوَآءِ ٱلْجَحِيمِ

**English**: Seize him and drag him into the midst of Hell!

### Verse 44:48
**Arabic**: ثُمَّ صُبُّوا۟ فَوْقَ رَأْسِهِۦ مِنْ عَذَابِ ٱلْحَمِيمِ

**English**: Then pour over his head the suffering of the Inferno!

### Verse 44:49
**Arabic**: ذُقْ إِنَّكَ أَنتَ ٱلْعَزِيزُ ٱلْكَرِيمُ

**English**: Taste! You who were powerful and noble.

### Verse 44:50
**Arabic**: إِنَّ هَٰذَا مَا كُنتُم بِهِۦ تَمْتَرُونَ

**English**: This is what you used to doubt.

### Verse 44:51
**Arabic**: إِنَّ ٱلْمُتَّقِينَ فِى مَقَامٍ أَمِينٍۢ

**English**: As for the righteous, they will be in a secure place.

### Verse 44:52
**Arabic**: فِى جَنَّٰتٍۢ وَعُيُونٍۢ

**English**: Amidst gardens and springs.

### Verse 44:53
**Arabic**: يَلْبَسُونَ مِن سُندُسٍۢ وَإِسْتَبْرَقٍۢ مُّتَقَٰبِلِينَ

**English**: Dressed in silk and brocade, facing one another.

### Verse 44:54
**Arabic**: كَذَٰلِكَ وَزَوَّجْنَٰهُم بِحُورٍ عِينٍۢ

**English**: So it is, and We will wed them to lovely companions.

### Verse 44:55
**Arabic**: يَدْعُونَ فِيهَا بِكُلِّ فَٰكِهَةٍ ءَامِنِينَ

**English**: They will call therein for every kind of fruit, in peace and security.

### Verse 44:56
**Arabic**: لَا يَذُوقُونَ فِيهَا ٱلْمَوْتَ إِلَّا ٱلْمَوْتَةَ ٱلْأُولَىٰ ۖ وَوَقَىٰهُمْ عَذَابَ ٱلْجَحِيمِ

**English**: Therein they will not taste death, beyond the first death; and He will protect them from the torment of Hell.

### Verse 44:57
**Arabic**: فَضْلًۭا مِّن رَّبِّكَ ۚ ذَٰلِكَ هُوَ ٱلْفَوْزُ ٱلْعَظِيمُ

**English**: A favor from your Lord. That is the supreme salvation.

### Verse 44:58
**Arabic**: فَإِنَّمَا يَسَّرْنَٰهُ بِلِسَانِكَ لَعَلَّهُمْ يَتَذَكَّرُونَ

**English**: We made it easy in your language, so that they may remember.

### Verse 44:59
**Arabic**: فَٱرْتَقِبْ إِنَّهُم مُّرْتَقِبُونَ

**English**: So wait and watch. They too are waiting and watching.
