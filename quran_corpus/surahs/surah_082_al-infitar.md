# Surah 82: Al-Infitar (الإنفطار)

- **Mushaf number**: 82
- **Place of revelation**: Mecca (Makkiyah)
- **Verse count**: 19
- **Themes**: [Day of Judgment](../themes/day-of-judgment.md)
- **Named entities mentioned**: [Allah](../entities/divine_allah.md), [Ar-Rahim (the Most Merciful)](../entities/divine_ar-rahim.md), [Ar-Rahman (the Most Gracious)](../entities/divine_ar-rahman.md), [Rabb (the Lord)](../entities/divine_rabb.md)

- **Revelation order (chronological)**: #82 of 114
- **Revelation phase**: [Late Meccan](../timeline/phase_late-mecca.md) (619-622 CE, Year 9-13 of Prophethood)

## Verses

### Verse 82:0
**Arabic**: بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

**English**: In the name of God, the Gracious, the Merciful.

### Verse 82:1
**Arabic**: إِذَا ٱلسَّمَآءُ ٱنفَطَرَتْ

**English**: When the sky breaks apart.

### Verse 82:2
**Arabic**: وَإِذَا ٱلْكَوَاكِبُ ٱنتَثَرَتْ

**English**: When the planets are scattered.

### Verse 82:3
**Arabic**: وَإِذَا ٱلْبِحَارُ فُجِّرَتْ

**English**: When the oceans are exploded.

### Verse 82:4
**Arabic**: وَإِذَا ٱلْقُبُورُ بُعْثِرَتْ

**English**: When the tombs are strewn around.

### Verse 82:5
**Arabic**: عَلِمَتْ نَفْسٌۭ مَّا قَدَّمَتْ وَأَخَّرَتْ

**English**: Each soul will know what it has advanced, and what it has deferred.

### Verse 82:6
**Arabic**: يَٰٓأَيُّهَا ٱلْإِنسَٰنُ مَا غَرَّكَ بِرَبِّكَ ٱلْكَرِيمِ

**English**: O man! What deluded you concerning your Lord, the Most Generous?

### Verse 82:7
**Arabic**: ٱلَّذِى خَلَقَكَ فَسَوَّىٰكَ فَعَدَلَكَ

**English**: He Who created you, and formed you, and proportioned you?

### Verse 82:8
**Arabic**: فِىٓ أَىِّ صُورَةٍۢ مَّا شَآءَ رَكَّبَكَ

**English**: In whatever shape He willed, He assembled you.

### Verse 82:9
**Arabic**: كَلَّا بَلْ تُكَذِّبُونَ بِٱلدِّينِ

**English**: But you reject the religion.

### Verse 82:10
**Arabic**: وَإِنَّ عَلَيْكُمْ لَحَٰفِظِينَ

**English**: Though over you are watchers.

### Verse 82:11
**Arabic**: كِرَامًۭا كَٰتِبِينَ

**English**: Honest recorders.

### Verse 82:12
**Arabic**: يَعْلَمُونَ مَا تَفْعَلُونَ

**English**: They know everything you do.

### Verse 82:13
**Arabic**: إِنَّ ٱلْأَبْرَارَ لَفِى نَعِيمٍۢ

**English**: The virtuous will be in bliss.

### Verse 82:14
**Arabic**: وَإِنَّ ٱلْفُجَّارَ لَفِى جَحِيمٍۢ

**English**: While the wicked will be in Hell.

### Verse 82:15
**Arabic**: يَصْلَوْنَهَا يَوْمَ ٱلدِّينِ

**English**: They will enter it on the Day of Justice.

### Verse 82:16
**Arabic**: وَمَا هُمْ عَنْهَا بِغَآئِبِينَ

**English**: And they will not be absent from it.

### Verse 82:17
**Arabic**: وَمَآ أَدْرَىٰكَ مَا يَوْمُ ٱلدِّينِ

**English**: But what will convey to you what the Day of Justice is?

### Verse 82:18
**Arabic**: ثُمَّ مَآ أَدْرَىٰكَ مَا يَوْمُ ٱلدِّينِ

**English**: Then again, what will convey to you what the Day of Justice is?

### Verse 82:19
**Arabic**: يَوْمَ لَا تَمْلِكُ نَفْسٌۭ لِّنَفْسٍۢ شَيْـًۭٔا ۖ وَٱلْأَمْرُ يَوْمَئِذٍۢ لِّلَّهِ

**English**: The Day when no soul will avail another soul anything; and the decision on that Day is God's.
