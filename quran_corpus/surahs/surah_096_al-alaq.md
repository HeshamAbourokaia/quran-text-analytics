# Surah 96: Al-Alaq (العلق)

- **Mushaf number**: 96
- **Place of revelation**: Mecca (Makkiyah)
- **Verse count**: 19
- **Themes**: [Worship and Devotion](../themes/worship.md)
- **Named entities mentioned**: [Allah](../entities/divine_allah.md), [Ar-Rahim (the Most Merciful)](../entities/divine_ar-rahim.md), [Ar-Rahman (the Most Gracious)](../entities/divine_ar-rahman.md), [Rabb (the Lord)](../entities/divine_rabb.md)

## Verses

### Verse 96:0
**Arabic**: بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

**English**: In the name of God, the Gracious, the Merciful.

### Verse 96:1
**Arabic**: ٱقْرَأْ بِٱسْمِ رَبِّكَ ٱلَّذِى خَلَقَ

**English**: Read: In the Name of your Lord who created.

### Verse 96:2
**Arabic**: خَلَقَ ٱلْإِنسَٰنَ مِنْ عَلَقٍ

**English**: Created man from a clot.

### Verse 96:3
**Arabic**: ٱقْرَأْ وَرَبُّكَ ٱلْأَكْرَمُ

**English**: Read: And your Lord is the Most Generous.

### Verse 96:4
**Arabic**: ٱلَّذِى عَلَّمَ بِٱلْقَلَمِ

**English**: He who taught by the pen.

### Verse 96:5
**Arabic**: عَلَّمَ ٱلْإِنسَٰنَ مَا لَمْ يَعْلَمْ

**English**: Taught man what he never knew.

### Verse 96:6
**Arabic**: كَلَّآ إِنَّ ٱلْإِنسَٰنَ لَيَطْغَىٰٓ

**English**: In fact, man oversteps all bounds.

### Verse 96:7
**Arabic**: أَن رَّءَاهُ ٱسْتَغْنَىٰٓ

**English**: When he considers himself exempt.

### Verse 96:8
**Arabic**: إِنَّ إِلَىٰ رَبِّكَ ٱلرُّجْعَىٰٓ

**English**: But to your Lord is the return.

### Verse 96:9
**Arabic**: أَرَءَيْتَ ٱلَّذِى يَنْهَىٰ

**English**: Have you seen him who prevents?

### Verse 96:10
**Arabic**: عَبْدًا إِذَا صَلَّىٰٓ

**English**: A servant when he prays?

### Verse 96:11
**Arabic**: أَرَءَيْتَ إِن كَانَ عَلَى ٱلْهُدَىٰٓ

**English**: Do you think he is upon guidance?

### Verse 96:12
**Arabic**: أَوْ أَمَرَ بِٱلتَّقْوَىٰٓ

**English**: Or advocates righteousness?

### Verse 96:13
**Arabic**: أَرَءَيْتَ إِن كَذَّبَ وَتَوَلَّىٰٓ

**English**: Do you see how he disbelieved and turned away?

### Verse 96:14
**Arabic**: أَلَمْ يَعْلَم بِأَنَّ ٱللَّهَ يَرَىٰ

**English**: Does he not know that God sees?

### Verse 96:15
**Arabic**: كَلَّا لَئِن لَّمْ يَنتَهِ لَنَسْفَعًۢا بِٱلنَّاصِيَةِ

**English**: No. If he does not desist, We will drag him by the forelock.

### Verse 96:16
**Arabic**: نَاصِيَةٍۢ كَٰذِبَةٍ خَاطِئَةٍۢ

**English**: A deceitful, sinful forelock.

### Verse 96:17
**Arabic**: فَلْيَدْعُ نَادِيَهُۥ

**English**: Let him call on his gang.

### Verse 96:18
**Arabic**: سَنَدْعُ ٱلزَّبَانِيَةَ

**English**: We will call the Guards.

### Verse 96:19
**Arabic**: كَلَّا لَا تُطِعْهُ وَٱسْجُدْ وَٱقْتَرِب ۩

**English**: No, do not obey him; but kneel down, and come near.
