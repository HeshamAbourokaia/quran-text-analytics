# Surah 71: Nuh (نوح)

- **Mushaf number**: 71
- **Place of revelation**: Mecca (Makkiyah)
- **Verse count**: 28
- **Themes**: [Stories of the Prophets](../themes/prophets-stories.md)
- **Named entities mentioned**: [Allah](../entities/divine_allah.md), [Ar-Rahim (the Most Merciful)](../entities/divine_ar-rahim.md), [Ar-Rahman (the Most Gracious)](../entities/divine_ar-rahman.md), [Noah (Nuh)](../entities/prophet_nuh.md), [Rabb (the Lord)](../entities/divine_rabb.md)

## Verses

### Verse 71:0
**Arabic**: بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

**English**: In the name of God, the Gracious, the Merciful.

### Verse 71:1
**Arabic**: إِنَّآ أَرْسَلْنَا نُوحًا إِلَىٰ قَوْمِهِۦٓ أَنْ أَنذِرْ قَوْمَكَ مِن قَبْلِ أَن يَأْتِيَهُمْ عَذَابٌ أَلِيمٌۭ

**English**: We sent Noah to his people: "Warn your people before there comes upon them a painful punishment."

### Verse 71:2
**Arabic**: قَالَ يَٰقَوْمِ إِنِّى لَكُمْ نَذِيرٌۭ مُّبِينٌ

**English**: He said, "O my people, I am to you a clear warner.

### Verse 71:3
**Arabic**: أَنِ ٱعْبُدُوا۟ ٱللَّهَ وَٱتَّقُوهُ وَأَطِيعُونِ

**English**: Worship God and reverence Him, and obey me.

### Verse 71:4
**Arabic**: يَغْفِرْ لَكُم مِّن ذُنُوبِكُمْ وَيُؤَخِّرْكُمْ إِلَىٰٓ أَجَلٍۢ مُّسَمًّى ۚ إِنَّ أَجَلَ ٱللَّهِ إِذَا جَآءَ لَا يُؤَخَّرُ ۖ لَوْ كُنتُمْ تَعْلَمُونَ

**English**: And He will forgive you of your sins, and reprieve you until a stated term. God's term cannot be deferred once it has arrived, if you only knew."

### Verse 71:5
**Arabic**: قَالَ رَبِّ إِنِّى دَعَوْتُ قَوْمِى لَيْلًۭا وَنَهَارًۭا

**English**: He said, "My Lord, I have called my people night and day.

### Verse 71:6
**Arabic**: فَلَمْ يَزِدْهُمْ دُعَآءِىٓ إِلَّا فِرَارًۭا

**English**: But my call added only to their flight.

### Verse 71:7
**Arabic**: وَإِنِّى كُلَّمَا دَعَوْتُهُمْ لِتَغْفِرَ لَهُمْ جَعَلُوٓا۟ أَصَٰبِعَهُمْ فِىٓ ءَاذَانِهِمْ وَٱسْتَغْشَوْا۟ ثِيَابَهُمْ وَأَصَرُّوا۟ وَٱسْتَكْبَرُوا۟ ٱسْتِكْبَارًۭا

**English**: Whenever I called them to Your forgiveness, they thrust their fingers into their ears, and wrapped themselves in their garments, and insisted, and became more and more arrogant.

### Verse 71:8
**Arabic**: ثُمَّ إِنِّى دَعَوْتُهُمْ جِهَارًۭا

**English**: Then I called them openly.

### Verse 71:9
**Arabic**: ثُمَّ إِنِّىٓ أَعْلَنتُ لَهُمْ وَأَسْرَرْتُ لَهُمْ إِسْرَارًۭا

**English**: Then I appealed to them publicly, and I spoke to them privately.

### Verse 71:10
**Arabic**: فَقُلْتُ ٱسْتَغْفِرُوا۟ رَبَّكُمْ إِنَّهُۥ كَانَ غَفَّارًۭا

**English**: I said, 'Ask your Lord for forgiveness; He is Forgiving.

### Verse 71:11
**Arabic**: يُرْسِلِ ٱلسَّمَآءَ عَلَيْكُم مِّدْرَارًۭا

**English**: He will let loose the sky upon you in torrents.

### Verse 71:12
**Arabic**: وَيُمْدِدْكُم بِأَمْوَٰلٍۢ وَبَنِينَ وَيَجْعَل لَّكُمْ جَنَّٰتٍۢ وَيَجْعَل لَّكُمْ أَنْهَٰرًۭا

**English**: And provide you with wealth and children, and allot for you gardens, and allot for you rivers.

### Verse 71:13
**Arabic**: مَّا لَكُمْ لَا تَرْجُونَ لِلَّهِ وَقَارًۭا

**English**: What is the matter with you, that you do not appreciate God's Greatness?

### Verse 71:14
**Arabic**: وَقَدْ خَلَقَكُمْ أَطْوَارًا

**English**: Although He created you in stages.

### Verse 71:15
**Arabic**: أَلَمْ تَرَوْا۟ كَيْفَ خَلَقَ ٱللَّهُ سَبْعَ سَمَٰوَٰتٍۢ طِبَاقًۭا

**English**: Do you not realize that God created seven heavens in layers?

### Verse 71:16
**Arabic**: وَجَعَلَ ٱلْقَمَرَ فِيهِنَّ نُورًۭا وَجَعَلَ ٱلشَّمْسَ سِرَاجًۭا

**English**: And He set the moon in their midst for light, and He made the sun a lamp.

### Verse 71:17
**Arabic**: وَٱللَّهُ أَنۢبَتَكُم مِّنَ ٱلْأَرْضِ نَبَاتًۭا

**English**: And God germinated you from the earth like plants.

### Verse 71:18
**Arabic**: ثُمَّ يُعِيدُكُمْ فِيهَا وَيُخْرِجُكُمْ إِخْرَاجًۭا

**English**: Then He will return you into it, and will bring you out again.

### Verse 71:19
**Arabic**: وَٱللَّهُ جَعَلَ لَكُمُ ٱلْأَرْضَ بِسَاطًۭا

**English**: And God made the earth a spread for you.

### Verse 71:20
**Arabic**: لِّتَسْلُكُوا۟ مِنْهَا سُبُلًۭا فِجَاجًۭا

**English**: That you may travel its diverse roads.'"

### Verse 71:21
**Arabic**: قَالَ نُوحٌۭ رَّبِّ إِنَّهُمْ عَصَوْنِى وَٱتَّبَعُوا۟ مَن لَّمْ يَزِدْهُ مَالُهُۥ وَوَلَدُهُۥٓ إِلَّا خَسَارًۭا

**English**: Noah said, "My Lord, they have defied me, and followed him whose wealth and children increase him only in perdition."

### Verse 71:22
**Arabic**: وَمَكَرُوا۟ مَكْرًۭا كُبَّارًۭا

**English**: And they schemed outrageous schemes.

### Verse 71:23
**Arabic**: وَقَالُوا۟ لَا تَذَرُنَّ ءَالِهَتَكُمْ وَلَا تَذَرُنَّ وَدًّۭا وَلَا سُوَاعًۭا وَلَا يَغُوثَ وَيَعُوقَ وَنَسْرًۭا

**English**: And they said, "Do not give up your gods; do not give up Wadd, nor Souwa, nor Yaghoos, and Yaooq, and Nassr.

### Verse 71:24
**Arabic**: وَقَدْ أَضَلُّوا۟ كَثِيرًۭا ۖ وَلَا تَزِدِ ٱلظَّٰلِمِينَ إِلَّا ضَلَٰلًۭا

**English**: They have misled many, so do not increase the wrongdoers except in confusion."

### Verse 71:25
**Arabic**: مِّمَّا خَطِيٓـَٰٔتِهِمْ أُغْرِقُوا۟ فَأُدْخِلُوا۟ نَارًۭا فَلَمْ يَجِدُوا۟ لَهُم مِّن دُونِ ٱللَّهِ أَنصَارًۭا

**English**: Because of their wrongs, they were drowned, and were hurled into a Fire. They did not find apart from God any helpers.

### Verse 71:26
**Arabic**: وَقَالَ نُوحٌۭ رَّبِّ لَا تَذَرْ عَلَى ٱلْأَرْضِ مِنَ ٱلْكَٰفِرِينَ دَيَّارًا

**English**: Noah said, "My Lord, do not leave of the unbelievers a single dweller on earth.

### Verse 71:27
**Arabic**: إِنَّكَ إِن تَذَرْهُمْ يُضِلُّوا۟ عِبَادَكَ وَلَا يَلِدُوٓا۟ إِلَّا فَاجِرًۭا كَفَّارًۭا

**English**: If You leave them, they will mislead your servants, and will breed only wicked unbelievers.

### Verse 71:28
**Arabic**: رَّبِّ ٱغْفِرْ لِى وَلِوَٰلِدَىَّ وَلِمَن دَخَلَ بَيْتِىَ مُؤْمِنًۭا وَلِلْمُؤْمِنِينَ وَٱلْمُؤْمِنَٰتِ وَلَا تَزِدِ ٱلظَّٰلِمِينَ إِلَّا تَبَارًۢا

**English**: My Lord! Forgive me and my parents, and anyone who enters my home in faith, and all the believing men and believing women; and do not increase the wrongdoers except in perdition."
