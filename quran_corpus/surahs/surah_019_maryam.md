# Surah 19: Maryam (مريم)

- **Mushaf number**: 19
- **Place of revelation**: Mecca (Makkiyah)
- **Verse count**: 98
- **Themes**: [Stories of the Prophets](../themes/prophets-stories.md)
- **Named entities mentioned**: [Adam](../entities/prophet_adam.md), [Allah](../entities/divine_allah.md), [Ar-Rahim (the Most Merciful)](../entities/divine_ar-rahim.md), [Ar-Rahman (the Most Gracious)](../entities/divine_ar-rahman.md), [Iblis (Satan)](../entities/figure_iblis.md), [Abraham (Ibrahim)](../entities/prophet_ibrahim.md), [Idris (Enoch)](../entities/prophet_idris.md), [Jesus (Isa)](../entities/prophet_isa.md), [Isaac (Ishaq)](../entities/prophet_ishaq.md), [Ishmael (Ismail)](../entities/prophet_ismail.md), [Mary (Maryam, mother of Jesus)](../entities/figure_maryam.md), [Moses (Musa)](../entities/prophet_musa.md), [Noah (Nuh)](../entities/prophet_nuh.md), [Rabb (the Lord)](../entities/divine_rabb.md), [Mount Sinai (Tur)](../entities/place_sinai.md), [John the Baptist (Yahya)](../entities/prophet_yahya.md), [Jacob (Yaqub)](../entities/prophet_yaqub.md), [Zechariah (Zakariya)](../entities/prophet_zakariya.md)

## Verses

### Verse 19:0
**Arabic**: بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

**English**: In the name of God, the Gracious, the Merciful.

### Verse 19:1
**Arabic**: كٓهيعٓصٓ

**English**: Kaf, Ha, Ya, Ayn, Saad.

### Verse 19:2
**Arabic**: ذِكْرُ رَحْمَتِ رَبِّكَ عَبْدَهُۥ زَكَرِيَّآ

**English**: A mention of the mercy of your Lord towards His servant Zechariah.

### Verse 19:3
**Arabic**: إِذْ نَادَىٰ رَبَّهُۥ نِدَآءً خَفِيًّۭا

**English**: When he called on his Lord, a call in seclusion.

### Verse 19:4
**Arabic**: قَالَ رَبِّ إِنِّى وَهَنَ ٱلْعَظْمُ مِنِّى وَٱشْتَعَلَ ٱلرَّأْسُ شَيْبًۭا وَلَمْ أَكُنۢ بِدُعَآئِكَ رَبِّ شَقِيًّۭا

**English**: He said, "My Lord, my bones have become feeble, and my hair is aflame with gray, and never, Lord, have I been disappointed in my prayer to you.

### Verse 19:5
**Arabic**: وَإِنِّى خِفْتُ ٱلْمَوَٰلِىَ مِن وَرَآءِى وَكَانَتِ ٱمْرَأَتِى عَاقِرًۭا فَهَبْ لِى مِن لَّدُنكَ وَلِيًّۭا

**English**: "And I fear for my dependents after me, and my wife is barren. So grant me, from Yourself, an heir.

### Verse 19:6
**Arabic**: يَرِثُنِى وَيَرِثُ مِنْ ءَالِ يَعْقُوبَ ۖ وَٱجْعَلْهُ رَبِّ رَضِيًّۭا

**English**: To inherit me, and inherit from the House of Jacob, and make him, my Lord, pleasing."

### Verse 19:7
**Arabic**: يَٰزَكَرِيَّآ إِنَّا نُبَشِّرُكَ بِغُلَٰمٍ ٱسْمُهُۥ يَحْيَىٰ لَمْ نَجْعَل لَّهُۥ مِن قَبْلُ سَمِيًّۭا

**English**: "O Zechariah, We give you good news of a son, whose name is John, a name We have never given before."

### Verse 19:8
**Arabic**: قَالَ رَبِّ أَنَّىٰ يَكُونُ لِى غُلَٰمٌۭ وَكَانَتِ ٱمْرَأَتِى عَاقِرًۭا وَقَدْ بَلَغْتُ مِنَ ٱلْكِبَرِ عِتِيًّۭا

**English**: He said, "My Lord, how can I have a son, when my wife is barren, and I have become decrepit with old age?"

### Verse 19:9
**Arabic**: قَالَ كَذَٰلِكَ قَالَ رَبُّكَ هُوَ عَلَىَّ هَيِّنٌۭ وَقَدْ خَلَقْتُكَ مِن قَبْلُ وَلَمْ تَكُ شَيْـًۭٔا

**English**: He said, "It will be so, your Lord says, 'it is easy for me, and I created you before, when you were nothing.'"

### Verse 19:10
**Arabic**: قَالَ رَبِّ ٱجْعَل لِّىٓ ءَايَةًۭ ۚ قَالَ ءَايَتُكَ أَلَّا تُكَلِّمَ ٱلنَّاسَ ثَلَٰثَ لَيَالٍۢ سَوِيًّۭا

**English**: He said, "My Lord, give me a sign." He said, "Your sign is that you will not speak to the people for three nights straight."

### Verse 19:11
**Arabic**: فَخَرَجَ عَلَىٰ قَوْمِهِۦ مِنَ ٱلْمِحْرَابِ فَأَوْحَىٰٓ إِلَيْهِمْ أَن سَبِّحُوا۟ بُكْرَةًۭ وَعَشِيًّۭا

**English**: And he came out to his people, from the sanctuary, and signaled to them to praise morning and evening.

### Verse 19:12
**Arabic**: يَٰيَحْيَىٰ خُذِ ٱلْكِتَٰبَ بِقُوَّةٍۢ ۖ وَءَاتَيْنَٰهُ ٱلْحُكْمَ صَبِيًّۭا

**English**: "O John, hold on to the Scripture firmly," and We gave him wisdom in his youth.

### Verse 19:13
**Arabic**: وَحَنَانًۭا مِّن لَّدُنَّا وَزَكَوٰةًۭ ۖ وَكَانَ تَقِيًّۭا

**English**: And tenderness from Us, and innocence. He was devout.

### Verse 19:14
**Arabic**: وَبَرًّۢا بِوَٰلِدَيْهِ وَلَمْ يَكُن جَبَّارًا عَصِيًّۭا

**English**: And kind to his parents; and he was not a disobedient tyrant.

### Verse 19:15
**Arabic**: وَسَلَٰمٌ عَلَيْهِ يَوْمَ وُلِدَ وَيَوْمَ يَمُوتُ وَيَوْمَ يُبْعَثُ حَيًّۭا

**English**: And peace be upon him the day he was born, and the day he dies, and the Day he is raised alive.

### Verse 19:16
**Arabic**: وَٱذْكُرْ فِى ٱلْكِتَٰبِ مَرْيَمَ إِذِ ٱنتَبَذَتْ مِنْ أَهْلِهَا مَكَانًۭا شَرْقِيًّۭا

**English**: And mention in the Scripture Mary, when she withdrew from her people to an eastern location.

### Verse 19:17
**Arabic**: فَٱتَّخَذَتْ مِن دُونِهِمْ حِجَابًۭا فَأَرْسَلْنَآ إِلَيْهَا رُوحَنَا فَتَمَثَّلَ لَهَا بَشَرًۭا سَوِيًّۭا

**English**: She screened herself away from them, and We sent to her Our spirit, and He appeared to her as an immaculate human.

### Verse 19:18
**Arabic**: قَالَتْ إِنِّىٓ أَعُوذُ بِٱلرَّحْمَٰنِ مِنكَ إِن كُنتَ تَقِيًّۭا

**English**: She said, "I take refuge from you in the Most Merciful, should you be righteous."

### Verse 19:19
**Arabic**: قَالَ إِنَّمَآ أَنَا۠ رَسُولُ رَبِّكِ لِأَهَبَ لَكِ غُلَٰمًۭا زَكِيًّۭا

**English**: He said, "I am only the messenger of your Lord, to give you the gift of a pure son."

### Verse 19:20
**Arabic**: قَالَتْ أَنَّىٰ يَكُونُ لِى غُلَٰمٌۭ وَلَمْ يَمْسَسْنِى بَشَرٌۭ وَلَمْ أَكُ بَغِيًّۭا

**English**: She said, "How can I have a son, when no man has touched me, and I was never unchaste?"

### Verse 19:21
**Arabic**: قَالَ كَذَٰلِكِ قَالَ رَبُّكِ هُوَ عَلَىَّ هَيِّنٌۭ ۖ وَلِنَجْعَلَهُۥٓ ءَايَةًۭ لِّلنَّاسِ وَرَحْمَةًۭ مِّنَّا ۚ وَكَانَ أَمْرًۭا مَّقْضِيًّۭا

**English**: He said, "Thus said your Lord, `It is easy for Me, and We will make him a sign for humanity, and a mercy from Us. It is a matter already decided.'"

### Verse 19:22
**Arabic**: ۞ فَحَمَلَتْهُ فَٱنتَبَذَتْ بِهِۦ مَكَانًۭا قَصِيًّۭا

**English**: So she carried him, and secluded herself with him in a remote place.

### Verse 19:23
**Arabic**: فَأَجَآءَهَا ٱلْمَخَاضُ إِلَىٰ جِذْعِ ٱلنَّخْلَةِ قَالَتْ يَٰلَيْتَنِى مِتُّ قَبْلَ هَٰذَا وَكُنتُ نَسْيًۭا مَّنسِيًّۭا

**English**: The labor-pains came upon her, by the trunk of a palm-tree. She said, "I wish I had died before this, and been completely forgotten."

### Verse 19:24
**Arabic**: فَنَادَىٰهَا مِن تَحْتِهَآ أَلَّا تَحْزَنِى قَدْ جَعَلَ رَبُّكِ تَحْتَكِ سَرِيًّۭا

**English**: Whereupon he called her from beneath her: "Do not worry; your Lord has placed a stream beneath you.

### Verse 19:25
**Arabic**: وَهُزِّىٓ إِلَيْكِ بِجِذْعِ ٱلنَّخْلَةِ تُسَٰقِطْ عَلَيْكِ رُطَبًۭا جَنِيًّۭا

**English**: And shake the trunk of the palm-tree towards you, and it will drop ripe dates by you."

### Verse 19:26
**Arabic**: فَكُلِى وَٱشْرَبِى وَقَرِّى عَيْنًۭا ۖ فَإِمَّا تَرَيِنَّ مِنَ ٱلْبَشَرِ أَحَدًۭا فَقُولِىٓ إِنِّى نَذَرْتُ لِلرَّحْمَٰنِ صَوْمًۭا فَلَنْ أُكَلِّمَ ٱلْيَوْمَ إِنسِيًّۭا

**English**: "So eat, and drink, and be consoled. And if you see any human, say, 'I have vowed a fast to the Most Gracious, so I will not speak to any human today.'"

### Verse 19:27
**Arabic**: فَأَتَتْ بِهِۦ قَوْمَهَا تَحْمِلُهُۥ ۖ قَالُوا۟ يَٰمَرْيَمُ لَقَدْ جِئْتِ شَيْـًۭٔا فَرِيًّۭا

**English**: Then she came to her people, carrying him. They said, "O Mary, you have done something terrible.

### Verse 19:28
**Arabic**: يَٰٓأُخْتَ هَٰرُونَ مَا كَانَ أَبُوكِ ٱمْرَأَ سَوْءٍۢ وَمَا كَانَتْ أُمُّكِ بَغِيًّۭا

**English**: O sister of Aaron, your father was not an evil man, and your mother was not a whore."

### Verse 19:29
**Arabic**: فَأَشَارَتْ إِلَيْهِ ۖ قَالُوا۟ كَيْفَ نُكَلِّمُ مَن كَانَ فِى ٱلْمَهْدِ صَبِيًّۭا

**English**: So she pointed to him. They said, "How can we speak to an infant in the crib?"

### Verse 19:30
**Arabic**: قَالَ إِنِّى عَبْدُ ٱللَّهِ ءَاتَىٰنِىَ ٱلْكِتَٰبَ وَجَعَلَنِى نَبِيًّۭا

**English**: He said, "I am the servant of God. He has given me the Scripture, and made me a prophet.

### Verse 19:31
**Arabic**: وَجَعَلَنِى مُبَارَكًا أَيْنَ مَا كُنتُ وَأَوْصَٰنِى بِٱلصَّلَوٰةِ وَٱلزَّكَوٰةِ مَا دُمْتُ حَيًّۭا

**English**: And has made me blessed wherever I may be; and has enjoined on me prayer and charity, so long as I live.

### Verse 19:32
**Arabic**: وَبَرًّۢا بِوَٰلِدَتِى وَلَمْ يَجْعَلْنِى جَبَّارًۭا شَقِيًّۭا

**English**: And kind to my mother, and He did not make me a disobedient rebel.

### Verse 19:33
**Arabic**: وَٱلسَّلَٰمُ عَلَىَّ يَوْمَ وُلِدتُّ وَيَوْمَ أَمُوتُ وَيَوْمَ أُبْعَثُ حَيًّۭا

**English**: So Peace is upon me the day I was born, and the day I die, and the Day I get resurrected alive."

### Verse 19:34
**Arabic**: ذَٰلِكَ عِيسَى ٱبْنُ مَرْيَمَ ۚ قَوْلَ ٱلْحَقِّ ٱلَّذِى فِيهِ يَمْتَرُونَ

**English**: That is Jesus son of Mary-the Word of truth about which they doubt.

### Verse 19:35
**Arabic**: مَا كَانَ لِلَّهِ أَن يَتَّخِذَ مِن وَلَدٍۢ ۖ سُبْحَٰنَهُۥٓ ۚ إِذَا قَضَىٰٓ أَمْرًۭا فَإِنَّمَا يَقُولُ لَهُۥ كُن فَيَكُونُ

**English**: It is not for God to have a child-glory be to Him. To have anything done, He says to it, "Be," and it becomes.

### Verse 19:36
**Arabic**: وَإِنَّ ٱللَّهَ رَبِّى وَرَبُّكُمْ فَٱعْبُدُوهُ ۚ هَٰذَا صِرَٰطٌۭ مُّسْتَقِيمٌۭ

**English**: "God is my Lord and your Lord, so worship Him. That is a straight path."

### Verse 19:37
**Arabic**: فَٱخْتَلَفَ ٱلْأَحْزَابُ مِنۢ بَيْنِهِمْ ۖ فَوَيْلٌۭ لِّلَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا۟ مِن مَّشْهَدِ يَوْمٍ عَظِيمٍ

**English**: But the various factions differed among themselves. So woe to those who disbelieve from the scene of a tremendous Day.

### Verse 19:38
**Arabic**: أَسْمِعْ بِهِمْ وَأَبْصِرْ يَوْمَ يَأْتُونَنَا ۖ لَٰكِنِ ٱلظَّٰلِمُونَ ٱلْيَوْمَ فِى ضَلَٰلٍۢ مُّبِينٍۢ

**English**: Listen to them and watch for them the Day they come to Us. But the wrongdoers today are completely lost.

### Verse 19:39
**Arabic**: وَأَنذِرْهُمْ يَوْمَ ٱلْحَسْرَةِ إِذْ قُضِىَ ٱلْأَمْرُ وَهُمْ فِى غَفْلَةٍۢ وَهُمْ لَا يُؤْمِنُونَ

**English**: And warn them of the Day of Regret, when the matter will be concluded. Yet they are heedless, and they do not believe.

### Verse 19:40
**Arabic**: إِنَّا نَحْنُ نَرِثُ ٱلْأَرْضَ وَمَنْ عَلَيْهَا وَإِلَيْنَا يُرْجَعُونَ

**English**: It is We who will inherit the earth and everyone on it, and to Us they will be returned.

### Verse 19:41
**Arabic**: وَٱذْكُرْ فِى ٱلْكِتَٰبِ إِبْرَٰهِيمَ ۚ إِنَّهُۥ كَانَ صِدِّيقًۭا نَّبِيًّا

**English**: And mention in the Scripture Abraham. He was a man of truth, a prophet.

### Verse 19:42
**Arabic**: إِذْ قَالَ لِأَبِيهِ يَٰٓأَبَتِ لِمَ تَعْبُدُ مَا لَا يَسْمَعُ وَلَا يُبْصِرُ وَلَا يُغْنِى عَنكَ شَيْـًۭٔا

**English**: He said to his father, "O my father, why do you worship what can neither hear, nor see, nor benefit you in any way?

### Verse 19:43
**Arabic**: يَٰٓأَبَتِ إِنِّى قَدْ جَآءَنِى مِنَ ٱلْعِلْمِ مَا لَمْ يَأْتِكَ فَٱتَّبِعْنِىٓ أَهْدِكَ صِرَٰطًۭا سَوِيًّۭا

**English**: O my father, there has come to me knowledge that never came to you. So follow me, and I will guide you along a straight way.

### Verse 19:44
**Arabic**: يَٰٓأَبَتِ لَا تَعْبُدِ ٱلشَّيْطَٰنَ ۖ إِنَّ ٱلشَّيْطَٰنَ كَانَ لِلرَّحْمَٰنِ عَصِيًّۭا

**English**: O my father, do not worship the devil. The devil is disobedient to the Most Gracious.

### Verse 19:45
**Arabic**: يَٰٓأَبَتِ إِنِّىٓ أَخَافُ أَن يَمَسَّكَ عَذَابٌۭ مِّنَ ٱلرَّحْمَٰنِ فَتَكُونَ لِلشَّيْطَٰنِ وَلِيًّۭا

**English**: O my father, I fear that a punishment from the Most Gracious will afflict you, and you become an ally of the devil."

### Verse 19:46
**Arabic**: قَالَ أَرَاغِبٌ أَنتَ عَنْ ءَالِهَتِى يَٰٓإِبْرَٰهِيمُ ۖ لَئِن لَّمْ تَنتَهِ لَأَرْجُمَنَّكَ ۖ وَٱهْجُرْنِى مَلِيًّۭا

**English**: He said, "Are you renouncing my gods, O Abraham? If you do not desist, I will stone you. So leave me alone for a while."

### Verse 19:47
**Arabic**: قَالَ سَلَٰمٌ عَلَيْكَ ۖ سَأَسْتَغْفِرُ لَكَ رَبِّىٓ ۖ إِنَّهُۥ كَانَ بِى حَفِيًّۭا

**English**: He said, "Peace be upon you. I will ask my Lord to forgive you; He has been Kind to me.

### Verse 19:48
**Arabic**: وَأَعْتَزِلُكُمْ وَمَا تَدْعُونَ مِن دُونِ ٱللَّهِ وَأَدْعُوا۟ رَبِّى عَسَىٰٓ أَلَّآ أَكُونَ بِدُعَآءِ رَبِّى شَقِيًّۭا

**English**: And I will withdraw from you, and from what you pray to instead of God. And I will pray to my Lord, and I hope I will not be disappointed in my prayer to my Lord."

### Verse 19:49
**Arabic**: فَلَمَّا ٱعْتَزَلَهُمْ وَمَا يَعْبُدُونَ مِن دُونِ ٱللَّهِ وَهَبْنَا لَهُۥٓ إِسْحَٰقَ وَيَعْقُوبَ ۖ وَكُلًّۭا جَعَلْنَا نَبِيًّۭا

**English**: When he withdrew from them, and from what they worship besides God, We granted him Isaac and Jacob. And each We made a prophet.

### Verse 19:50
**Arabic**: وَوَهَبْنَا لَهُم مِّن رَّحْمَتِنَا وَجَعَلْنَا لَهُمْ لِسَانَ صِدْقٍ عَلِيًّۭا

**English**: And We gave them freely of Our mercy, and gave them a noble reputation of truth.

### Verse 19:51
**Arabic**: وَٱذْكُرْ فِى ٱلْكِتَٰبِ مُوسَىٰٓ ۚ إِنَّهُۥ كَانَ مُخْلَصًۭا وَكَانَ رَسُولًۭا نَّبِيًّۭا

**English**: And mention in the Scripture Moses. He was dedicated. He was a messenger and a prophet.

### Verse 19:52
**Arabic**: وَنَٰدَيْنَٰهُ مِن جَانِبِ ٱلطُّورِ ٱلْأَيْمَنِ وَقَرَّبْنَٰهُ نَجِيًّۭا

**English**: And We called him from the right side of the Mount, and brought him near in communion.

### Verse 19:53
