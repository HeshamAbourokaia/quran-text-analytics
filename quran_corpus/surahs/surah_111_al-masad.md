# Surah 111: Al-Masad (المسد)

- **Mushaf number**: 111
- **Place of revelation**: Mecca (Makkiyah)
- **Verse count**: 5
- **Themes**: [Warning](../themes/warning.md)
- **Named entities mentioned**: [Allah](../entities/divine_allah.md), [Ar-Rahim (the Most Merciful)](../entities/divine_ar-rahim.md), [Ar-Rahman (the Most Gracious)](../entities/divine_ar-rahman.md)

## Verses

### Verse 111:0
**Arabic**: بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

**English**: In the name of God, the Gracious, the Merciful.

### Verse 111:1
**Arabic**: تَبَّتْ يَدَآ أَبِى لَهَبٍۢ وَتَبَّ

**English**: Condemned are the hands of Abee Lahab, and he is condemned.

### Verse 111:2
**Arabic**: مَآ أَغْنَىٰ عَنْهُ مَالُهُۥ وَمَا كَسَبَ

**English**: His wealth did not avail him, nor did what he acquired.

### Verse 111:3
**Arabic**: سَيَصْلَىٰ نَارًۭا ذَاتَ لَهَبٍۢ

**English**: He will burn in a Flaming Fire.

### Verse 111:4
**Arabic**: وَٱمْرَأَتُهُۥ حَمَّالَةَ ٱلْحَطَبِ

**English**: And his wife-the firewood carrier.

### Verse 111:5
**Arabic**: فِى جِيدِهَا حَبْلٌۭ مِّن مَّسَدٍۭ

**English**: Around her neck is a rope of thorns.
