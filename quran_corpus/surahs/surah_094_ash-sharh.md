# Surah 94: Ash-Sharh (الشرح)

- **Mushaf number**: 94
- **Place of revelation**: Mecca (Makkiyah)
- **Verse count**: 8
- **Themes**: [Ethics and Character](../themes/ethics.md), [Patience and Endurance](../themes/patience.md)
- **Named entities mentioned**: [Allah](../entities/divine_allah.md), [Ar-Rahim (the Most Merciful)](../entities/divine_ar-rahim.md), [Ar-Rahman (the Most Gracious)](../entities/divine_ar-rahman.md), [Rabb (the Lord)](../entities/divine_rabb.md)

## Verses

### Verse 94:0
**Arabic**: بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

**English**: In the name of God, the Gracious, the Merciful.

### Verse 94:1
**Arabic**: أَلَمْ نَشْرَحْ لَكَ صَدْرَكَ

**English**: Did We not soothe your heart?

### Verse 94:2
**Arabic**: وَوَضَعْنَا عَنكَ وِزْرَكَ

**English**: And lift from you your burden.

### Verse 94:3
**Arabic**: ٱلَّذِىٓ أَنقَضَ ظَهْرَكَ

**English**: Which weighed down your back?

### Verse 94:4
**Arabic**: وَرَفَعْنَا لَكَ ذِكْرَكَ

**English**: And raised for you your reputation?

### Verse 94:5
**Arabic**: فَإِنَّ مَعَ ٱلْعُسْرِ يُسْرًا

**English**: With hardship comes ease.

### Verse 94:6
**Arabic**: إِنَّ مَعَ ٱلْعُسْرِ يُسْرًۭا

**English**: With hardship comes ease.

### Verse 94:7
**Arabic**: فَإِذَا فَرَغْتَ فَٱنصَبْ

**English**: When your work is done, turn to devotion.

### Verse 94:8
**Arabic**: وَإِلَىٰ رَبِّكَ فَٱرْغَب

**English**: And to your Lord turn for everything.
