# Surah 72: Al-Jinn (الجن)

- **Mushaf number**: 72
- **Place of revelation**: Mecca (Makkiyah)
- **Verse count**: 28
- **Named entities mentioned**: [Allah](../entities/divine_allah.md), [Ar-Rahim (the Most Merciful)](../entities/divine_ar-rahim.md), [Ar-Rahman (the Most Gracious)](../entities/divine_ar-rahman.md), [Rabb (the Lord)](../entities/divine_rabb.md)

- **Revelation order (chronological)**: #40 of 114
- **Revelation phase**: [Early Meccan](../timeline/phase_early-mecca.md) (610-615 CE, Year 1-5 of Prophethood)

## Verses

### Verse 72:0
**Arabic**: بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

**English**: In the name of God, the Gracious, the Merciful.

### Verse 72:1
**Arabic**: قُلْ أُوحِىَ إِلَىَّ أَنَّهُ ٱسْتَمَعَ نَفَرٌۭ مِّنَ ٱلْجِنِّ فَقَالُوٓا۟ إِنَّا سَمِعْنَا قُرْءَانًا عَجَبًۭا

**English**: Say, "It was revealed to me that a band of jinn listened in, and said, 'We have heard a wondrous Quran.

### Verse 72:2
**Arabic**: يَهْدِىٓ إِلَى ٱلرُّشْدِ فَـَٔامَنَّا بِهِۦ ۖ وَلَن نُّشْرِكَ بِرَبِّنَآ أَحَدًۭا

**English**: It guides to rectitude, so we have believed in it; and we will never associate anyone with our Lord.

### Verse 72:3
**Arabic**: وَأَنَّهُۥ تَعَٰلَىٰ جَدُّ رَبِّنَا مَا ٱتَّخَذَ صَٰحِبَةًۭ وَلَا وَلَدًۭا

**English**: And Exalted is the Grandeur of our Lord-He never had a mate, nor a child.

### Verse 72:4
**Arabic**: وَأَنَّهُۥ كَانَ يَقُولُ سَفِيهُنَا عَلَى ٱللَّهِ شَطَطًۭا

**English**: But the fools among us used to say nonsense about God.

### Verse 72:5
**Arabic**: وَأَنَّا ظَنَنَّآ أَن لَّن تَقُولَ ٱلْإِنسُ وَٱلْجِنُّ عَلَى ٱللَّهِ كَذِبًۭا

**English**: And we thought that humans and jinn would never utter lies about God.

### Verse 72:6
**Arabic**: وَأَنَّهُۥ كَانَ رِجَالٌۭ مِّنَ ٱلْإِنسِ يَعُوذُونَ بِرِجَالٍۢ مِّنَ ٱلْجِنِّ فَزَادُوهُمْ رَهَقًۭا

**English**: Some individual humans used to seek power through some individual jinn, but they only increased them in confusion.

### Verse 72:7
**Arabic**: وَأَنَّهُمْ ظَنُّوا۟ كَمَا ظَنَنتُمْ أَن لَّن يَبْعَثَ ٱللَّهُ أَحَدًۭا

**English**: They thought, as you thought, that God would never resurrect anyone.

### Verse 72:8
**Arabic**: وَأَنَّا لَمَسْنَا ٱلسَّمَآءَ فَوَجَدْنَٰهَا مُلِئَتْ حَرَسًۭا شَدِيدًۭا وَشُهُبًۭا

**English**: We probed the heaven, and found it filled with stern guards and projectiles.

### Verse 72:9
**Arabic**: وَأَنَّا كُنَّا نَقْعُدُ مِنْهَا مَقَٰعِدَ لِلسَّمْعِ ۖ فَمَن يَسْتَمِعِ ٱلْءَانَ يَجِدْ لَهُۥ شِهَابًۭا رَّصَدًۭا

**English**: We used to take up positions to listen in; but whoever listens now finds a projectile in wait for him.

### Verse 72:10
**Arabic**: وَأَنَّا لَا نَدْرِىٓ أَشَرٌّ أُرِيدَ بِمَن فِى ٱلْأَرْضِ أَمْ أَرَادَ بِهِمْ رَبُّهُمْ رَشَدًۭا

**English**: We do not know whether ill is intended for those on earth, or if their Lord intends goodness for them.

### Verse 72:11
**Arabic**: وَأَنَّا مِنَّا ٱلصَّٰلِحُونَ وَمِنَّا دُونَ ذَٰلِكَ ۖ كُنَّا طَرَآئِقَ قِدَدًۭا

**English**: Some of us are righteous, but some of us are less than that; we follow divergent paths.

### Verse 72:12
**Arabic**: وَأَنَّا ظَنَنَّآ أَن لَّن نُّعْجِزَ ٱللَّهَ فِى ٱلْأَرْضِ وَلَن نُّعْجِزَهُۥ هَرَبًۭا

**English**: We realized that we cannot defeat God on earth, and that we cannot escape Him by fleeing.

### Verse 72:13
**Arabic**: وَأَنَّا لَمَّا سَمِعْنَا ٱلْهُدَىٰٓ ءَامَنَّا بِهِۦ ۖ فَمَن يُؤْمِنۢ بِرَبِّهِۦ فَلَا يَخَافُ بَخْسًۭا وَلَا رَهَقًۭا

**English**: And when we heard the guidance, we believed in it. Whoever believes in his Lord fears neither loss, nor burden.

### Verse 72:14
**Arabic**: وَأَنَّا مِنَّا ٱلْمُسْلِمُونَ وَمِنَّا ٱلْقَٰسِطُونَ ۖ فَمَنْ أَسْلَمَ فَأُو۟لَٰٓئِكَ تَحَرَّوْا۟ رَشَدًۭا

**English**: Among us are those who are submitting, and among us are the compromisers. As for those who have submitted-it is they who pursue rectitude.

### Verse 72:15
**Arabic**: وَأَمَّا ٱلْقَٰسِطُونَ فَكَانُوا۟ لِجَهَنَّمَ حَطَبًۭا

**English**: But as for the compromisers-they will be firewood for Hell.'"

### Verse 72:16
**Arabic**: وَأَلَّوِ ٱسْتَقَٰمُوا۟ عَلَى ٱلطَّرِيقَةِ لَأَسْقَيْنَٰهُم مَّآءً غَدَقًۭا

**English**: Had they kept true to the Path, We would have given them plenty water to drink.

### Verse 72:17
**Arabic**: لِّنَفْتِنَهُمْ فِيهِ ۚ وَمَن يُعْرِضْ عَن ذِكْرِ رَبِّهِۦ يَسْلُكْهُ عَذَابًۭا صَعَدًۭا

**English**: To test them with it. Whoever turns away from the remembrance of his Lord, He will direct him to torment ever mounting.

### Verse 72:18
**Arabic**: وَأَنَّ ٱلْمَسَٰجِدَ لِلَّهِ فَلَا تَدْعُوا۟ مَعَ ٱللَّهِ أَحَدًۭا

**English**: The places of worship are for God. So do not call, besides God, upon anyone else.

### Verse 72:19
**Arabic**: وَأَنَّهُۥ لَمَّا قَامَ عَبْدُ ٱللَّهِ يَدْعُوهُ كَادُوا۟ يَكُونُونَ عَلَيْهِ لِبَدًۭا

**English**: And when the servant of God got up calling on Him, they almost fell on him in a mass.

### Verse 72:20
**Arabic**: قُلْ إِنَّمَآ أَدْعُوا۟ رَبِّى وَلَآ أُشْرِكُ بِهِۦٓ أَحَدًۭا

**English**: Say, "I pray only to my Lord, and I never associate anyone with Him."

### Verse 72:21
**Arabic**: قُلْ إِنِّى لَآ أَمْلِكُ لَكُمْ ضَرًّۭا وَلَا رَشَدًۭا

**English**: Say, "It is not in my power to harm you, nor to bring you to right conduct."

### Verse 72:22
**Arabic**: قُلْ إِنِّى لَن يُجِيرَنِى مِنَ ٱللَّهِ أَحَدٌۭ وَلَنْ أَجِدَ مِن دُونِهِۦ مُلْتَحَدًا

**English**: Say, "No one can protect me from God, and I will not find any refuge except with Him.

### Verse 72:23
**Arabic**: إِلَّا بَلَٰغًۭا مِّنَ ٱللَّهِ وَرِسَٰلَٰتِهِۦ ۚ وَمَن يَعْصِ ٱللَّهَ وَرَسُولَهُۥ فَإِنَّ لَهُۥ نَارَ جَهَنَّمَ خَٰلِدِينَ فِيهَآ أَبَدًا

**English**: Except for a proclamation from God and His messages. He who defies God and His Messenger-for him is the Fire of Hell, in which they will dwell forever."

### Verse 72:24
**Arabic**: حَتَّىٰٓ إِذَا رَأَوْا۟ مَا يُوعَدُونَ فَسَيَعْلَمُونَ مَنْ أَضْعَفُ نَاصِرًۭا وَأَقَلُّ عَدَدًۭا

**English**: Until, when they see what they were promised, they will know who is weaker in helpers, and fewer in numbers.

### Verse 72:25
**Arabic**: قُلْ إِنْ أَدْرِىٓ أَقَرِيبٌۭ مَّا تُوعَدُونَ أَمْ يَجْعَلُ لَهُۥ رَبِّىٓ أَمَدًا

**English**: Say, "I do not know whether what you are promised is near, or whether my Lord will extend it for a period."

### Verse 72:26
**Arabic**: عَٰلِمُ ٱلْغَيْبِ فَلَا يُظْهِرُ عَلَىٰ غَيْبِهِۦٓ أَحَدًا

**English**: The Knower of the Invisible; He does not disclose His Invisible to anyone.

### Verse 72:27
**Arabic**: إِلَّا مَنِ ٱرْتَضَىٰ مِن رَّسُولٍۢ فَإِنَّهُۥ يَسْلُكُ مِنۢ بَيْنِ يَدَيْهِ وَمِنْ خَلْفِهِۦ رَصَدًۭا

**English**: Except to a Messenger of His choosing. He then dispatches guards before him and behind him.

### Verse 72:28
**Arabic**: لِّيَعْلَمَ أَن قَدْ أَبْلَغُوا۟ رِسَٰلَٰتِ رَبِّهِمْ وَأَحَاطَ بِمَا لَدَيْهِمْ وَأَحْصَىٰ كُلَّ شَىْءٍ عَدَدًۢا

**English**: That He may know that they have conveyed the messages of their Lord. He encompasses what they have, and has tallied everything by number.
