# Surah 86: At-Tariq (الطارق)

- **Mushaf number**: 86
- **Place of revelation**: Mecca (Makkiyah)
- **Verse count**: 17
- **Themes**: [Ethics and Character](../themes/ethics.md)
- **Named entities mentioned**: [Allah](../entities/divine_allah.md), [Ar-Rahim (the Most Merciful)](../entities/divine_ar-rahim.md), [Ar-Rahman (the Most Gracious)](../entities/divine_ar-rahman.md)

## Verses

### Verse 86:0
**Arabic**: بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

**English**: In the name of God, the Gracious, the Merciful.

### Verse 86:1
**Arabic**: وَٱلسَّمَآءِ وَٱلطَّارِقِ

**English**: By the sky and at-Tariq.

### Verse 86:2
**Arabic**: وَمَآ أَدْرَىٰكَ مَا ٱلطَّارِقُ

**English**: But what will let you know what at-Tariq is?

### Verse 86:3
**Arabic**: ٱلنَّجْمُ ٱلثَّاقِبُ

**English**: The Piercing Star.

### Verse 86:4
**Arabic**: إِن كُلُّ نَفْسٍۢ لَّمَّا عَلَيْهَا حَافِظٌۭ

**English**: There is no soul without a Protector over it.

### Verse 86:5
**Arabic**: فَلْيَنظُرِ ٱلْإِنسَٰنُ مِمَّ خُلِقَ

**English**: Let man consider what he was created from.

### Verse 86:6
**Arabic**: خُلِقَ مِن مَّآءٍۢ دَافِقٍۢ

**English**: He was created from gushing liquid.

### Verse 86:7
**Arabic**: يَخْرُجُ مِنۢ بَيْنِ ٱلصُّلْبِ وَٱلتَّرَآئِبِ

**English**: Issuing from between the backbone and the breastbones.

### Verse 86:8
**Arabic**: إِنَّهُۥ عَلَىٰ رَجْعِهِۦ لَقَادِرٌۭ

**English**: He is certainly able to return him.

### Verse 86:9
**Arabic**: يَوْمَ تُبْلَى ٱلسَّرَآئِرُ

**English**: On the Day when the secrets are disclosed.

### Verse 86:10
**Arabic**: فَمَا لَهُۥ مِن قُوَّةٍۢ وَلَا نَاصِرٍۢ

**English**: He will have no strength, and no supporter.

### Verse 86:11
**Arabic**: وَٱلسَّمَآءِ ذَاتِ ٱلرَّجْعِ

**English**: By the sky that returns.

### Verse 86:12
**Arabic**: وَٱلْأَرْضِ ذَاتِ ٱلصَّدْعِ

**English**: And the earth that cracks open.

### Verse 86:13
**Arabic**: إِنَّهُۥ لَقَوْلٌۭ فَصْلٌۭ

**English**: It is a Decisive Word.

### Verse 86:14
**Arabic**: وَمَا هُوَ بِٱلْهَزْلِ

**English**: It is no joke.

### Verse 86:15
**Arabic**: إِنَّهُمْ يَكِيدُونَ كَيْدًۭا

**English**: They plot and scheme.

### Verse 86:16
**Arabic**: وَأَكِيدُ كَيْدًۭا

**English**: But I plot and scheme.

### Verse 86:17
**Arabic**: فَمَهِّلِ ٱلْكَٰفِرِينَ أَمْهِلْهُمْ رُوَيْدًۢا

**English**: Therefore, give the blasphemers respite, a brief respite.
