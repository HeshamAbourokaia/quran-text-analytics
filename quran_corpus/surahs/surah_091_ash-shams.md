# Surah 91: Ash-Shams (الشمس)

- **Mushaf number**: 91
- **Place of revelation**: Mecca (Makkiyah)
- **Verse count**: 15
- **Themes**: [Faith and Monotheism](../themes/faith-monotheism.md)
- **Named entities mentioned**: [Allah](../entities/divine_allah.md), [Ar-Rahim (the Most Merciful)](../entities/divine_ar-rahim.md), [Ar-Rahman (the Most Gracious)](../entities/divine_ar-rahman.md), [Rabb (the Lord)](../entities/divine_rabb.md)

- **Revelation order (chronological)**: #26 of 114
- **Revelation phase**: [Early Meccan](../timeline/phase_early-mecca.md) (610-615 CE, Year 1-5 of Prophethood)

## Verses

### Verse 91:0
**Arabic**: بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

**English**: In the name of God, the Gracious, the Merciful.

### Verse 91:1
**Arabic**: وَٱلشَّمْسِ وَضُحَىٰهَا

**English**: By the sun and its radiance.

### Verse 91:2
**Arabic**: وَٱلْقَمَرِ إِذَا تَلَىٰهَا

**English**: And the moon as it follows it.

### Verse 91:3
**Arabic**: وَٱلنَّهَارِ إِذَا جَلَّىٰهَا

**English**: And the day as it reveals it.

### Verse 91:4
**Arabic**: وَٱلَّيْلِ إِذَا يَغْشَىٰهَا

**English**: And the night as it conceals it.

### Verse 91:5
**Arabic**: وَٱلسَّمَآءِ وَمَا بَنَىٰهَا

**English**: And the sky and He who built it.

### Verse 91:6
**Arabic**: وَٱلْأَرْضِ وَمَا طَحَىٰهَا

**English**: And the earth and He who spread it.

### Verse 91:7
**Arabic**: وَنَفْسٍۢ وَمَا سَوَّىٰهَا

**English**: And the soul and He who proportioned it.

### Verse 91:8
**Arabic**: فَأَلْهَمَهَا فُجُورَهَا وَتَقْوَىٰهَا

**English**: And inspired it with its wickedness and its righteousness.

### Verse 91:9
**Arabic**: قَدْ أَفْلَحَ مَن زَكَّىٰهَا

**English**: Successful is he who purifies it.

### Verse 91:10
**Arabic**: وَقَدْ خَابَ مَن دَسَّىٰهَا

**English**: Failing is he who corrupts it.

### Verse 91:11
**Arabic**: كَذَّبَتْ ثَمُودُ بِطَغْوَىٰهَآ

**English**: Thamood denied in its pride.

### Verse 91:12
**Arabic**: إِذِ ٱنۢبَعَثَ أَشْقَىٰهَا

**English**: When it followed its most wicked.

### Verse 91:13
**Arabic**: فَقَالَ لَهُمْ رَسُولُ ٱللَّهِ نَاقَةَ ٱللَّهِ وَسُقْيَٰهَا

**English**: The messenger of God said to them, "This is the she-camel of God, so let her drink."

### Verse 91:14
**Arabic**: فَكَذَّبُوهُ فَعَقَرُوهَا فَدَمْدَمَ عَلَيْهِمْ رَبُّهُم بِذَنۢبِهِمْ فَسَوَّىٰهَا

**English**: But they called him a liar, and hamstrung her. So their Lord crushed them for their sin, and leveled it.

### Verse 91:15
**Arabic**: وَلَا يَخَافُ عُقْبَٰهَا

**English**: And He does not fear its sequel.
