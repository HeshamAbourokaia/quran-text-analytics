# Surah 81: At-Takwir (التكوير)

- **Mushaf number**: 81
- **Place of revelation**: Mecca (Makkiyah)
- **Verse count**: 29
- **Themes**: [Day of Judgment](../themes/day-of-judgment.md)
- **Named entities mentioned**: [Allah](../entities/divine_allah.md), [Ar-Rahim (the Most Merciful)](../entities/divine_ar-rahim.md), [Ar-Rahman (the Most Gracious)](../entities/divine_ar-rahman.md), [Rabb (the Lord)](../entities/divine_rabb.md)

- **Revelation order (chronological)**: #7 of 114
- **Revelation phase**: [Early Meccan](../timeline/phase_early-mecca.md) (610-615 CE, Year 1-5 of Prophethood)

## Verses

### Verse 81:0
**Arabic**: بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

**English**: In the name of God, the Gracious, the Merciful.

### Verse 81:1
**Arabic**: إِذَا ٱلشَّمْسُ كُوِّرَتْ

**English**: When the sun is rolled up.

### Verse 81:2
**Arabic**: وَإِذَا ٱلنُّجُومُ ٱنكَدَرَتْ

**English**: When the stars are dimmed.

### Verse 81:3
**Arabic**: وَإِذَا ٱلْجِبَالُ سُيِّرَتْ

**English**: When the mountains are set in motion.

### Verse 81:4
**Arabic**: وَإِذَا ٱلْعِشَارُ عُطِّلَتْ

**English**: When the relationships are suspended.

### Verse 81:5
**Arabic**: وَإِذَا ٱلْوُحُوشُ حُشِرَتْ

**English**: When the beasts are gathered.

### Verse 81:6
**Arabic**: وَإِذَا ٱلْبِحَارُ سُجِّرَتْ

**English**: When the oceans are set aflame.

### Verse 81:7
**Arabic**: وَإِذَا ٱلنُّفُوسُ زُوِّجَتْ

**English**: When the souls are paired.

### Verse 81:8
**Arabic**: وَإِذَا ٱلْمَوْءُۥدَةُ سُئِلَتْ

**English**: When the girl, buried alive, is asked:

### Verse 81:9
**Arabic**: بِأَىِّ ذَنۢبٍۢ قُتِلَتْ

**English**: For what crime was she killed?

### Verse 81:10
**Arabic**: وَإِذَا ٱلصُّحُفُ نُشِرَتْ

**English**: When the records are made public.

### Verse 81:11
**Arabic**: وَإِذَا ٱلسَّمَآءُ كُشِطَتْ

**English**: When the sky is peeled away.

### Verse 81:12
**Arabic**: وَإِذَا ٱلْجَحِيمُ سُعِّرَتْ

**English**: When the Fire is set ablaze.

### Verse 81:13
**Arabic**: وَإِذَا ٱلْجَنَّةُ أُزْلِفَتْ

**English**: When Paradise is brought near.

### Verse 81:14
**Arabic**: عَلِمَتْ نَفْسٌۭ مَّآ أَحْضَرَتْ

**English**: Each soul will know what it has readied.

### Verse 81:15
**Arabic**: فَلَآ أُقْسِمُ بِٱلْخُنَّسِ

**English**: I swear by the galaxies.

### Verse 81:16
**Arabic**: ٱلْجَوَارِ ٱلْكُنَّسِ

**English**: Precisely running their courses.

### Verse 81:17
**Arabic**: وَٱلَّيْلِ إِذَا عَسْعَسَ

**English**: And by the night as it recedes.

### Verse 81:18
**Arabic**: وَٱلصُّبْحِ إِذَا تَنَفَّسَ

**English**: And by the morn as it breathes.

### Verse 81:19
**Arabic**: إِنَّهُۥ لَقَوْلُ رَسُولٍۢ كَرِيمٍۢ

**English**: This is the speech of a noble messenger.

### Verse 81:20
**Arabic**: ذِى قُوَّةٍ عِندَ ذِى ٱلْعَرْشِ مَكِينٍۢ

**English**: Endowed with power, eminent with the Lord of the Throne.

### Verse 81:21
**Arabic**: مُّطَاعٍۢ ثَمَّ أَمِينٍۢ

**English**: Obeyed and honest.

### Verse 81:22
**Arabic**: وَمَا صَاحِبُكُم بِمَجْنُونٍۢ

**English**: Your friend is not possessed.

### Verse 81:23
**Arabic**: وَلَقَدْ رَءَاهُ بِٱلْأُفُقِ ٱلْمُبِينِ

**English**: He saw him on the luminous horizon.

### Verse 81:24
**Arabic**: وَمَا هُوَ عَلَى ٱلْغَيْبِ بِضَنِينٍۢ

**English**: And He does not withhold knowledge of the Unseen.

### Verse 81:25
**Arabic**: وَمَا هُوَ بِقَوْلِ شَيْطَٰنٍۢ رَّجِيمٍۢ

**English**: And it is not the word of an accursed devil.

### Verse 81:26
**Arabic**: فَأَيْنَ تَذْهَبُونَ

**English**: So where are you heading?

### Verse 81:27
**Arabic**: إِنْ هُوَ إِلَّا ذِكْرٌۭ لِّلْعَٰلَمِينَ

**English**: It is only a Reminder to all mankind.

### Verse 81:28
**Arabic**: لِمَن شَآءَ مِنكُمْ أَن يَسْتَقِيمَ

**English**: To whoever of you wills to go straight.

### Verse 81:29
**Arabic**: وَمَا تَشَآءُونَ إِلَّآ أَن يَشَآءَ ٱللَّهُ رَبُّ ٱلْعَٰلَمِينَ

**English**: But you cannot will, unless God wills-The Lord of the Worlds.
