# Surah 78: An-Naba (النبأ)

- **Mushaf number**: 78
- **Place of revelation**: Mecca (Makkiyah)
- **Verse count**: 40
- **Themes**: [Day of Judgment](../themes/day-of-judgment.md)
- **Named entities mentioned**: [Allah](../entities/divine_allah.md), [Ar-Rahim (the Most Merciful)](../entities/divine_ar-rahim.md), [Ar-Rahman (the Most Gracious)](../entities/divine_ar-rahman.md), [Rabb (the Lord)](../entities/divine_rabb.md)

## Verses

### Verse 78:0
**Arabic**: بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

**English**: In the name of God, the Gracious, the Merciful.

### Verse 78:1
**Arabic**: عَمَّ يَتَسَآءَلُونَ

**English**: What are they asking one another about?

### Verse 78:2
**Arabic**: عَنِ ٱلنَّبَإِ ٱلْعَظِيمِ

**English**: About the Great Event.

### Verse 78:3
**Arabic**: ٱلَّذِى هُمْ فِيهِ مُخْتَلِفُونَ

**English**: About which they disagree.

### Verse 78:4
**Arabic**: كَلَّا سَيَعْلَمُونَ

**English**: Surely, they will find out.

### Verse 78:5
**Arabic**: ثُمَّ كَلَّا سَيَعْلَمُونَ

**English**: Most certainly, they will find out.

### Verse 78:6
**Arabic**: أَلَمْ نَجْعَلِ ٱلْأَرْضَ مِهَٰدًۭا

**English**: Did We not make the earth a cradle?

### Verse 78:7
**Arabic**: وَٱلْجِبَالَ أَوْتَادًۭا

**English**: And the mountains pegs?

### Verse 78:8
**Arabic**: وَخَلَقْنَٰكُمْ أَزْوَٰجًۭا

**English**: And created you in pairs?

### Verse 78:9
**Arabic**: وَجَعَلْنَا نَوْمَكُمْ سُبَاتًۭا

**English**: And made your sleep for rest?

### Verse 78:10
**Arabic**: وَجَعَلْنَا ٱلَّيْلَ لِبَاسًۭا

**English**: And made the night a cover?

### Verse 78:11
**Arabic**: وَجَعَلْنَا ٱلنَّهَارَ مَعَاشًۭا

**English**: And made the day for livelihood?

### Verse 78:12
**Arabic**: وَبَنَيْنَا فَوْقَكُمْ سَبْعًۭا شِدَادًۭا

**English**: And built above you seven strong ones?

### Verse 78:13
**Arabic**: وَجَعَلْنَا سِرَاجًۭا وَهَّاجًۭا

**English**: And placed a blazing lamp?

### Verse 78:14
**Arabic**: وَأَنزَلْنَا مِنَ ٱلْمُعْصِرَٰتِ مَآءًۭ ثَجَّاجًۭا

**English**: And brought down from the clouds pouring water?

### Verse 78:15
**Arabic**: لِّنُخْرِجَ بِهِۦ حَبًّۭا وَنَبَاتًۭا

**English**: To produce with it grains and vegetation?

### Verse 78:16
**Arabic**: وَجَنَّٰتٍ أَلْفَافًا

**English**: And luxuriant gardens?

### Verse 78:17
**Arabic**: إِنَّ يَوْمَ ٱلْفَصْلِ كَانَ مِيقَٰتًۭا

**English**: The Day of Sorting has been appointed.

### Verse 78:18
**Arabic**: يَوْمَ يُنفَخُ فِى ٱلصُّورِ فَتَأْتُونَ أَفْوَاجًۭا

**English**: The Day when the Trumpet is blown, and you will come in droves.

### Verse 78:19
**Arabic**: وَفُتِحَتِ ٱلسَّمَآءُ فَكَانَتْ أَبْوَٰبًۭا

**English**: And the sky is opened up, and becomes gateways.

### Verse 78:20
**Arabic**: وَسُيِّرَتِ ٱلْجِبَالُ فَكَانَتْ سَرَابًا

**English**: And the mountains are set in motion, and become a mirage.

### Verse 78:21
**Arabic**: إِنَّ جَهَنَّمَ كَانَتْ مِرْصَادًۭا

**English**: Hell is lying in ambush.

### Verse 78:22
**Arabic**: لِّلطَّٰغِينَ مَـَٔابًۭا

**English**: For the oppressors, a destination.

### Verse 78:23
**Arabic**: لَّٰبِثِينَ فِيهَآ أَحْقَابًۭا

**English**: Where they will remain for eons.

### Verse 78:24
**Arabic**: لَّا يَذُوقُونَ فِيهَا بَرْدًۭا وَلَا شَرَابًا

**English**: They will taste therein neither coolness, nor drink.

### Verse 78:25
**Arabic**: إِلَّا حَمِيمًۭا وَغَسَّاقًۭا

**English**: Except boiling water, and freezing hail.

### Verse 78:26
**Arabic**: جَزَآءًۭ وِفَاقًا

**English**: A fitting requital.

### Verse 78:27
**Arabic**: إِنَّهُمْ كَانُوا۟ لَا يَرْجُونَ حِسَابًۭا

**English**: For they were not anticipating any reckoning.

### Verse 78:28
**Arabic**: وَكَذَّبُوا۟ بِـَٔايَٰتِنَا كِذَّابًۭا

**English**: And they denied Our signs utterly.

### Verse 78:29
**Arabic**: وَكُلَّ شَىْءٍ أَحْصَيْنَٰهُ كِتَٰبًۭا

**English**: But We have enumerated everything in writing.

### Verse 78:30
**Arabic**: فَذُوقُوا۟ فَلَن نَّزِيدَكُمْ إِلَّا عَذَابًا

**English**: So taste! We will increase you only in suffering.

### Verse 78:31
**Arabic**: إِنَّ لِلْمُتَّقِينَ مَفَازًا

**English**: But for the righteous there is triumph.

### Verse 78:32
**Arabic**: حَدَآئِقَ وَأَعْنَٰبًۭا

**English**: Gardens and vineyards.

### Verse 78:33
**Arabic**: وَكَوَاعِبَ أَتْرَابًۭا

**English**: And splendid spouses, well matched.

### Verse 78:34
**Arabic**: وَكَأْسًۭا دِهَاقًۭا

**English**: And delicious drinks.

### Verse 78:35
**Arabic**: لَّا يَسْمَعُونَ فِيهَا لَغْوًۭا وَلَا كِذَّٰبًۭا

**English**: They will hear therein neither gossip, nor lies.

### Verse 78:36
**Arabic**: جَزَآءًۭ مِّن رَّبِّكَ عَطَآءً حِسَابًۭا

**English**: A reward from your Lord, a fitting gift.

### Verse 78:37
**Arabic**: رَّبِّ ٱلسَّمَٰوَٰتِ وَٱلْأَرْضِ وَمَا بَيْنَهُمَا ٱلرَّحْمَٰنِ ۖ لَا يَمْلِكُونَ مِنْهُ خِطَابًۭا

**English**: Lord of the heavens and the earth, and everything between them-The Most Merciful-none can argue with Him.

### Verse 78:38
**Arabic**: يَوْمَ يَقُومُ ٱلرُّوحُ وَٱلْمَلَٰٓئِكَةُ صَفًّۭا ۖ لَّا يَتَكَلَّمُونَ إِلَّا مَنْ أَذِنَ لَهُ ٱلرَّحْمَٰنُ وَقَالَ صَوَابًۭا

**English**: On the Day when the Spirit and the angels stand in row. They will not speak, unless it be one permitted by the Most Merciful, and he will say what is right.

### Verse 78:39
**Arabic**: ذَٰلِكَ ٱلْيَوْمُ ٱلْحَقُّ ۖ فَمَن شَآءَ ٱتَّخَذَ إِلَىٰ رَبِّهِۦ مَـَٔابًا

**English**: That is the Day of Reality. So whoever wills, let him take a way back to his Lord.

### Verse 78:40
**Arabic**: إِنَّآ أَنذَرْنَٰكُمْ عَذَابًۭا قَرِيبًۭا يَوْمَ يَنظُرُ ٱلْمَرْءُ مَا قَدَّمَتْ يَدَاهُ وَيَقُولُ ٱلْكَافِرُ يَٰلَيْتَنِى كُنتُ تُرَٰبًۢا

**English**: We have warned you of a near punishment-the Day when a person will observe what his hands have produced, and the faithless will say, "O, I wish I were dust."
