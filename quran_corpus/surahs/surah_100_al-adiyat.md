# Surah 100: Al-Adiyat (العاديات)

- **Mushaf number**: 100
- **Place of revelation**: Mecca (Makkiyah)
- **Verse count**: 11
- **Themes**: [Day of Judgment](../themes/day-of-judgment.md)
- **Named entities mentioned**: [Allah](../entities/divine_allah.md), [Ar-Rahim (the Most Merciful)](../entities/divine_ar-rahim.md), [Ar-Rahman (the Most Gracious)](../entities/divine_ar-rahman.md), [Rabb (the Lord)](../entities/divine_rabb.md)

- **Revelation order (chronological)**: #14 of 114
- **Revelation phase**: [Early Meccan](../timeline/phase_early-mecca.md) (610-615 CE, Year 1-5 of Prophethood)

## Verses

### Verse 100:0
**Arabic**: بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

**English**: In the name of God, the Gracious, the Merciful.

### Verse 100:1
**Arabic**: وَٱلْعَٰدِيَٰتِ ضَبْحًۭا

**English**: By the racers panting.

### Verse 100:2
**Arabic**: فَٱلْمُورِيَٰتِ قَدْحًۭا

**English**: Igniting sparks.

### Verse 100:3
**Arabic**: فَٱلْمُغِيرَٰتِ صُبْحًۭا

**English**: Raiding at dawn.

### Verse 100:4
**Arabic**: فَأَثَرْنَ بِهِۦ نَقْعًۭا

**English**: Raising clouds of dust.

### Verse 100:5
**Arabic**: فَوَسَطْنَ بِهِۦ جَمْعًا

**English**: Storming into the midst.

### Verse 100:6
**Arabic**: إِنَّ ٱلْإِنسَٰنَ لِرَبِّهِۦ لَكَنُودٌۭ

**English**: Indeed, the human being is ungrateful to his Lord.

### Verse 100:7
**Arabic**: وَإِنَّهُۥ عَلَىٰ ذَٰلِكَ لَشَهِيدٌۭ

**English**: And he bears witness to that.

### Verse 100:8
**Arabic**: وَإِنَّهُۥ لِحُبِّ ٱلْخَيْرِ لَشَدِيدٌ

**English**: And he is fierce in his love of wealth.

### Verse 100:9
**Arabic**: ۞ أَفَلَا يَعْلَمُ إِذَا بُعْثِرَ مَا فِى ٱلْقُبُورِ

**English**: Does he not know? When the contents of the graves are scattered around.

### Verse 100:10
**Arabic**: وَحُصِّلَ مَا فِى ٱلصُّدُورِ

**English**: And the contents of the hearts are obtained.

### Verse 100:11
**Arabic**: إِنَّ رَبَّهُم بِهِمْ يَوْمَئِذٍۢ لَّخَبِيرٌۢ

**English**: Their Lord, on that Day, is fully informed of them.
