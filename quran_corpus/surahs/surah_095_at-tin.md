# Surah 95: At-Tin (التين)

- **Mushaf number**: 95
- **Place of revelation**: Mecca (Makkiyah)
- **Verse count**: 8
- **Themes**: [Faith and Monotheism](../themes/faith-monotheism.md)
- **Named entities mentioned**: [Allah](../entities/divine_allah.md), [Ar-Rahim (the Most Merciful)](../entities/divine_ar-rahim.md), [Ar-Rahman (the Most Gracious)](../entities/divine_ar-rahman.md), [Mount Sinai (Tur)](../entities/place_sinai.md)

## Verses

### Verse 95:0
**Arabic**: بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

**English**: In the name of God, the Gracious, the Merciful.

### Verse 95:1
**Arabic**: وَٱلتِّينِ وَٱلزَّيْتُونِ

**English**: By the fig and the olive.

### Verse 95:2
**Arabic**: وَطُورِ سِينِينَ

**English**: And Mount Sinai.

### Verse 95:3
**Arabic**: وَهَٰذَا ٱلْبَلَدِ ٱلْأَمِينِ

**English**: And this safe land.

### Verse 95:4
**Arabic**: لَقَدْ خَلَقْنَا ٱلْإِنسَٰنَ فِىٓ أَحْسَنِ تَقْوِيمٍۢ

**English**: We created man in the best design.

### Verse 95:5
**Arabic**: ثُمَّ رَدَدْنَٰهُ أَسْفَلَ سَٰفِلِينَ

**English**: Then reduced him to the lowest of the low.

### Verse 95:6
**Arabic**: إِلَّا ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوا۟ وَعَمِلُوا۟ ٱلصَّٰلِحَٰتِ فَلَهُمْ أَجْرٌ غَيْرُ مَمْنُونٍۢ

**English**: Except those who believe and do righteous deeds; for them is a reward without end.

### Verse 95:7
**Arabic**: فَمَا يُكَذِّبُكَ بَعْدُ بِٱلدِّينِ

**English**: So why do you still reject the religion?

### Verse 95:8
**Arabic**: أَلَيْسَ ٱللَّهُ بِأَحْكَمِ ٱلْحَٰكِمِينَ

**English**: Is God not the Wisest of the wise?
