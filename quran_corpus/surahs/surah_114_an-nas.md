# Surah 114: An-Nas (الناس)

- **Mushaf number**: 114
- **Place of revelation**: Mecca (Makkiyah)
- **Verse count**: 6
- **Themes**: [Worship and Devotion](../themes/worship.md)
- **Named entities mentioned**: [Allah](../entities/divine_allah.md), [Ar-Rahim (the Most Merciful)](../entities/divine_ar-rahim.md), [Ar-Rahman (the Most Gracious)](../entities/divine_ar-rahman.md), [Rabb (the Lord)](../entities/divine_rabb.md)

- **Revelation order (chronological)**: #21 of 114
- **Revelation phase**: [Early Meccan](../timeline/phase_early-mecca.md) (610-615 CE, Year 1-5 of Prophethood)

## Verses

### Verse 114:0
**Arabic**: بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

**English**: In the name of God, the Gracious, the Merciful.

### Verse 114:1
**Arabic**: قُلْ أَعُوذُ بِرَبِّ ٱلنَّاسِ

**English**: Say, "I seek refuge in the Lord of mankind.

### Verse 114:2
**Arabic**: مَلِكِ ٱلنَّاسِ

**English**: The King of mankind.

### Verse 114:3
**Arabic**: إِلَٰهِ ٱلنَّاسِ

**English**: The God of mankind.

### Verse 114:4
**Arabic**: مِن شَرِّ ٱلْوَسْوَاسِ ٱلْخَنَّاسِ

**English**: From the evil of the sneaky whisperer.

### Verse 114:5
**Arabic**: ٱلَّذِى يُوَسْوِسُ فِى صُدُورِ ٱلنَّاسِ

**English**: Who whispers into the hearts of people.

### Verse 114:6
**Arabic**: مِنَ ٱلْجِنَّةِ وَٱلنَّاسِ

**English**: From among jinn and among people."
