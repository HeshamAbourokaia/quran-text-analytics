# Surah 75: Al-Qiyamah (القيامة)

- **Mushaf number**: 75
- **Place of revelation**: Mecca (Makkiyah)
- **Verse count**: 40
- **Themes**: [Day of Judgment](../themes/day-of-judgment.md)
- **Named entities mentioned**: [Allah](../entities/divine_allah.md), [Ar-Rahim (the Most Merciful)](../entities/divine_ar-rahim.md), [Ar-Rahman (the Most Gracious)](../entities/divine_ar-rahman.md), [Rabb (the Lord)](../entities/divine_rabb.md)

## Verses

### Verse 75:0
**Arabic**: بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

**English**: In the name of God, the Gracious, the Merciful.

### Verse 75:1
**Arabic**: لَآ أُقْسِمُ بِيَوْمِ ٱلْقِيَٰمَةِ

**English**: I swear by the Day of Resurrection.

### Verse 75:2
**Arabic**: وَلَآ أُقْسِمُ بِٱلنَّفْسِ ٱللَّوَّامَةِ

**English**: And I swear by the blaming soul.

### Verse 75:3
**Arabic**: أَيَحْسَبُ ٱلْإِنسَٰنُ أَلَّن نَّجْمَعَ عِظَامَهُۥ

**English**: Does man think that We will not reassemble his bones?

### Verse 75:4
**Arabic**: بَلَىٰ قَٰدِرِينَ عَلَىٰٓ أَن نُّسَوِّىَ بَنَانَهُۥ

**English**: Yes indeed; We are Able to reconstruct his fingertips.

### Verse 75:5
**Arabic**: بَلْ يُرِيدُ ٱلْإِنسَٰنُ لِيَفْجُرَ أَمَامَهُۥ

**English**: But man wants to deny what is ahead of him.

### Verse 75:6
**Arabic**: يَسْـَٔلُ أَيَّانَ يَوْمُ ٱلْقِيَٰمَةِ

**English**: He asks, "When is the Day of Resurrection?"

### Verse 75:7
**Arabic**: فَإِذَا بَرِقَ ٱلْبَصَرُ

**English**: When vision is dazzled.

### Verse 75:8
**Arabic**: وَخَسَفَ ٱلْقَمَرُ

**English**: And the moon is eclipsed.

### Verse 75:9
**Arabic**: وَجُمِعَ ٱلشَّمْسُ وَٱلْقَمَرُ

**English**: And the sun and the moon are joined together.

### Verse 75:10
**Arabic**: يَقُولُ ٱلْإِنسَٰنُ يَوْمَئِذٍ أَيْنَ ٱلْمَفَرُّ

**English**: On that Day, man will say, "Where is the escape?"

### Verse 75:11
**Arabic**: كَلَّا لَا وَزَرَ

**English**: No indeed! There is no refuge.

### Verse 75:12
**Arabic**: إِلَىٰ رَبِّكَ يَوْمَئِذٍ ٱلْمُسْتَقَرُّ

**English**: To your Lord on that Day is the settlement.

### Verse 75:13
**Arabic**: يُنَبَّؤُا۟ ٱلْإِنسَٰنُ يَوْمَئِذٍۭ بِمَا قَدَّمَ وَأَخَّرَ

**English**: On that Day man will be informed of everything he put forward, and everything he left behind.

### Verse 75:14
**Arabic**: بَلِ ٱلْإِنسَٰنُ عَلَىٰ نَفْسِهِۦ بَصِيرَةٌۭ

**English**: And man will be evidence against himself.

### Verse 75:15
**Arabic**: وَلَوْ أَلْقَىٰ مَعَاذِيرَهُۥ

**English**: Even as he presents his excuses.

### Verse 75:16
**Arabic**: لَا تُحَرِّكْ بِهِۦ لِسَانَكَ لِتَعْجَلَ بِهِۦٓ

**English**: Do not wag your tongue with it, to hurry on with it.

### Verse 75:17
**Arabic**: إِنَّ عَلَيْنَا جَمْعَهُۥ وَقُرْءَانَهُۥ

**English**: Upon Us is its collection and its recitation.

### Verse 75:18
**Arabic**: فَإِذَا قَرَأْنَٰهُ فَٱتَّبِعْ قُرْءَانَهُۥ

**English**: Then, when We have recited it, follow its recitation.

### Verse 75:19
**Arabic**: ثُمَّ إِنَّ عَلَيْنَا بَيَانَهُۥ

**English**: Then upon Us is its explanation.

### Verse 75:20
**Arabic**: كَلَّا بَلْ تُحِبُّونَ ٱلْعَاجِلَةَ

**English**: Alas, you love the fleeting life.

### Verse 75:21
**Arabic**: وَتَذَرُونَ ٱلْءَاخِرَةَ

**English**: And you disregard the Hereafter.

### Verse 75:22
**Arabic**: وُجُوهٌۭ يَوْمَئِذٍۢ نَّاضِرَةٌ

**English**: Faces on that Day will be radiant.

### Verse 75:23
**Arabic**: إِلَىٰ رَبِّهَا نَاظِرَةٌۭ

**English**: Looking towards their Lord.

### Verse 75:24
**Arabic**: وَوُجُوهٌۭ يَوْمَئِذٍۭ بَاسِرَةٌۭ

**English**: And faces on that Day will be gloomy.

### Verse 75:25
**Arabic**: تَظُنُّ أَن يُفْعَلَ بِهَا فَاقِرَةٌۭ

**English**: Realizing that a back-breaker has befallen them.

### Verse 75:26
**Arabic**: كَلَّآ إِذَا بَلَغَتِ ٱلتَّرَاقِىَ

**English**: Indeed, when it has reached the breast-bones.

### Verse 75:27
**Arabic**: وَقِيلَ مَنْ ۜ رَاقٍۢ

**English**: And it is said, "Who is the healer?"

### Verse 75:28
**Arabic**: وَظَنَّ أَنَّهُ ٱلْفِرَاقُ

**English**: And He realizes that it is the parting.

### Verse 75:29
**Arabic**: وَٱلْتَفَّتِ ٱلسَّاقُ بِٱلسَّاقِ

**English**: And leg is entwined with leg.

### Verse 75:30
**Arabic**: إِلَىٰ رَبِّكَ يَوْمَئِذٍ ٱلْمَسَاقُ

**English**: To your Lord on that Day is the drive.

### Verse 75:31
**Arabic**: فَلَا صَدَّقَ وَلَا صَلَّىٰ

**English**: He neither believed nor prayed.

### Verse 75:32
**Arabic**: وَلَٰكِن كَذَّبَ وَتَوَلَّىٰ

**English**: But he denied and turned away.

### Verse 75:33
**Arabic**: ثُمَّ ذَهَبَ إِلَىٰٓ أَهْلِهِۦ يَتَمَطَّىٰٓ

**English**: Then he went to his family, full of pride.

### Verse 75:34
**Arabic**: أَوْلَىٰ لَكَ فَأَوْلَىٰ

**English**: Woe to you; and woe.

### Verse 75:35
**Arabic**: ثُمَّ أَوْلَىٰ لَكَ فَأَوْلَىٰٓ

**English**: Then again: Woe to you; and woe.

### Verse 75:36
**Arabic**: أَيَحْسَبُ ٱلْإِنسَٰنُ أَن يُتْرَكَ سُدًى

**English**: Does man think that he will be left without purpose?

### Verse 75:37
**Arabic**: أَلَمْ يَكُ نُطْفَةًۭ مِّن مَّنِىٍّۢ يُمْنَىٰ

**English**: Was he not a drop of ejaculated semen?

### Verse 75:38
**Arabic**: ثُمَّ كَانَ عَلَقَةًۭ فَخَلَقَ فَسَوَّىٰ

**English**: Then he became a clot. And He created and proportioned?

### Verse 75:39
**Arabic**: فَجَعَلَ مِنْهُ ٱلزَّوْجَيْنِ ٱلذَّكَرَ وَٱلْأُنثَىٰٓ

**English**: And made of him the two sexes, the male and the female?

### Verse 75:40
**Arabic**: أَلَيْسَ ذَٰلِكَ بِقَٰدِرٍ عَلَىٰٓ أَن يُحْۦِىَ ٱلْمَوْتَىٰ

**English**: Is He not Able to revive the dead?
