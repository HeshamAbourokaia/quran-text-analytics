# Surah 1: Al-Fatiha (الفاتحة)

- **Mushaf number**: 1
- **Place of revelation**: Mecca (Makkiyah)
- **Verse count**: 7
- **Themes**: [Faith and Monotheism](../themes/faith-monotheism.md)
- **Named entities mentioned**: [Allah](../entities/divine_allah.md), [Ar-Rahim (the Most Merciful)](../entities/divine_ar-rahim.md), [Ar-Rahman (the Most Gracious)](../entities/divine_ar-rahman.md), [Rabb (the Lord)](../entities/divine_rabb.md)

## Verses

### Verse 1:1
**Arabic**: ﻿بِسْمِ ٱللَّهِ ٱلرَّحْمَٰنِ ٱلرَّحِيمِ

**English**: In the name of God, the Gracious, the Merciful.

### Verse 1:2
**Arabic**: ٱلْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ رَبِّ ٱلْعَٰلَمِينَ

**English**: Praise be to God, Lord of the Worlds.

### Verse 1:3
**Arabic**: ٱلرَّحْمَٰنِ ٱلرَّحِيمِ

**English**: The Most Gracious, the Most Merciful.

### Verse 1:4
**Arabic**: مَٰلِكِ يَوْمِ ٱلدِّينِ

**English**: Master of the Day of Judgment.

### Verse 1:5
**Arabic**: إِيَّاكَ نَعْبُدُ وَإِيَّاكَ نَسْتَعِينُ

**English**: It is You we worship, and upon You we call for help.

### Verse 1:6
**Arabic**: ٱهْدِنَا ٱلصِّرَٰطَ ٱلْمُسْتَقِيمَ

**English**: Guide us to the straight path.

### Verse 1:7
**Arabic**: صِرَٰطَ ٱلَّذِينَ أَنْعَمْتَ عَلَيْهِمْ غَيْرِ ٱلْمَغْضُوبِ عَلَيْهِمْ وَلَا ٱلضَّآلِّينَ

**English**: The path of those You have blessed, not of those against whom there is anger, nor of those who are misguided.
