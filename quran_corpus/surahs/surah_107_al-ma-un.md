# Surah 107: Al-Ma'un (الماعون)

- **Mushaf number**: 107
- **Place of revelation**: Mecca (Makkiyah)
- **Verse count**: 7
- **Themes**: [Ethics and Character](../themes/ethics.md)
- **Named entities mentioned**: [Allah](../entities/divine_allah.md), [Ar-Rahim (the Most Merciful)](../entities/divine_ar-rahim.md), [Ar-Rahman (the Most Gracious)](../entities/divine_ar-rahman.md)

- **Revelation order (chronological)**: #17 of 114
- **Revelation phase**: [Early Meccan](../timeline/phase_early-mecca.md) (610-615 CE, Year 1-5 of Prophethood)

## Verses

### Verse 107:0
**Arabic**: بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

**English**: In the name of God, the Gracious, the Merciful.

### Verse 107:1
**Arabic**: أَرَءَيْتَ ٱلَّذِى يُكَذِّبُ بِٱلدِّينِ

**English**: Have you considered him who denies the religion?

### Verse 107:2
**Arabic**: فَذَٰلِكَ ٱلَّذِى يَدُعُّ ٱلْيَتِيمَ

**English**: It is he who mistreats the orphan.

### Verse 107:3
**Arabic**: وَلَا يَحُضُّ عَلَىٰ طَعَامِ ٱلْمِسْكِينِ

**English**: And does not encourage the feeding of the poor.

### Verse 107:4
**Arabic**: فَوَيْلٌۭ لِّلْمُصَلِّينَ

**English**: So woe to those who pray.

### Verse 107:5
**Arabic**: ٱلَّذِينَ هُمْ عَن صَلَاتِهِمْ سَاهُونَ

**English**: Those who are heedless of their prayers.

### Verse 107:6
**Arabic**: ٱلَّذِينَ هُمْ يُرَآءُونَ

**English**: Those who put on the appearance.

### Verse 107:7
**Arabic**: وَيَمْنَعُونَ ٱلْمَاعُونَ

**English**: And withhold the assistance.
