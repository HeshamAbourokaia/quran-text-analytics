# Surah 85: Al-Buruj (البروج)

- **Mushaf number**: 85
- **Place of revelation**: Mecca (Makkiyah)
- **Verse count**: 22
- **Themes**: [Warning](../themes/warning.md)
- **Named entities mentioned**: [Allah](../entities/divine_allah.md), [Ar-Rahim (the Most Merciful)](../entities/divine_ar-rahim.md), [Ar-Rahman (the Most Gracious)](../entities/divine_ar-rahman.md), [Pharaoh (Firaun)](../entities/figure_firaun.md), [Hud](../entities/prophet_hud.md), [Rabb (the Lord)](../entities/divine_rabb.md)

- **Revelation order (chronological)**: #27 of 114
- **Revelation phase**: [Early Meccan](../timeline/phase_early-mecca.md) (610-615 CE, Year 1-5 of Prophethood)

## Verses

### Verse 85:0
**Arabic**: بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

**English**: In the name of God, the Gracious, the Merciful.

### Verse 85:1
**Arabic**: وَٱلسَّمَآءِ ذَاتِ ٱلْبُرُوجِ

**English**: By the sky with the constellations.

### Verse 85:2
**Arabic**: وَٱلْيَوْمِ ٱلْمَوْعُودِ

**English**: And by the Promised Day.

### Verse 85:3
**Arabic**: وَشَاهِدٍۢ وَمَشْهُودٍۢ

**English**: And by the witness and the witnessed.

### Verse 85:4
**Arabic**: قُتِلَ أَصْحَٰبُ ٱلْأُخْدُودِ

**English**: Destroyed were the People of the Trench.

### Verse 85:5
**Arabic**: ٱلنَّارِ ذَاتِ ٱلْوَقُودِ

**English**: The fire supplied with fuel.

### Verse 85:6
**Arabic**: إِذْ هُمْ عَلَيْهَا قُعُودٌۭ

**English**: While they sat around it.

### Verse 85:7
**Arabic**: وَهُمْ عَلَىٰ مَا يَفْعَلُونَ بِٱلْمُؤْمِنِينَ شُهُودٌۭ

**English**: And were witnessing what they did to the believers.

### Verse 85:8
**Arabic**: وَمَا نَقَمُوا۟ مِنْهُمْ إِلَّآ أَن يُؤْمِنُوا۟ بِٱللَّهِ ٱلْعَزِيزِ ٱلْحَمِيدِ

**English**: They begrudged them only because they believed in God the Almighty, the Praiseworthy.

### Verse 85:9
**Arabic**: ٱلَّذِى لَهُۥ مُلْكُ ٱلسَّمَٰوَٰتِ وَٱلْأَرْضِ ۚ وَٱللَّهُ عَلَىٰ كُلِّ شَىْءٍۢ شَهِيدٌ

**English**: To Whom belongs the sovereignty of the heavens and the earth. God is witness over everything.

### Verse 85:10
**Arabic**: إِنَّ ٱلَّذِينَ فَتَنُوا۟ ٱلْمُؤْمِنِينَ وَٱلْمُؤْمِنَٰتِ ثُمَّ لَمْ يَتُوبُوا۟ فَلَهُمْ عَذَابُ جَهَنَّمَ وَلَهُمْ عَذَابُ ٱلْحَرِيقِ

**English**: Those who tempt the believers, men and women, then do not repent; for them is the punishment of Hell; for them is the punishment of Burning.

### Verse 85:11
**Arabic**: إِنَّ ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوا۟ وَعَمِلُوا۟ ٱلصَّٰلِحَٰتِ لَهُمْ جَنَّٰتٌۭ تَجْرِى مِن تَحْتِهَا ٱلْأَنْهَٰرُ ۚ ذَٰلِكَ ٱلْفَوْزُ ٱلْكَبِيرُ

**English**: Those who believe and do righteous deeds will have Gardens beneath which rivers flow. That is the great triumph.

### Verse 85:12
**Arabic**: إِنَّ بَطْشَ رَبِّكَ لَشَدِيدٌ

**English**: The onslaught of your Lord is severe.

### Verse 85:13
**Arabic**: إِنَّهُۥ هُوَ يُبْدِئُ وَيُعِيدُ

**English**: It is He who begins and repeats.

### Verse 85:14
**Arabic**: وَهُوَ ٱلْغَفُورُ ٱلْوَدُودُ

**English**: And He is the Forgiving, the Loving.

### Verse 85:15
**Arabic**: ذُو ٱلْعَرْشِ ٱلْمَجِيدُ

**English**: Possessor of the Glorious Throne.

### Verse 85:16
**Arabic**: فَعَّالٌۭ لِّمَا يُرِيدُ

**English**: Doer of whatever He wills.

### Verse 85:17
**Arabic**: هَلْ أَتَىٰكَ حَدِيثُ ٱلْجُنُودِ

**English**: Has there come to you the story of the legions?

### Verse 85:18
**Arabic**: فِرْعَوْنَ وَثَمُودَ

**English**: Of Pharaoh and Thamood?

### Verse 85:19
**Arabic**: بَلِ ٱلَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا۟ فِى تَكْذِيبٍۢ

**English**: In fact, those who disbelieve are in denial.

### Verse 85:20
**Arabic**: وَٱللَّهُ مِن وَرَآئِهِم مُّحِيطٌۢ

**English**: And God encloses them from beyond.

### Verse 85:21
**Arabic**: بَلْ هُوَ قُرْءَانٌۭ مَّجِيدٌۭ

**English**: In fact, it is a Glorious Quran.

### Verse 85:22
**Arabic**: فِى لَوْحٍۢ مَّحْفُوظٍۭ

**English**: In a Preserved Tablet.
