# Surah 68: Al-Qalam (القلم)

- **Mushaf number**: 68
- **Place of revelation**: Mecca (Makkiyah)
- **Verse count**: 52
- **Themes**: [Warning](../themes/warning.md), [Patience and Endurance](../themes/patience.md)
- **Named entities mentioned**: [Allah](../entities/divine_allah.md), [Ar-Rahim (the Most Merciful)](../entities/divine_ar-rahim.md), [Ar-Rahman (the Most Gracious)](../entities/divine_ar-rahman.md), [Rabb (the Lord)](../entities/divine_rabb.md)

## Verses

### Verse 68:0
**Arabic**: بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

**English**: In the name of God, the Gracious, the Merciful.

### Verse 68:1
**Arabic**: نٓ ۚ وَٱلْقَلَمِ وَمَا يَسْطُرُونَ

**English**: Noon. By the pen, and by what they inscribe.

### Verse 68:2
**Arabic**: مَآ أَنتَ بِنِعْمَةِ رَبِّكَ بِمَجْنُونٍۢ

**English**: By the grace of your Lord, you are not insane.

### Verse 68:3
**Arabic**: وَإِنَّ لَكَ لَأَجْرًا غَيْرَ مَمْنُونٍۢ

**English**: In fact, you will have a reward that will never end.

### Verse 68:4
**Arabic**: وَإِنَّكَ لَعَلَىٰ خُلُقٍ عَظِيمٍۢ

**English**: And you are of a great moral character.

### Verse 68:5
**Arabic**: فَسَتُبْصِرُ وَيُبْصِرُونَ

**English**: You will see, and they will see.

### Verse 68:6
**Arabic**: بِأَييِّكُمُ ٱلْمَفْتُونُ

**English**: Which of you is the afflicted.

### Verse 68:7
**Arabic**: إِنَّ رَبَّكَ هُوَ أَعْلَمُ بِمَن ضَلَّ عَن سَبِيلِهِۦ وَهُوَ أَعْلَمُ بِٱلْمُهْتَدِينَ

**English**: Your Lord knows best who has strayed from His path, and He knows best the well-guided.

### Verse 68:8
**Arabic**: فَلَا تُطِعِ ٱلْمُكَذِّبِينَ

**English**: So do not obey the deniers.

### Verse 68:9
**Arabic**: وَدُّوا۟ لَوْ تُدْهِنُ فَيُدْهِنُونَ

**English**: They would like you to compromise, so they would compromise.

### Verse 68:10
**Arabic**: وَلَا تُطِعْ كُلَّ حَلَّافٍۢ مَّهِينٍ

**English**: And do not obey any vile swearer.

### Verse 68:11
**Arabic**: هَمَّازٍۢ مَّشَّآءٍۭ بِنَمِيمٍۢ

**English**: Backbiter, spreader of slander.

### Verse 68:12
**Arabic**: مَّنَّاعٍۢ لِّلْخَيْرِ مُعْتَدٍ أَثِيمٍ

**English**: Preventer of good, transgressor, sinner.

### Verse 68:13
**Arabic**: عُتُلٍّۭ بَعْدَ ذَٰلِكَ زَنِيمٍ

**English**: Rude and fake besides.

### Verse 68:14
**Arabic**: أَن كَانَ ذَا مَالٍۢ وَبَنِينَ

**English**: Just because he has money and children.

### Verse 68:15
**Arabic**: إِذَا تُتْلَىٰ عَلَيْهِ ءَايَٰتُنَا قَالَ أَسَٰطِيرُ ٱلْأَوَّلِينَ

**English**: When Our Verses are recited to him, he says, "Myths of the ancients!"

### Verse 68:16
**Arabic**: سَنَسِمُهُۥ عَلَى ٱلْخُرْطُومِ

**English**: We will brand him on the muzzle.

### Verse 68:17
**Arabic**: إِنَّا بَلَوْنَٰهُمْ كَمَا بَلَوْنَآ أَصْحَٰبَ ٱلْجَنَّةِ إِذْ أَقْسَمُوا۟ لَيَصْرِمُنَّهَا مُصْبِحِينَ

**English**: We tested them, as We tested the owners of the garden, when they vowed to harvest it in the morning.

### Verse 68:18
**Arabic**: وَلَا يَسْتَثْنُونَ

**English**: Without any reservation.

### Verse 68:19
**Arabic**: فَطَافَ عَلَيْهَا طَآئِفٌۭ مِّن رَّبِّكَ وَهُمْ نَآئِمُونَ

**English**: But a calamity from your Lord went around it while they slept.

### Verse 68:20
**Arabic**: فَأَصْبَحَتْ كَٱلصَّرِيمِ

**English**: And in the morning it was as if picked.

### Verse 68:21
**Arabic**: فَتَنَادَوْا۟ مُصْبِحِينَ

**English**: In the morning, they called to one another.

### Verse 68:22
**Arabic**: أَنِ ٱغْدُوا۟ عَلَىٰ حَرْثِكُمْ إِن كُنتُمْ صَٰرِمِينَ

**English**: "Go early to your plantation, if you are going to harvest."

### Verse 68:23
**Arabic**: فَٱنطَلَقُوا۟ وَهُمْ يَتَخَٰفَتُونَ

**English**: So off they went, murmuring to one another.

### Verse 68:24
**Arabic**: أَن لَّا يَدْخُلَنَّهَا ٱلْيَوْمَ عَلَيْكُم مِّسْكِينٌۭ

**English**: "No poor person is to enter it upon you today."

### Verse 68:25
**Arabic**: وَغَدَوْا۟ عَلَىٰ حَرْدٍۢ قَٰدِرِينَ

**English**: And early they went, resolved in intent.

### Verse 68:26
**Arabic**: فَلَمَّا رَأَوْهَا قَالُوٓا۟ إِنَّا لَضَآلُّونَ

**English**: But when they saw it, they said, "We were wrong.

### Verse 68:27
**Arabic**: بَلْ نَحْنُ مَحْرُومُونَ

**English**: We are now deprived."

### Verse 68:28
**Arabic**: قَالَ أَوْسَطُهُمْ أَلَمْ أَقُل لَّكُمْ لَوْلَا تُسَبِّحُونَ

**English**: The most reasonable of them said, "Did I not say to you, 'if only you would glorify?'"

### Verse 68:29
**Arabic**: قَالُوا۟ سُبْحَٰنَ رَبِّنَآ إِنَّا كُنَّا ظَٰلِمِينَ

**English**: They said, "Glory to our Lord-We were indeed in the wrong."

### Verse 68:30
**Arabic**: فَأَقْبَلَ بَعْضُهُمْ عَلَىٰ بَعْضٍۢ يَتَلَٰوَمُونَ

**English**: Then they turned to one another, blaming one another.

### Verse 68:31
**Arabic**: قَالُوا۟ يَٰوَيْلَنَآ إِنَّا كُنَّا طَٰغِينَ

**English**: They said, "Woe to us-we were indeed domineering.

### Verse 68:32
**Arabic**: عَسَىٰ رَبُّنَآ أَن يُبْدِلَنَا خَيْرًۭا مِّنْهَآ إِنَّآ إِلَىٰ رَبِّنَا رَٰغِبُونَ

**English**: Perhaps our Lord will give us a better substitute for it. We are turning to our Lord."

### Verse 68:33
**Arabic**: كَذَٰلِكَ ٱلْعَذَابُ ۖ وَلَعَذَابُ ٱلْءَاخِرَةِ أَكْبَرُ ۚ لَوْ كَانُوا۟ يَعْلَمُونَ

**English**: Such is the punishment; but the punishment of the Hereafter is greater, if they only knew.

### Verse 68:34
**Arabic**: إِنَّ لِلْمُتَّقِينَ عِندَ رَبِّهِمْ جَنَّٰتِ ٱلنَّعِيمِ

**English**: For the righteous are Gardens of Delight with their Lord.

### Verse 68:35
**Arabic**: أَفَنَجْعَلُ ٱلْمُسْلِمِينَ كَٱلْمُجْرِمِينَ

**English**: Shall We treat the Muslims like the villains?

### Verse 68:36
**Arabic**: مَا لَكُمْ كَيْفَ تَحْكُمُونَ

**English**: What is the matter with you? How do you judge?

### Verse 68:37
**Arabic**: أَمْ لَكُمْ كِتَٰبٌۭ فِيهِ تَدْرُسُونَ

**English**: Or do you have a scripture in which you study.

### Verse 68:38
**Arabic**: إِنَّ لَكُمْ فِيهِ لَمَا تَخَيَّرُونَ

**English**: Wherein there is whatever you choose?

### Verse 68:39
**Arabic**: أَمْ لَكُمْ أَيْمَٰنٌ عَلَيْنَا بَٰلِغَةٌ إِلَىٰ يَوْمِ ٱلْقِيَٰمَةِ ۙ إِنَّ لَكُمْ لَمَا تَحْكُمُونَ

**English**: Or do you have oaths from Us, binding until the Day of Resurrection, that you will have whatever you demand?

### Verse 68:40
**Arabic**: سَلْهُمْ أَيُّهُم بِذَٰلِكَ زَعِيمٌ

**English**: Ask them, which of them will guarantee that.

### Verse 68:41
**Arabic**: أَمْ لَهُمْ شُرَكَآءُ فَلْيَأْتُوا۟ بِشُرَكَآئِهِمْ إِن كَانُوا۟ صَٰدِقِينَ

**English**: Or do they have partners? Then let them produce their partners, if they are truthful.

### Verse 68:42
**Arabic**: يَوْمَ يُكْشَفُ عَن سَاقٍۢ وَيُدْعَوْنَ إِلَى ٱلسُّجُودِ فَلَا يَسْتَطِيعُونَ

**English**: On the Day when the Shin will be exposed, and they will be called to bow down, but they will be unable.

### Verse 68:43
**Arabic**: خَٰشِعَةً أَبْصَٰرُهُمْ تَرْهَقُهُمْ ذِلَّةٌۭ ۖ وَقَدْ كَانُوا۟ يُدْعَوْنَ إِلَى ٱلسُّجُودِ وَهُمْ سَٰلِمُونَ

**English**: Their eyes subdued, shame will cover them. They were invited to bow down when they were sound.

### Verse 68:44
**Arabic**: فَذَرْنِى وَمَن يُكَذِّبُ بِهَٰذَا ٱلْحَدِيثِ ۖ سَنَسْتَدْرِجُهُم مِّنْ حَيْثُ لَا يَعْلَمُونَ

**English**: So leave Me to those who reject this discourse; We will proceed against them gradually, from where they do not know.

### Verse 68:45
**Arabic**: وَأُمْلِى لَهُمْ ۚ إِنَّ كَيْدِى مَتِينٌ

**English**: And I will give them respite. My plan is firm.

### Verse 68:46
**Arabic**: أَمْ تَسْـَٔلُهُمْ أَجْرًۭا فَهُم مِّن مَّغْرَمٍۢ مُّثْقَلُونَ

**English**: Or do you ask them for a fee, so they are burdened with debt?

### Verse 68:47
**Arabic**: أَمْ عِندَهُمُ ٱلْغَيْبُ فَهُمْ يَكْتُبُونَ

**English**: Or do they know the future, and so they write it down?

### Verse 68:48
**Arabic**: فَٱصْبِرْ لِحُكْمِ رَبِّكَ وَلَا تَكُن كَصَاحِبِ ٱلْحُوتِ إِذْ نَادَىٰ وَهُوَ مَكْظُومٌۭ

**English**: So wait patiently for the Decision of your Lord, and do not be like the Fellow of the Fish who cried out in despair.

### Verse 68:49
**Arabic**: لَّوْلَآ أَن تَدَٰرَكَهُۥ نِعْمَةٌۭ مِّن رَّبِّهِۦ لَنُبِذَ بِٱلْعَرَآءِ وَهُوَ مَذْمُومٌۭ

**English**: Were it not for his Lord's favor that reached him, he would have been thrown into the wilderness, fully despised.

### Verse 68:50
**Arabic**: فَٱجْتَبَٰهُ رَبُّهُۥ فَجَعَلَهُۥ مِنَ ٱلصَّٰلِحِينَ

**English**: But his Lord chose him, and made him one of the righteous.

### Verse 68:51
**Arabic**: وَإِن يَكَادُ ٱلَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا۟ لَيُزْلِقُونَكَ بِأَبْصَٰرِهِمْ لَمَّا سَمِعُوا۟ ٱلذِّكْرَ وَيَقُولُونَ إِنَّهُۥ لَمَجْنُونٌۭ

**English**: Those who disbelieve almost stab you with their glances when they hear the message, and say, "He is crazy!"

### Verse 68:52
**Arabic**: وَمَا هُوَ إِلَّا ذِكْرٌۭ لِّلْعَٰلَمِينَ

**English**: But it is no less than a reminder to all the Worlds.
