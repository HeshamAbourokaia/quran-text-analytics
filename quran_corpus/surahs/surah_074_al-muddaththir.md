# Surah 74: Al-Muddaththir (المدثر)

- **Mushaf number**: 74
- **Place of revelation**: Mecca (Makkiyah)
- **Verse count**: 56
- **Themes**: [Warning](../themes/warning.md)
- **Named entities mentioned**: [Allah](../entities/divine_allah.md), [Ar-Rahim (the Most Merciful)](../entities/divine_ar-rahim.md), [Ar-Rahman (the Most Gracious)](../entities/divine_ar-rahman.md), [Hud](../entities/prophet_hud.md), [Rabb (the Lord)](../entities/divine_rabb.md)

## Verses

### Verse 74:0
**Arabic**: بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

**English**: In the name of God, the Gracious, the Merciful.

### Verse 74:1
**Arabic**: يَٰٓأَيُّهَا ٱلْمُدَّثِّرُ

**English**: O you Enrobed one.

### Verse 74:2
**Arabic**: قُمْ فَأَنذِرْ

**English**: Arise and warn.

### Verse 74:3
**Arabic**: وَرَبَّكَ فَكَبِّرْ

**English**: And magnify your Lord.

### Verse 74:4
**Arabic**: وَثِيَابَكَ فَطَهِّرْ

**English**: And purify your clothes.

### Verse 74:5
**Arabic**: وَٱلرُّجْزَ فَٱهْجُرْ

**English**: And abandon abominations.

### Verse 74:6
**Arabic**: وَلَا تَمْنُن تَسْتَكْثِرُ

**English**: And show no favor seeking gain.

### Verse 74:7
**Arabic**: وَلِرَبِّكَ فَٱصْبِرْ

**English**: And be constant for your Lord.

### Verse 74:8
**Arabic**: فَإِذَا نُقِرَ فِى ٱلنَّاقُورِ

**English**: When the Trumpet is blown.

### Verse 74:9
**Arabic**: فَذَٰلِكَ يَوْمَئِذٍۢ يَوْمٌ عَسِيرٌ

**English**: That Day will be a difficult day.

### Verse 74:10
**Arabic**: عَلَى ٱلْكَٰفِرِينَ غَيْرُ يَسِيرٍۢ

**English**: For the disbelievers-not easy.

### Verse 74:11
**Arabic**: ذَرْنِى وَمَنْ خَلَقْتُ وَحِيدًۭا

**English**: Leave Me to him whom I created alone.

### Verse 74:12
**Arabic**: وَجَعَلْتُ لَهُۥ مَالًۭا مَّمْدُودًۭا

**English**: And gave him vast wealth.

### Verse 74:13
**Arabic**: وَبَنِينَ شُهُودًۭا

**English**: And children as witnesses.

### Verse 74:14
**Arabic**: وَمَهَّدتُّ لَهُۥ تَمْهِيدًۭا

**English**: And smoothed things for him.

### Verse 74:15
**Arabic**: ثُمَّ يَطْمَعُ أَنْ أَزِيدَ

**English**: Then he wants Me to add yet more!

### Verse 74:16
**Arabic**: كَلَّآ ۖ إِنَّهُۥ كَانَ لِءَايَٰتِنَا عَنِيدًۭا

**English**: By no means! He was stubborn towards Our revelations.

### Verse 74:17
**Arabic**: سَأُرْهِقُهُۥ صَعُودًا

**English**: I will exhaust him increasingly.

### Verse 74:18
**Arabic**: إِنَّهُۥ فَكَّرَ وَقَدَّرَ

**English**: He thought and analyzed.

### Verse 74:19
**Arabic**: فَقُتِلَ كَيْفَ قَدَّرَ

**English**: May he perish, how he analyzed.

### Verse 74:20
**Arabic**: ثُمَّ قُتِلَ كَيْفَ قَدَّرَ

**English**: Again: may he perish, how he analyzed.

### Verse 74:21
**Arabic**: ثُمَّ نَظَرَ

**English**: Then he looked.

### Verse 74:22
**Arabic**: ثُمَّ عَبَسَ وَبَسَرَ

**English**: Then he frowned and whined.

### Verse 74:23
**Arabic**: ثُمَّ أَدْبَرَ وَٱسْتَكْبَرَ

**English**: Then he turned back and was proud.

### Verse 74:24
**Arabic**: فَقَالَ إِنْ هَٰذَآ إِلَّا سِحْرٌۭ يُؤْثَرُ

**English**: And said, "This is nothing but magic from the past.

### Verse 74:25
**Arabic**: إِنْ هَٰذَآ إِلَّا قَوْلُ ٱلْبَشَرِ

**English**: This is nothing but the word of a mortal."

### Verse 74:26
**Arabic**: سَأُصْلِيهِ سَقَرَ

**English**: I will roast him in Saqar.

### Verse 74:27
**Arabic**: وَمَآ أَدْرَىٰكَ مَا سَقَرُ

**English**: But what will explain to you what Saqar is?

### Verse 74:28
**Arabic**: لَا تُبْقِى وَلَا تَذَرُ

**English**: It neither leaves, nor spares.

### Verse 74:29
**Arabic**: لَوَّاحَةٌۭ لِّلْبَشَرِ

**English**: It scorches the flesh.

### Verse 74:30
**Arabic**: عَلَيْهَا تِسْعَةَ عَشَرَ

**English**: Over it are Nineteen.

### Verse 74:31
**Arabic**: وَمَا جَعَلْنَآ أَصْحَٰبَ ٱلنَّارِ إِلَّا مَلَٰٓئِكَةًۭ ۙ وَمَا جَعَلْنَا عِدَّتَهُمْ إِلَّا فِتْنَةًۭ لِّلَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا۟ لِيَسْتَيْقِنَ ٱلَّذِينَ أُوتُوا۟ ٱلْكِتَٰبَ وَيَزْدَادَ ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوٓا۟ إِيمَٰنًۭا ۙ وَلَا يَرْتَابَ ٱلَّذِينَ أُوتُوا۟ ٱلْكِتَٰبَ وَٱلْمُؤْمِنُونَ ۙ وَلِيَقُولَ ٱلَّذِينَ فِى قُلُوبِهِم مَّرَضٌۭ وَٱلْكَٰفِرُونَ مَاذَآ أَرَادَ ٱللَّهُ بِهَٰذَا مَثَلًۭا ۚ كَذَٰلِكَ يُضِلُّ ٱللَّهُ مَن يَشَآءُ وَيَهْدِى مَن يَشَآءُ ۚ وَمَا يَعْلَمُ جُنُودَ رَبِّكَ إِلَّا هُوَ ۚ وَمَا هِىَ إِلَّا ذِكْرَىٰ لِلْبَشَرِ

**English**: We have appointed only angels to be wardens of the Fire, and caused their number to be a stumbling block for those who disbelieve; so that those given the Scripture may attain certainty; and those who believe may increase in faith; and those given the Scripture and the believers may not doubt; and those in whose hearts is sickness and the unbelievers may say, "What did God intend by this parable?" Thus God leads astray whom He wills, and guides whom He wills. None knows the soldiers of your Lord except He. This is nothing but a reminder for the mortals.

### Verse 74:32
**Arabic**: كَلَّا وَٱلْقَمَرِ

**English**: Nay! By the moon.

### Verse 74:33
**Arabic**: وَٱلَّيْلِ إِذْ أَدْبَرَ

**English**: And the night as it retreats.

### Verse 74:34
**Arabic**: وَٱلصُّبْحِ إِذَآ أَسْفَرَ

**English**: And the morning as it lights up.

### Verse 74:35
**Arabic**: إِنَّهَا لَإِحْدَى ٱلْكُبَرِ

**English**: It is one of the greatest.

### Verse 74:36
**Arabic**: نَذِيرًۭا لِّلْبَشَرِ

**English**: A warning to the mortals.

### Verse 74:37
**Arabic**: لِمَن شَآءَ مِنكُمْ أَن يَتَقَدَّمَ أَوْ يَتَأَخَّرَ

**English**: To whomever among you wishes to advance, or regress.

### Verse 74:38
**Arabic**: كُلُّ نَفْسٍۭ بِمَا كَسَبَتْ رَهِينَةٌ

**English**: Every soul is hostage to what it has earned.

### Verse 74:39
**Arabic**: إِلَّآ أَصْحَٰبَ ٱلْيَمِينِ

**English**: Except for those on the Right.

### Verse 74:40
**Arabic**: فِى جَنَّٰتٍۢ يَتَسَآءَلُونَ

**English**: In Gardens, inquiring.

### Verse 74:41
**Arabic**: عَنِ ٱلْمُجْرِمِينَ

**English**: About the guilty.

### Verse 74:42
**Arabic**: مَا سَلَكَكُمْ فِى سَقَرَ

**English**: "What drove you into Saqar?"

### Verse 74:43
**Arabic**: قَالُوا۟ لَمْ نَكُ مِنَ ٱلْمُصَلِّينَ

**English**: They will say, "We were not of those who prayed.

### Verse 74:44
**Arabic**: وَلَمْ نَكُ نُطْعِمُ ٱلْمِسْكِينَ

**English**: Nor did we feed the destitute.

### Verse 74:45
**Arabic**: وَكُنَّا نَخُوضُ مَعَ ٱلْخَآئِضِينَ

**English**: And we used to indulge with those who indulge.

### Verse 74:46
**Arabic**: وَكُنَّا نُكَذِّبُ بِيَوْمِ ٱلدِّينِ

**English**: And we used to deny the Day of Judgment.

### Verse 74:47
**Arabic**: حَتَّىٰٓ أَتَىٰنَا ٱلْيَقِينُ

**English**: Until the Inevitable came upon us."

### Verse 74:48
**Arabic**: فَمَا تَنفَعُهُمْ شَفَٰعَةُ ٱلشَّٰفِعِينَ

**English**: But the intercession of intercessors will not help them.

### Verse 74:49
**Arabic**: فَمَا لَهُمْ عَنِ ٱلتَّذْكِرَةِ مُعْرِضِينَ

**English**: Why are they turning away from the Reminder?

### Verse 74:50
**Arabic**: كَأَنَّهُمْ حُمُرٌۭ مُّسْتَنفِرَةٌۭ

**English**: As though they were panicked donkeys.

### Verse 74:51
**Arabic**: فَرَّتْ مِن قَسْوَرَةٍۭ

**English**: Fleeing from a lion?

### Verse 74:52
**Arabic**: بَلْ يُرِيدُ كُلُّ ٱمْرِئٍۢ مِّنْهُمْ أَن يُؤْتَىٰ صُحُفًۭا مُّنَشَّرَةًۭ

**English**: Yet every one of them desires to be given scrolls unrolled.

### Verse 74:53
**Arabic**: كَلَّا ۖ بَل لَّا يَخَافُونَ ٱلْءَاخِرَةَ

**English**: No indeed! But they do not fear the Hereafter.

### Verse 74:54
**Arabic**: كَلَّآ إِنَّهُۥ تَذْكِرَةٌۭ

**English**: Nevertheless, it is a reminder.

### Verse 74:55
**Arabic**: فَمَن شَآءَ ذَكَرَهُۥ

**English**: So whoever wills, shall remember it.

### Verse 74:56
**Arabic**: وَمَا يَذْكُرُونَ إِلَّآ أَن يَشَآءَ ٱللَّهُ ۚ هُوَ أَهْلُ ٱلتَّقْوَىٰ وَأَهْلُ ٱلْمَغْفِرَةِ

**English**: But they will not remember, unless God wills. He is the Source of Righteousness, and the Source of Forgiveness.
