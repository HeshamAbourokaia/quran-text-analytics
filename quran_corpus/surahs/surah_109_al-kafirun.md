# Surah 109: Al-Kafirun (الكافرون)

- **Mushaf number**: 109
- **Place of revelation**: Mecca (Makkiyah)
- **Verse count**: 6
- **Themes**: [Community and Society](../themes/community.md)
- **Named entities mentioned**: [Allah](../entities/divine_allah.md), [Ar-Rahim (the Most Merciful)](../entities/divine_ar-rahim.md), [Ar-Rahman (the Most Gracious)](../entities/divine_ar-rahman.md)

- **Revelation order (chronological)**: #18 of 114
- **Revelation phase**: [Early Meccan](../timeline/phase_early-mecca.md) (610-615 CE, Year 1-5 of Prophethood)

## Verses

### Verse 109:0
**Arabic**: بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

**English**: In the name of God, the Gracious, the Merciful.

### Verse 109:1
**Arabic**: قُلْ يَٰٓأَيُّهَا ٱلْكَٰفِرُونَ

**English**: Say, "O disbelievers.

### Verse 109:2
**Arabic**: لَآ أَعْبُدُ مَا تَعْبُدُونَ

**English**: I do not worship what you worship.

### Verse 109:3
**Arabic**: وَلَآ أَنتُمْ عَٰبِدُونَ مَآ أَعْبُدُ

**English**: Nor do you worship what I worship.

### Verse 109:4
**Arabic**: وَلَآ أَنَا۠ عَابِدٌۭ مَّا عَبَدتُّمْ

**English**: Nor do I serve what you serve.

### Verse 109:5
**Arabic**: وَلَآ أَنتُمْ عَٰبِدُونَ مَآ أَعْبُدُ

**English**: Nor do you serve what I serve.

### Verse 109:6
**Arabic**: لَكُمْ دِينُكُمْ وَلِىَ دِينِ

**English**: You have your way, and I have my way."
