# Surah 113: Al-Falaq (الفلق)

- **Mushaf number**: 113
- **Place of revelation**: Mecca (Makkiyah)
- **Verse count**: 5
- **Themes**: [Worship and Devotion](../themes/worship.md)
- **Named entities mentioned**: [Allah](../entities/divine_allah.md), [Ar-Rahim (the Most Merciful)](../entities/divine_ar-rahim.md), [Ar-Rahman (the Most Gracious)](../entities/divine_ar-rahman.md), [Rabb (the Lord)](../entities/divine_rabb.md)

## Verses

### Verse 113:0
**Arabic**: بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

**English**: In the name of God, the Gracious, the Merciful.

### Verse 113:1
**Arabic**: قُلْ أَعُوذُ بِرَبِّ ٱلْفَلَقِ

**English**: Say, "I take refuge with the Lord of Daybreak.

### Verse 113:2
**Arabic**: مِن شَرِّ مَا خَلَقَ

**English**: From the evil of what He created.

### Verse 113:3
**Arabic**: وَمِن شَرِّ غَاسِقٍ إِذَا وَقَبَ

**English**: And from the evil of the darkness as it gathers.

### Verse 113:4
**Arabic**: وَمِن شَرِّ ٱلنَّفَّٰثَٰتِ فِى ٱلْعُقَدِ

**English**: And from the evil of those who practice sorcery.

### Verse 113:5
**Arabic**: وَمِن شَرِّ حَاسِدٍ إِذَا حَسَدَ

**English**: And from the evil of an envious when he envies."
