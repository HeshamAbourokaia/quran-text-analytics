# Surah 15: Al-Hijr (الحجر)

- **Mushaf number**: 15
- **Place of revelation**: Mecca (Makkiyah)
- **Verse count**: 99
- **Themes**: [Stories of the Prophets](../themes/prophets-stories.md)
- **Named entities mentioned**: [Allah](../entities/divine_allah.md), [Ar-Rahim (the Most Merciful)](../entities/divine_ar-rahim.md), [Ar-Rahman (the Most Gracious)](../entities/divine_ar-rahman.md), [Iblis (Satan)](../entities/figure_iblis.md), [Abraham (Ibrahim)](../entities/prophet_ibrahim.md), [Lot (Lut)](../entities/prophet_lut.md), [Medina](../entities/place_madinah.md), [Madyan](../entities/place_madyan.md), [Rabb (the Lord)](../entities/divine_rabb.md)

## Verses

### Verse 15:0
**Arabic**: بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

**English**: In the name of God, the Gracious, the Merciful.

### Verse 15:1
**Arabic**: الٓر ۚ تِلْكَ ءَايَٰتُ ٱلْكِتَٰبِ وَقُرْءَانٍۢ مُّبِينٍۢ

**English**: Alif, Lam, Ra. These are the Verses of the Book; a Quran that makes things clear.

### Verse 15:2
**Arabic**: رُّبَمَا يَوَدُّ ٱلَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا۟ لَوْ كَانُوا۟ مُسْلِمِينَ

**English**: Perhaps those who disbelieve would like to become Muslims.

### Verse 15:3
**Arabic**: ذَرْهُمْ يَأْكُلُوا۟ وَيَتَمَتَّعُوا۟ وَيُلْهِهِمُ ٱلْأَمَلُ ۖ فَسَوْفَ يَعْلَمُونَ

**English**: Leave them to eat, and enjoy, and be lulled by hope. They will find out.

### Verse 15:4
**Arabic**: وَمَآ أَهْلَكْنَا مِن قَرْيَةٍ إِلَّا وَلَهَا كِتَابٌۭ مَّعْلُومٌۭ

**English**: We have never destroyed a town unless it had a set time.

### Verse 15:5
**Arabic**: مَّا تَسْبِقُ مِنْ أُمَّةٍ أَجَلَهَا وَمَا يَسْتَـْٔخِرُونَ

**English**: No nation can bring its time forward, nor can they delay it.

### Verse 15:6
**Arabic**: وَقَالُوا۟ يَٰٓأَيُّهَا ٱلَّذِى نُزِّلَ عَلَيْهِ ٱلذِّكْرُ إِنَّكَ لَمَجْنُونٌۭ

**English**: And they said, "O you who received the message, you are insane."

### Verse 15:7
**Arabic**: لَّوْ مَا تَأْتِينَا بِٱلْمَلَٰٓئِكَةِ إِن كُنتَ مِنَ ٱلصَّٰدِقِينَ

**English**: Why do you not bring us the angels, if you are truthful?"

### Verse 15:8
**Arabic**: مَا نُنَزِّلُ ٱلْمَلَٰٓئِكَةَ إِلَّا بِٱلْحَقِّ وَمَا كَانُوٓا۟ إِذًۭا مُّنظَرِينَ

**English**: We do not send the angels down except with reason, and they will not be held back.

### Verse 15:9
**Arabic**: إِنَّا نَحْنُ نَزَّلْنَا ٱلذِّكْرَ وَإِنَّا لَهُۥ لَحَٰفِظُونَ

**English**: Surely We revealed the Message, and We will surely preserve it.

### Verse 15:10
**Arabic**: وَلَقَدْ أَرْسَلْنَا مِن قَبْلِكَ فِى شِيَعِ ٱلْأَوَّلِينَ

**English**: We sent others before you, to the former communities.

### Verse 15:11
**Arabic**: وَمَا يَأْتِيهِم مِّن رَّسُولٍ إِلَّا كَانُوا۟ بِهِۦ يَسْتَهْزِءُونَ

**English**: But no messenger came to them, but they ridiculed him.

### Verse 15:12
**Arabic**: كَذَٰلِكَ نَسْلُكُهُۥ فِى قُلُوبِ ٱلْمُجْرِمِينَ

**English**: Thus We slip it into the hearts of the guilty.

### Verse 15:13
**Arabic**: لَا يُؤْمِنُونَ بِهِۦ ۖ وَقَدْ خَلَتْ سُنَّةُ ٱلْأَوَّلِينَ

**English**: They do not believe in it, though the ways of the ancients have passed away.

### Verse 15:14
**Arabic**: وَلَوْ فَتَحْنَا عَلَيْهِم بَابًۭا مِّنَ ٱلسَّمَآءِ فَظَلُّوا۟ فِيهِ يَعْرُجُونَ

**English**: Even if We opened for them a gateway into the sky, and they began to ascend through it.

### Verse 15:15
**Arabic**: لَقَالُوٓا۟ إِنَّمَا سُكِّرَتْ أَبْصَٰرُنَا بَلْ نَحْنُ قَوْمٌۭ مَّسْحُورُونَ

**English**: They would still say, "Our eyes are hallucinating; in fact, we are people bewitched."

### Verse 15:16
**Arabic**: وَلَقَدْ جَعَلْنَا فِى ٱلسَّمَآءِ بُرُوجًۭا وَزَيَّنَّٰهَا لِلنَّٰظِرِينَ

**English**: We placed constellations in the sky, and made them beautiful to the beholders.

### Verse 15:17
**Arabic**: وَحَفِظْنَٰهَا مِن كُلِّ شَيْطَٰنٍۢ رَّجِيمٍ

**English**: And We guarded them from every outcast devil.

### Verse 15:18
**Arabic**: إِلَّا مَنِ ٱسْتَرَقَ ٱلسَّمْعَ فَأَتْبَعَهُۥ شِهَابٌۭ مُّبِينٌۭ

**English**: Except one who steals a hearing, and is followed by a visible projectile.

### Verse 15:19
**Arabic**: وَٱلْأَرْضَ مَدَدْنَٰهَا وَأَلْقَيْنَا فِيهَا رَوَٰسِىَ وَأَنۢبَتْنَا فِيهَا مِن كُلِّ شَىْءٍۢ مَّوْزُونٍۢ

**English**: We spread the earth, and placed stabilizers in it, and in it We grew all things in proper measure.

### Verse 15:20
**Arabic**: وَجَعَلْنَا لَكُمْ فِيهَا مَعَٰيِشَ وَمَن لَّسْتُمْ لَهُۥ بِرَٰزِقِينَ

**English**: And in it We created livelihoods for you, and for those for whom you are not the providers.

### Verse 15:21
**Arabic**: وَإِن مِّن شَىْءٍ إِلَّا عِندَنَا خَزَآئِنُهُۥ وَمَا نُنَزِّلُهُۥٓ إِلَّا بِقَدَرٍۢ مَّعْلُومٍۢ

**English**: There is not a thing but with Us are its stores, and We send it down only in precise measure.

### Verse 15:22
**Arabic**: وَأَرْسَلْنَا ٱلرِّيَٰحَ لَوَٰقِحَ فَأَنزَلْنَا مِنَ ٱلسَّمَآءِ مَآءًۭ فَأَسْقَيْنَٰكُمُوهُ وَمَآ أَنتُمْ لَهُۥ بِخَٰزِنِينَ

**English**: We send the fertilizing winds; and send down water from the sky, and give it to you to drink, and you are not the ones who store it.

### Verse 15:23
**Arabic**: وَإِنَّا لَنَحْنُ نُحْىِۦ وَنُمِيتُ وَنَحْنُ ٱلْوَٰرِثُونَ

**English**: It is We who give life and cause death, and We are the Inheritors.

### Verse 15:24
**Arabic**: وَلَقَدْ عَلِمْنَا ٱلْمُسْتَقْدِمِينَ مِنكُمْ وَلَقَدْ عَلِمْنَا ٱلْمُسْتَـْٔخِرِينَ

**English**: And We know those of you who go forward, and We know those who lag behind.

### Verse 15:25
**Arabic**: وَإِنَّ رَبَّكَ هُوَ يَحْشُرُهُمْ ۚ إِنَّهُۥ حَكِيمٌ عَلِيمٌۭ

**English**: It is your Lord who will gather them together. He is the Wise, the Knowing.

### Verse 15:26
**Arabic**: وَلَقَدْ خَلَقْنَا ٱلْإِنسَٰنَ مِن صَلْصَٰلٍۢ مِّنْ حَمَإٍۢ مَّسْنُونٍۢ

**English**: We created the human being from clay, from molded mud.

### Verse 15:27
**Arabic**: وَٱلْجَآنَّ خَلَقْنَٰهُ مِن قَبْلُ مِن نَّارِ ٱلسَّمُومِ

**English**: And the jinn We created before, from piercing fire.

### Verse 15:28
**Arabic**: وَإِذْ قَالَ رَبُّكَ لِلْمَلَٰٓئِكَةِ إِنِّى خَٰلِقٌۢ بَشَرًۭا مِّن صَلْصَٰلٍۢ مِّنْ حَمَإٍۢ مَّسْنُونٍۢ

**English**: Your Lord said to the angels, "I am creating a human being from clay, from molded mud."

### Verse 15:29
**Arabic**: فَإِذَا سَوَّيْتُهُۥ وَنَفَخْتُ فِيهِ مِن رُّوحِى فَقَعُوا۟ لَهُۥ سَٰجِدِينَ

**English**: "When I have formed him, and breathed into him of My spirit, fall down prostrating before him."

### Verse 15:30
**Arabic**: فَسَجَدَ ٱلْمَلَٰٓئِكَةُ كُلُّهُمْ أَجْمَعُونَ

**English**: So the angels prostrated themselves, all together.

### Verse 15:31
**Arabic**: إِلَّآ إِبْلِيسَ أَبَىٰٓ أَن يَكُونَ مَعَ ٱلسَّٰجِدِينَ

**English**: Except for Satan. He refused to be among those who prostrated themselves.

### Verse 15:32
**Arabic**: قَالَ يَٰٓإِبْلِيسُ مَا لَكَ أَلَّا تَكُونَ مَعَ ٱلسَّٰجِدِينَ

**English**: He said, "O Satan, what kept you from being among those who prostrated themselves?"

### Verse 15:33
**Arabic**: قَالَ لَمْ أَكُن لِّأَسْجُدَ لِبَشَرٍ خَلَقْتَهُۥ مِن صَلْصَٰلٍۢ مِّنْ حَمَإٍۢ مَّسْنُونٍۢ

**English**: He said, "I am not about to prostrate myself before a human being, whom You created from clay, from molded mud."

### Verse 15:34
**Arabic**: قَالَ فَٱخْرُجْ مِنْهَا فَإِنَّكَ رَجِيمٌۭ

**English**: He said, "Then get out of here, for you are an outcast".

### Verse 15:35
**Arabic**: وَإِنَّ عَلَيْكَ ٱللَّعْنَةَ إِلَىٰ يَوْمِ ٱلدِّينِ

**English**: "And the curse will be upon you until the Day of Judgment."

### Verse 15:36
**Arabic**: قَالَ رَبِّ فَأَنظِرْنِىٓ إِلَىٰ يَوْمِ يُبْعَثُونَ

**English**: He said, "My Lord, reprieve me until the Day they are resurrected."

### Verse 15:37
**Arabic**: قَالَ فَإِنَّكَ مِنَ ٱلْمُنظَرِينَ

**English**: He said, "You are of those reprieved."

### Verse 15:38
**Arabic**: إِلَىٰ يَوْمِ ٱلْوَقْتِ ٱلْمَعْلُومِ

**English**: "Until the Day of the time appointed."

### Verse 15:39
**Arabic**: قَالَ رَبِّ بِمَآ أَغْوَيْتَنِى لَأُزَيِّنَنَّ لَهُمْ فِى ٱلْأَرْضِ وَلَأُغْوِيَنَّهُمْ أَجْمَعِينَ

**English**: He said, "My Lord, since You have lured me away, I will glamorize for them on earth, and I will lure them all away."

### Verse 15:40
**Arabic**: إِلَّا عِبَادَكَ مِنْهُمُ ٱلْمُخْلَصِينَ

**English**: "Except for Your sincere servants among them."

### Verse 15:41
**Arabic**: قَالَ هَٰذَا صِرَٰطٌ عَلَىَّ مُسْتَقِيمٌ

**English**: He said, "This is a right way with Me."

### Verse 15:42
**Arabic**: إِنَّ عِبَادِى لَيْسَ لَكَ عَلَيْهِمْ سُلْطَٰنٌ إِلَّا مَنِ ٱتَّبَعَكَ مِنَ ٱلْغَاوِينَ

**English**: "Over My servants you have no authority, except for the sinners who follow you."

### Verse 15:43
**Arabic**: وَإِنَّ جَهَنَّمَ لَمَوْعِدُهُمْ أَجْمَعِينَ

**English**: And Hell is the meeting-place for them all.

### Verse 15:44
**Arabic**: لَهَا سَبْعَةُ أَبْوَٰبٍۢ لِّكُلِّ بَابٍۢ مِّنْهُمْ جُزْءٌۭ مَّقْسُومٌ

**English**: "It has seven doors; for each door is an assigned class."

### Verse 15:45
**Arabic**: إِنَّ ٱلْمُتَّقِينَ فِى جَنَّٰتٍۢ وَعُيُونٍ

**English**: But the righteous will be in gardens with springs.

### Verse 15:46
**Arabic**: ٱدْخُلُوهَا بِسَلَٰمٍ ءَامِنِينَ

**English**: "Enter it in peace and security."

### Verse 15:47
**Arabic**: وَنَزَعْنَا مَا فِى صُدُورِهِم مِّنْ غِلٍّ إِخْوَٰنًا عَلَىٰ سُرُرٍۢ مُّتَقَٰبِلِينَ

**English**: And We will remove all ill-feelings from their hearts-brothers and sisters, on couches facing one another.

### Verse 15:48
**Arabic**: لَا يَمَسُّهُمْ فِيهَا نَصَبٌۭ وَمَا هُم مِّنْهَا بِمُخْرَجِينَ

**English**: No fatigue will ever touch them therein, nor will they be asked to leave it.

### Verse 15:49
**Arabic**: ۞ نَبِّئْ عِبَادِىٓ أَنِّىٓ أَنَا ٱلْغَفُورُ ٱلرَّحِيمُ

**English**: Inform My servants that I am the Forgiver, the Merciful.

### Verse 15:50
**Arabic**: وَأَنَّ عَذَابِى هُوَ ٱلْعَذَابُ ٱلْأَلِيمُ

**English**: And that My punishment is the painful punishment.

### Verse 15:51
**Arabic**: وَنَبِّئْهُمْ عَن ضَيْفِ إِبْرَٰهِيمَ

**English**: And inform them of the guests of Abraham.

### Verse 15:52
**Arabic**: إِذْ دَخَلُوا۟ عَلَيْهِ فَقَالُوا۟ سَلَٰمًۭا قَالَ إِنَّا مِنكُمْ وَجِلُونَ

**English**: When they entered upon him, and said, "Peace." He said, "We are wary of you."

### Verse 15:53
**Arabic**: قَالُوا۟ لَا تَوْجَلْ إِنَّا نُبَشِّرُكَ بِغُلَٰمٍ عَلِيمٍۢ

**English**: They said, "Do not fear; we bring you good news of a boy endowed with knowledge."

### Verse 15:54
**Arabic**: قَالَ أَبَشَّرْتُمُونِى عَلَىٰٓ أَن مَّسَّنِىَ ٱلْكِبَرُ فَبِمَ تُبَشِّرُونَ

**English**: He said, "Do you bring me good news, when old age has overtaken me? What good news do you bring?"

### Verse 15:55
**Arabic**: قَالُوا۟ بَشَّرْنَٰكَ بِٱلْحَقِّ فَلَا تَكُن مِّنَ ٱلْقَٰنِطِينَ

**English**: They said, "We bring you good news in truth, so do not despair."

### Verse 15:56
**Arabic**: قَالَ وَمَن يَقْنَطُ مِن رَّحْمَةِ رَبِّهِۦٓ إِلَّا ٱلضَّآلُّونَ

**English**: He said, "And who despairs of his Lord's mercy but the lost?"

### Verse 15:57
**Arabic**: قَالَ فَمَا خَطْبُكُمْ أَيُّهَا ٱلْمُرْسَلُونَ

**English**: He said, "So what is your business, O envoys?"

### Verse 15:58
**Arabic**: قَالُوٓا۟ إِنَّآ أُرْسِلْنَآ إِلَىٰ قَوْمٍۢ مُّجْرِمِينَ

**English**: They said, "We were sent to a sinful people."

### Verse 15:59
**Arabic**: إِلَّآ ءَالَ لُوطٍ إِنَّا لَمُنَجُّوهُمْ أَجْمَعِينَ

**English**: "Except for the family of Lot; we will save them all."

### Verse 15:60
**Arabic**: إِلَّا ٱمْرَأَتَهُۥ قَدَّرْنَآ ۙ إِنَّهَا لَمِنَ ٱلْغَٰبِرِينَ

**English**: "Except for his wife." We have determined that she will be of those who lag behind.

### Verse 15:61
**Arabic**: فَلَمَّا جَآءَ ءَالَ لُوطٍ ٱلْمُرْسَلُونَ

**English**: And when the envoys came to the family of Lot.

### Verse 15:62
**Arabic**: قَالَ إِنَّكُمْ قَوْمٌۭ مُّنكَرُونَ

**English**: He said, "You are a people unknown to me."

### Verse 15:63
**Arabic**: قَالُوا۟ بَلْ جِئْنَٰكَ بِمَا كَانُوا۟ فِيهِ يَمْتَرُونَ

**English**: They said, "We bring you what they have doubts about."

### Verse 15:64
**Arabic**: وَأَتَيْنَٰكَ بِٱلْحَقِّ وَإِنَّا لَصَٰدِقُونَ

**English**: "We bring you the truth, and we are truthful."

### Verse 15:65
**Arabic**: فَأَسْرِ بِأَهْلِكَ بِقِطْعٍۢ مِّنَ ٱلَّيْلِ وَٱتَّبِعْ أَدْبَٰرَهُمْ وَلَا يَلْتَفِتْ مِنكُمْ أَحَدٌۭ وَٱمْضُوا۟ حَيْثُ تُؤْمَرُونَ

**English**: "Travel with your family at the dead of the night, and follow up behind them, and let none of you look back, and proceed as commanded."

### Verse 15:66
**Arabic**: وَقَضَيْنَآ إِلَيْهِ ذَٰلِكَ ٱلْأَمْرَ أَنَّ دَابِرَ هَٰٓؤُلَآءِ مَقْطُوعٌۭ مُّصْبِحِينَ

**English**: And We informed him of Our decree: the last remnant of these will be uprooted by early morning.

### Verse 15:67
**Arabic**: وَجَآءَ أَهْلُ ٱلْمَدِينَةِ يَسْتَبْشِرُونَ

**English**: And the people of the town came joyfully.

### Verse 15:68
**Arabic**: قَالَ إِنَّ هَٰٓؤُلَآءِ ضَيْفِى فَلَا تَفْضَحُونِ

**English**: He said, "These are my guests, so do not embarrass me."

### Verse 15:69
**Arabic**: وَٱتَّقُوا۟ ٱللَّهَ وَلَا تُخْزُونِ

**English**: "And fear God, and do not disgrace me."

### Verse 15:70
**Arabic**: قَالُوٓا۟ أَوَلَمْ نَنْهَكَ عَنِ ٱلْعَٰلَمِينَ

**English**: They said, "Did we not forbid you from strangers?"

### Verse 15:71
**Arabic**: قَالَ هَٰٓؤُلَآءِ بَنَاتِىٓ إِن كُنتُمْ فَٰعِلِينَ
