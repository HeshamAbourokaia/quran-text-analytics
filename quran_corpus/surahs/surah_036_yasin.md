# Surah 36: Yasin (يس)

- **Mushaf number**: 36
- **Place of revelation**: Mecca (Makkiyah)
- **Verse count**: 83
- **Themes**: [Day of Judgment](../themes/day-of-judgment.md)
- **Named entities mentioned**: [Adam](../entities/prophet_adam.md), [Allah](../entities/divine_allah.md), [Ar-Rahim (the Most Merciful)](../entities/divine_ar-rahim.md), [Ar-Rahman (the Most Gracious)](../entities/divine_ar-rahman.md), [Iblis (Satan)](../entities/figure_iblis.md), [Medina](../entities/place_madinah.md), [Madyan](../entities/place_madyan.md), [Rabb (the Lord)](../entities/divine_rabb.md), [John the Baptist (Yahya)](../entities/prophet_yahya.md)

## Verses

### Verse 36:0
**Arabic**: بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

**English**: In the name of God, the Gracious, the Merciful.

### Verse 36:1
**Arabic**: يسٓ

**English**: Ya, Seen.

### Verse 36:2
**Arabic**: وَٱلْقُرْءَانِ ٱلْحَكِيمِ

**English**: By the Wise Quran.

### Verse 36:3
**Arabic**: إِنَّكَ لَمِنَ ٱلْمُرْسَلِينَ

**English**: You are one of the messengers.

### Verse 36:4
**Arabic**: عَلَىٰ صِرَٰطٍۢ مُّسْتَقِيمٍۢ

**English**: On a straight path.

### Verse 36:5
**Arabic**: تَنزِيلَ ٱلْعَزِيزِ ٱلرَّحِيمِ

**English**: The revelation of the Almighty, the Merciful.

### Verse 36:6
**Arabic**: لِتُنذِرَ قَوْمًۭا مَّآ أُنذِرَ ءَابَآؤُهُمْ فَهُمْ غَٰفِلُونَ

**English**: To warn a people whose ancestors were not warned, and so they are unaware.

### Verse 36:7
**Arabic**: لَقَدْ حَقَّ ٱلْقَوْلُ عَلَىٰٓ أَكْثَرِهِمْ فَهُمْ لَا يُؤْمِنُونَ

**English**: The Word was realized against most of them, for they do not believe.

### Verse 36:8
**Arabic**: إِنَّا جَعَلْنَا فِىٓ أَعْنَٰقِهِمْ أَغْلَٰلًۭا فَهِىَ إِلَى ٱلْأَذْقَانِ فَهُم مُّقْمَحُونَ

**English**: We placed shackles around their necks, up to their chins, so they are stiff-necked.

### Verse 36:9
**Arabic**: وَجَعَلْنَا مِنۢ بَيْنِ أَيْدِيهِمْ سَدًّۭا وَمِنْ خَلْفِهِمْ سَدًّۭا فَأَغْشَيْنَٰهُمْ فَهُمْ لَا يُبْصِرُونَ

**English**: And We placed a barrier in front of them, and a barrier behind them, and We have enshrouded them, so they cannot see.

### Verse 36:10
**Arabic**: وَسَوَآءٌ عَلَيْهِمْ ءَأَنذَرْتَهُمْ أَمْ لَمْ تُنذِرْهُمْ لَا يُؤْمِنُونَ

**English**: It is the same for them, whether you warn them, or do not warn them-they will not believe.

### Verse 36:11
**Arabic**: إِنَّمَا تُنذِرُ مَنِ ٱتَّبَعَ ٱلذِّكْرَ وَخَشِىَ ٱلرَّحْمَٰنَ بِٱلْغَيْبِ ۖ فَبَشِّرْهُ بِمَغْفِرَةٍۢ وَأَجْرٍۢ كَرِيمٍ

**English**: You warn only him who follows the Message, and fears the Most Gracious inwardly. So give him good news of forgiveness, and a generous reward.

### Verse 36:12
**Arabic**: إِنَّا نَحْنُ نُحْىِ ٱلْمَوْتَىٰ وَنَكْتُبُ مَا قَدَّمُوا۟ وَءَاثَٰرَهُمْ ۚ وَكُلَّ شَىْءٍ أَحْصَيْنَٰهُ فِىٓ إِمَامٍۢ مُّبِينٍۢ

**English**: It is We who revive the dead; and We write down what they have forwarded, and their traces. We have tallied all things in a Clear Record.

### Verse 36:13
**Arabic**: وَٱضْرِبْ لَهُم مَّثَلًا أَصْحَٰبَ ٱلْقَرْيَةِ إِذْ جَآءَهَا ٱلْمُرْسَلُونَ

**English**: And cite for them the parable of the landlords of the town-when the messengers came to it.

### Verse 36:14
**Arabic**: إِذْ أَرْسَلْنَآ إِلَيْهِمُ ٱثْنَيْنِ فَكَذَّبُوهُمَا فَعَزَّزْنَا بِثَالِثٍۢ فَقَالُوٓا۟ إِنَّآ إِلَيْكُم مُّرْسَلُونَ

**English**: We sent them two messengers, but they denied them both, so We reinforced them with a third. They said, "We are messengers to you."

### Verse 36:15
**Arabic**: قَالُوا۟ مَآ أَنتُمْ إِلَّا بَشَرٌۭ مِّثْلُنَا وَمَآ أَنزَلَ ٱلرَّحْمَٰنُ مِن شَىْءٍ إِنْ أَنتُمْ إِلَّا تَكْذِبُونَ

**English**: They said, "You are nothing but humans like us, and the Gracious did not send down anything; you are only lying."

### Verse 36:16
**Arabic**: قَالُوا۟ رَبُّنَا يَعْلَمُ إِنَّآ إِلَيْكُمْ لَمُرْسَلُونَ

**English**: They said, "Our Lord knows that we are messengers to you.

### Verse 36:17
**Arabic**: وَمَا عَلَيْنَآ إِلَّا ٱلْبَلَٰغُ ٱلْمُبِينُ

**English**: And our only duty is clear communication."

### Verse 36:18
**Arabic**: قَالُوٓا۟ إِنَّا تَطَيَّرْنَا بِكُمْ ۖ لَئِن لَّمْ تَنتَهُوا۟ لَنَرْجُمَنَّكُمْ وَلَيَمَسَّنَّكُم مِّنَّا عَذَابٌ أَلِيمٌۭ

**English**: They said, "We see an evil omen in you; if you do not give up, we will stone you, and a painful punishment from us will befall you."

### Verse 36:19
**Arabic**: قَالُوا۟ طَٰٓئِرُكُم مَّعَكُمْ ۚ أَئِن ذُكِّرْتُم ۚ بَلْ أَنتُمْ قَوْمٌۭ مُّسْرِفُونَ

**English**: They said, "Your evil omen is upon you. Is it because you were reminded? But you are an extravagant people."

### Verse 36:20
**Arabic**: وَجَآءَ مِنْ أَقْصَا ٱلْمَدِينَةِ رَجُلٌۭ يَسْعَىٰ قَالَ يَٰقَوْمِ ٱتَّبِعُوا۟ ٱلْمُرْسَلِينَ

**English**: Then a man came running from the remotest part of the city. He said, "O my people, follow the messengers.

### Verse 36:21
**Arabic**: ٱتَّبِعُوا۟ مَن لَّا يَسْـَٔلُكُمْ أَجْرًۭا وَهُم مُّهْتَدُونَ

**English**: Follow those who ask you of no wage, and are themselves guided.

### Verse 36:22
**Arabic**: وَمَا لِىَ لَآ أَعْبُدُ ٱلَّذِى فَطَرَنِى وَإِلَيْهِ تُرْجَعُونَ

**English**: "And why should I not worship Him Who created me, and to Whom you will be returned?

### Verse 36:23
**Arabic**: ءَأَتَّخِذُ مِن دُونِهِۦٓ ءَالِهَةً إِن يُرِدْنِ ٱلرَّحْمَٰنُ بِضُرٍّۢ لَّا تُغْنِ عَنِّى شَفَٰعَتُهُمْ شَيْـًۭٔا وَلَا يُنقِذُونِ

**English**: Shall I take other gods instead of Him? If the Merciful desires harm for me, their intercession will not avail me at all, nor will they save me.

### Verse 36:24
**Arabic**: إِنِّىٓ إِذًۭا لَّفِى ضَلَٰلٍۢ مُّبِينٍ

**English**: In that case, I would be completely lost.

### Verse 36:25
**Arabic**: إِنِّىٓ ءَامَنتُ بِرَبِّكُمْ فَٱسْمَعُونِ

**English**: I have believed in your Lord, so listen to me."

### Verse 36:26
**Arabic**: قِيلَ ٱدْخُلِ ٱلْجَنَّةَ ۖ قَالَ يَٰلَيْتَ قَوْمِى يَعْلَمُونَ

**English**: It was said, "Enter Paradise." He said, "If only my people knew.

### Verse 36:27
**Arabic**: بِمَا غَفَرَ لِى رَبِّى وَجَعَلَنِى مِنَ ٱلْمُكْرَمِينَ

**English**: How my Lord has forgiven me, and made me one of the honored."

### Verse 36:28
**Arabic**: ۞ وَمَآ أَنزَلْنَا عَلَىٰ قَوْمِهِۦ مِنۢ بَعْدِهِۦ مِن جُندٍۢ مِّنَ ٱلسَّمَآءِ وَمَا كُنَّا مُنزِلِينَ

**English**: After him, We sent down no hosts from heaven to his people; nor would We ever send any down.

### Verse 36:29
**Arabic**: إِن كَانَتْ إِلَّا صَيْحَةًۭ وَٰحِدَةًۭ فَإِذَا هُمْ خَٰمِدُونَ

**English**: It was just one Cry, and they were stilled.

### Verse 36:30
**Arabic**: يَٰحَسْرَةً عَلَى ٱلْعِبَادِ ۚ مَا يَأْتِيهِم مِّن رَّسُولٍ إِلَّا كَانُوا۟ بِهِۦ يَسْتَهْزِءُونَ

**English**: Alas for the servants. No messenger ever came to them, but they ridiculed him.

### Verse 36:31
**Arabic**: أَلَمْ يَرَوْا۟ كَمْ أَهْلَكْنَا قَبْلَهُم مِّنَ ٱلْقُرُونِ أَنَّهُمْ إِلَيْهِمْ لَا يَرْجِعُونَ

**English**: Have they not considered how many generations We destroyed before them; and that unto them they will not return?

### Verse 36:32
**Arabic**: وَإِن كُلٌّۭ لَّمَّا جَمِيعٌۭ لَّدَيْنَا مُحْضَرُونَ

**English**: All of them, every single one of them, will be arraigned before Us.

### Verse 36:33
**Arabic**: وَءَايَةٌۭ لَّهُمُ ٱلْأَرْضُ ٱلْمَيْتَةُ أَحْيَيْنَٰهَا وَأَخْرَجْنَا مِنْهَا حَبًّۭا فَمِنْهُ يَأْكُلُونَ

**English**: And there is a sign for them in the dead land: We give it life, and produce from it grains from which they eat.

### Verse 36:34
**Arabic**: وَجَعَلْنَا فِيهَا جَنَّٰتٍۢ مِّن نَّخِيلٍۢ وَأَعْنَٰبٍۢ وَفَجَّرْنَا فِيهَا مِنَ ٱلْعُيُونِ

**English**: And We place in it gardens of palm-trees and vines, and cause springs to gush out of it.

### Verse 36:35
**Arabic**: لِيَأْكُلُوا۟ مِن ثَمَرِهِۦ وَمَا عَمِلَتْهُ أَيْدِيهِمْ ۖ أَفَلَا يَشْكُرُونَ

**English**: That they may eat from its fruits, although their hands did not make it. Will they not be appreciative?

### Verse 36:36
**Arabic**: سُبْحَٰنَ ٱلَّذِى خَلَقَ ٱلْأَزْوَٰجَ كُلَّهَا مِمَّا تُنۢبِتُ ٱلْأَرْضُ وَمِنْ أَنفُسِهِمْ وَمِمَّا لَا يَعْلَمُونَ

**English**: Glory be to Him who created all the pairs; of what the earth produces, and of their own selves, and of what they do not know.

### Verse 36:37
**Arabic**: وَءَايَةٌۭ لَّهُمُ ٱلَّيْلُ نَسْلَخُ مِنْهُ ٱلنَّهَارَ فَإِذَا هُم مُّظْلِمُونَ

**English**: Another sign for them is the night: We strip the day out of it-and they are in darkness.

### Verse 36:38
**Arabic**: وَٱلشَّمْسُ تَجْرِى لِمُسْتَقَرٍّۢ لَّهَا ۚ ذَٰلِكَ تَقْدِيرُ ٱلْعَزِيزِ ٱلْعَلِيمِ

**English**: And the sun runs towards its destination. Such is the design of the Almighty, the All-Knowing.

### Verse 36:39
**Arabic**: وَٱلْقَمَرَ قَدَّرْنَٰهُ مَنَازِلَ حَتَّىٰ عَادَ كَٱلْعُرْجُونِ ٱلْقَدِيمِ

**English**: And the moon: We have disposed it in phases, until it returns like the old twig.

### Verse 36:40
**Arabic**: لَا ٱلشَّمْسُ يَنۢبَغِى لَهَآ أَن تُدْرِكَ ٱلْقَمَرَ وَلَا ٱلَّيْلُ سَابِقُ ٱلنَّهَارِ ۚ وَكُلٌّۭ فِى فَلَكٍۢ يَسْبَحُونَ

**English**: The sun is not to overtake the moon, nor is the night to outpace the day. Each floats in an orbit.

### Verse 36:41
**Arabic**: وَءَايَةٌۭ لَّهُمْ أَنَّا حَمَلْنَا ذُرِّيَّتَهُمْ فِى ٱلْفُلْكِ ٱلْمَشْحُونِ

**English**: Another sign for them is that We carried their offspring in the laden Ark.

### Verse 36:42
**Arabic**: وَخَلَقْنَا لَهُم مِّن مِّثْلِهِۦ مَا يَرْكَبُونَ

**English**: And We created for them the like of it, in which they ride.

### Verse 36:43
**Arabic**: وَإِن نَّشَأْ نُغْرِقْهُمْ فَلَا صَرِيخَ لَهُمْ وَلَا هُمْ يُنقَذُونَ

**English**: If We will, We can drown them-with no screaming to be heard from them, nor will they be saved.

### Verse 36:44
**Arabic**: إِلَّا رَحْمَةًۭ مِّنَّا وَمَتَٰعًا إِلَىٰ حِينٍۢ

**English**: Except by a mercy from Us, and enjoyment for a while.

### Verse 36:45
**Arabic**: وَإِذَا قِيلَ لَهُمُ ٱتَّقُوا۟ مَا بَيْنَ أَيْدِيكُمْ وَمَا خَلْفَكُمْ لَعَلَّكُمْ تُرْحَمُونَ

**English**: Yet when it is said to them, "Beware of what lies before you, and what lies behind you, that you may receive mercy."

### Verse 36:46
**Arabic**: وَمَا تَأْتِيهِم مِّنْ ءَايَةٍۢ مِّنْ ءَايَٰتِ رَبِّهِمْ إِلَّا كَانُوا۟ عَنْهَا مُعْرِضِينَ

**English**: Yet never came to them a sign of their Lord's signs, but they turned away from it.

### Verse 36:47
**Arabic**: وَإِذَا قِيلَ لَهُمْ أَنفِقُوا۟ مِمَّا رَزَقَكُمُ ٱللَّهُ قَالَ ٱلَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا۟ لِلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوٓا۟ أَنُطْعِمُ مَن لَّوْ يَشَآءُ ٱللَّهُ أَطْعَمَهُۥٓ إِنْ أَنتُمْ إِلَّا فِى ضَلَٰلٍۢ مُّبِينٍۢ

**English**: And when it is said to them, "Spend of what God has provided for you," those who disbelieve say to those who believe, "Shall we feed someone whom God could feed, if He so willed? You must be deeply misguided."

### Verse 36:48
**Arabic**: وَيَقُولُونَ مَتَىٰ هَٰذَا ٱلْوَعْدُ إِن كُنتُمْ صَٰدِقِينَ

**English**: And they say, "When will this promise be, if you are truthful?"

### Verse 36:49
**Arabic**: مَا يَنظُرُونَ إِلَّا صَيْحَةًۭ وَٰحِدَةًۭ تَأْخُذُهُمْ وَهُمْ يَخِصِّمُونَ

**English**: All they can expect is a single blast, which will seize them while they feud.

### Verse 36:50
**Arabic**: فَلَا يَسْتَطِيعُونَ تَوْصِيَةًۭ وَلَآ إِلَىٰٓ أَهْلِهِمْ يَرْجِعُونَ

**English**: They will not be able to make a will, nor will they return to their families.

### Verse 36:51
**Arabic**: وَنُفِخَ فِى ٱلصُّورِ فَإِذَا هُم مِّنَ ٱلْأَجْدَاثِ إِلَىٰ رَبِّهِمْ يَنسِلُونَ

**English**: The Trumpet will be blown, then behold, they will rush from the tombs to their Lord.

### Verse 36:52
**Arabic**: قَالُوا۟ يَٰوَيْلَنَا مَنۢ بَعَثَنَا مِن مَّرْقَدِنَا ۜ ۗ هَٰذَا مَا وَعَدَ ٱلرَّحْمَٰنُ وَصَدَقَ ٱلْمُرْسَلُونَ

**English**: They will say, "Woe to us! Who resurrected us from our resting-place?" This is what the Most Gracious had promised, and the messengers have spoken the truth."

### Verse 36:53
**Arabic**: إِن كَانَتْ إِلَّا صَيْحَةًۭ وَٰحِدَةًۭ فَإِذَا هُمْ جَمِيعٌۭ لَّدَيْنَا مُحْضَرُونَ

**English**: It will be but a single scream; and behold, they will all be brought before Us.

### Verse 36:54
**Arabic**: فَٱلْيَوْمَ لَا تُظْلَمُ نَفْسٌۭ شَيْـًۭٔا وَلَا تُجْزَوْنَ إِلَّا مَا كُنتُمْ تَعْمَلُونَ

**English**: On that Day, no soul will be wronged in the least, and you will be recompensed only for what you used to do.

### Verse 36:55
**Arabic**: إِنَّ أَصْحَٰبَ ٱلْجَنَّةِ ٱلْيَوْمَ فِى شُغُلٍۢ فَٰكِهُونَ

**English**: The inhabitants of Paradise, on that Day, will be happily busy.

### Verse 36:56
**Arabic**: هُمْ وَأَزْوَٰجُهُمْ فِى ظِلَٰلٍ عَلَى ٱلْأَرَآئِكِ مُتَّكِـُٔونَ

**English**: They and their spouses, in shades, reclining on couches.

### Verse 36:57
**Arabic**: لَهُمْ فِيهَا فَٰكِهَةٌۭ وَلَهُم مَّا يَدَّعُونَ

**English**: They will have therein fruits. They will have whatever they call for.

### Verse 36:58
**Arabic**: سَلَٰمٌۭ قَوْلًۭا مِّن رَّبٍّۢ رَّحِيمٍۢ

**English**: Peace-a saying from a Most Merciful Lord.

### Verse 36:59
**Arabic**: وَٱمْتَٰزُوا۟ ٱلْيَوْمَ أَيُّهَا ٱلْمُجْرِمُونَ

**English**: But step aside today, you criminals.

### Verse 36:60
**Arabic**: ۞ أَلَمْ أَعْهَدْ إِلَيْكُمْ يَٰبَنِىٓ ءَادَمَ أَن لَّا تَعْبُدُوا۟ ٱلشَّيْطَٰنَ ۖ إِنَّهُۥ لَكُمْ عَدُوٌّۭ مُّبِينٌۭ

**English**: Did I not covenant with you, O Children of Adam, that you shall not serve the devil? That he is your sworn enemy?

### Verse 36:61
**Arabic**: وَأَنِ ٱعْبُدُونِى ۚ هَٰذَا صِرَٰطٌۭ مُّسْتَقِيمٌۭ

**English**: And that you shall serve Me? This is a straight path.

### Verse 36:62
**Arabic**: وَلَقَدْ أَضَلَّ مِنكُمْ جِبِلًّۭا كَثِيرًا ۖ أَفَلَمْ تَكُونُوا۟ تَعْقِلُونَ

**English**: He has misled a great multitude of you. Did you not understand?

### Verse 36:63
**Arabic**: هَٰذِهِۦ جَهَنَّمُ ٱلَّتِى كُنتُمْ تُوعَدُونَ

**English**: This is Hellfire, which you were promised.

### Verse 36:64
**Arabic**: ٱصْلَوْهَا ٱلْيَوْمَ بِمَا كُنتُمْ تَكْفُرُونَ

**English**: Roast in it today, because you persistently disbelieved.

### Verse 36:65
**Arabic**: ٱلْيَوْمَ نَخْتِمُ عَلَىٰٓ أَفْوَٰهِهِمْ وَتُكَلِّمُنَآ أَيْدِيهِمْ وَتَشْهَدُ أَرْجُلُهُم بِمَا كَانُوا۟ يَكْسِبُونَ

**English**: On this Day, We will seal their mouths, and their hands will speak to Us, and their feet will testify to everything they had done.

### Verse 36:66
**Arabic**: وَلَوْ نَشَآءُ لَطَمَسْنَا عَلَىٰٓ أَعْيُنِهِمْ فَٱسْتَبَقُوا۟ ٱلصِّرَٰطَ فَأَنَّىٰ يُبْصِرُونَ

**English**: If We will, We can blind their eyes as they rush towards the path-but how will they see?

### Verse 36:67
**Arabic**: وَلَوْ نَشَآءُ لَمَسَخْنَٰهُمْ عَلَىٰ مَكَانَتِهِمْ فَمَا ٱسْتَطَٰعُوا۟ مُضِيًّۭا وَلَا يَرْجِعُونَ

**English**: And if We will, We can cripple them in their place; so they can neither move forward, nor go back.

### Verse 36:68
**Arabic**: وَمَن نُّعَمِّرْهُ نُنَكِّسْهُ فِى ٱلْخَلْقِ ۖ أَفَلَا يَعْقِلُونَ

**English**: Whomever We grant old age, We reverse his development. Do they not understand?

### Verse 36:69
**Arabic**: وَمَا عَلَّمْنَٰهُ ٱلشِّعْرَ وَمَا يَنۢبَغِى لَهُۥٓ ۚ إِنْ هُوَ إِلَّا ذِكْرٌۭ وَقُرْءَانٌۭ مُّبِينٌۭ

**English**: We did not teach him poetry, nor is it proper for him. It is only a reminder, and a Clear Quran.

### Verse 36:70
**Arabic**: لِّيُنذِرَ مَن كَانَ حَيًّۭا وَيَحِقَّ ٱلْقَوْلُ عَلَى ٱلْكَٰفِرِينَ

**English**: That he may warn whoever is alive, and prove the Word against the faithless.

### Verse 36:71
**Arabic**: أَوَلَمْ يَرَوْا۟ أَنَّا خَلَقْنَا لَهُم مِّمَّا عَمِلَتْ أَيْدِينَآ أَنْعَٰمًۭا فَهُمْ لَهَا مَٰلِكُونَ

**English**: Have they not seen that We created for them, of Our Handiwork, livestock that they own?

### Verse 36:72
**Arabic**: وَذَلَّلْنَٰهَا لَهُمْ فَمِنْهَا رَكُوبُهُمْ وَمِنْهَا يَأْكُلُونَ

**English**: And We subdued them for them. Some they ride, and some they eat.

### Verse 36:73
**Arabic**: وَلَهُمْ فِيهَا مَنَٰفِعُ وَمَشَارِبُ ۖ أَفَلَا يَشْكُرُونَ

**English**: And they have in them other benefits, and drinks. Will they not give thanks?

### Verse 36:74
**Arabic**: وَٱتَّخَذُوا۟ مِن دُونِ ٱللَّهِ ءَالِهَةًۭ لَّعَلَّهُمْ يُنصَرُونَ

**English**: Yet they have taken to themselves gods other than God, that perhaps they may be helped.

### Verse 36:75
**Arabic**: لَا يَسْتَطِيعُونَ نَصْرَهُمْ وَهُمْ لَهُمْ جُندٌۭ مُّحْضَرُونَ

**English**: They cannot help them, although they are arrayed as troops for them.

### Verse 36:76
**Arabic**: فَلَا يَحْزُنكَ قَوْلُهُمْ ۘ إِنَّا نَعْلَمُ مَا يُسِرُّونَ وَمَا يُعْلِنُونَ

**English**: So let their words not sadden you. We know what they conceal, and what they reveal.

### Verse 36:77
**Arabic**: أَوَلَمْ يَرَ ٱلْإِنسَٰنُ أَنَّا خَلَقْنَٰهُ مِن نُّطْفَةٍۢ فَإِذَا هُوَ خَصِيمٌۭ مُّبِينٌۭ

**English**: Does the human being not consider that We created him from a seed? Yet he becomes a fierce adversary.

### Verse 36:78
**Arabic**: وَضَرَبَ لَنَا مَثَلًۭا وَنَسِىَ خَلْقَهُۥ ۖ قَالَ مَن يُحْىِ ٱلْعِظَٰمَ وَهِىَ رَمِيمٌۭ

**English**: And he produces arguments against Us, and he forgets his own creation. He says, "Who will revive the bones when they have decayed?"

### Verse 36:79
**Arabic**: قُلْ يُحْيِيهَا ٱلَّذِىٓ أَنشَأَهَآ أَوَّلَ مَرَّةٍۢ ۖ وَهُوَ بِكُلِّ خَلْقٍ عَلِيمٌ

**English**: Say, "He who initiated them in the first instance will revive them. He has knowledge of every creation."

### Verse 36:80
**Arabic**: ٱلَّذِى جَعَلَ لَكُم مِّنَ ٱلشَّجَرِ ٱلْأَخْضَرِ نَارًۭا فَإِذَآ أَنتُم مِّنْهُ تُوقِدُونَ

**English**: He who produced fuel for you from the green trees, with which you kindle a fire.

### Verse 36:81
**Arabic**: أَوَلَيْسَ ٱلَّذِى خَلَقَ ٱلسَّمَٰوَٰتِ وَٱلْأَرْضَ بِقَٰدِرٍ عَلَىٰٓ أَن يَخْلُقَ مِثْلَهُم ۚ بَلَىٰ وَهُوَ ٱلْخَلَّٰقُ ٱلْعَلِيمُ

**English**: Is not He who created the heavens and the earth able to create the like of them? Certainly. He is the Supreme All-Knowing Creator.

### Verse 36:82
**Arabic**: إِنَّمَآ أَمْرُهُۥٓ إِذَآ أَرَادَ شَيْـًٔا أَن يَقُولَ لَهُۥ كُن فَيَكُونُ

**English**: His command, when He wills a thing, is to say to it, "Be," and it comes to be.

### Verse 36:83
**Arabic**: فَسُبْحَٰنَ ٱلَّذِى بِيَدِهِۦ مَلَكُوتُ كُلِّ شَىْءٍۢ وَإِلَيْهِ تُرْجَعُونَ

**English**: So glory be to Him in whose hand is the dominion of everything, and to Him you will be returned.
