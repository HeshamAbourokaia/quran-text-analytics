# Surah 89: Al-Fajr (الفجر)

- **Mushaf number**: 89
- **Place of revelation**: Mecca (Makkiyah)
- **Verse count**: 30
- **Themes**: [Ethics and Character](../themes/ethics.md)
- **Named entities mentioned**: [Allah](../entities/divine_allah.md), [Ar-Rahim (the Most Merciful)](../entities/divine_ar-rahim.md), [Ar-Rahman (the Most Gracious)](../entities/divine_ar-rahman.md), [Pharaoh (Firaun)](../entities/figure_firaun.md), [Rabb (the Lord)](../entities/divine_rabb.md)

## Verses

### Verse 89:0
**Arabic**: بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

**English**: In the name of God, the Gracious, the Merciful.

### Verse 89:1
**Arabic**: وَٱلْفَجْرِ

**English**: By the daybreak.

### Verse 89:2
**Arabic**: وَلَيَالٍ عَشْرٍۢ

**English**: And ten nights.

### Verse 89:3
**Arabic**: وَٱلشَّفْعِ وَٱلْوَتْرِ

**English**: And the even and the odd.

### Verse 89:4
**Arabic**: وَٱلَّيْلِ إِذَا يَسْرِ

**English**: And the night as it recedes.

### Verse 89:5
**Arabic**: هَلْ فِى ذَٰلِكَ قَسَمٌۭ لِّذِى حِجْرٍ

**English**: Is there in this an oath for a rational person?

### Verse 89:6
**Arabic**: أَلَمْ تَرَ كَيْفَ فَعَلَ رَبُّكَ بِعَادٍ

**English**: Have you not seen how your Lord dealt with Aad?

### Verse 89:7
**Arabic**: إِرَمَ ذَاتِ ٱلْعِمَادِ

**English**: Erum of the pillars.

### Verse 89:8
**Arabic**: ٱلَّتِى لَمْ يُخْلَقْ مِثْلُهَا فِى ٱلْبِلَٰدِ

**English**: The like of which was never created in the land.

### Verse 89:9
**Arabic**: وَثَمُودَ ٱلَّذِينَ جَابُوا۟ ٱلصَّخْرَ بِٱلْوَادِ

**English**: And Thamood-those who carved the rocks in the valley.

### Verse 89:10
**Arabic**: وَفِرْعَوْنَ ذِى ٱلْأَوْتَادِ

**English**: And Pharaoh of the Stakes.

### Verse 89:11
**Arabic**: ٱلَّذِينَ طَغَوْا۟ فِى ٱلْبِلَٰدِ

**English**: Those who committed excesses in the lands.

### Verse 89:12
**Arabic**: فَأَكْثَرُوا۟ فِيهَا ٱلْفَسَادَ

**English**: And spread much corruption therein.

### Verse 89:13
**Arabic**: فَصَبَّ عَلَيْهِمْ رَبُّكَ سَوْطَ عَذَابٍ

**English**: So your Lord poured down upon them a scourge of punishment.

### Verse 89:14
**Arabic**: إِنَّ رَبَّكَ لَبِٱلْمِرْصَادِ

**English**: Your Lord is on the lookout.

### Verse 89:15
**Arabic**: فَأَمَّا ٱلْإِنسَٰنُ إِذَا مَا ٱبْتَلَىٰهُ رَبُّهُۥ فَأَكْرَمَهُۥ وَنَعَّمَهُۥ فَيَقُولُ رَبِّىٓ أَكْرَمَنِ

**English**: As for man, whenever his Lord tests him, and honors him, and prospers him, he says, "My Lord has honored me."

### Verse 89:16
**Arabic**: وَأَمَّآ إِذَا مَا ٱبْتَلَىٰهُ فَقَدَرَ عَلَيْهِ رِزْقَهُۥ فَيَقُولُ رَبِّىٓ أَهَٰنَنِ

**English**: But whenever He tests him, and restricts his livelihood for him, he says, "My Lord has insulted me."

### Verse 89:17
**Arabic**: كَلَّا ۖ بَل لَّا تُكْرِمُونَ ٱلْيَتِيمَ

**English**: Not at all. But you do not honor the orphan.

### Verse 89:18
**Arabic**: وَلَا تَحَٰٓضُّونَ عَلَىٰ طَعَامِ ٱلْمِسْكِينِ

**English**: And you do not urge the feeding of the poor.

### Verse 89:19
**Arabic**: وَتَأْكُلُونَ ٱلتُّرَاثَ أَكْلًۭا لَّمًّۭا

**English**: And you devour inheritance with all greed.

### Verse 89:20
**Arabic**: وَتُحِبُّونَ ٱلْمَالَ حُبًّۭا جَمًّۭا

**English**: And you love wealth with immense love.

### Verse 89:21
**Arabic**: كَلَّآ إِذَا دُكَّتِ ٱلْأَرْضُ دَكًّۭا دَكًّۭا

**English**: No-when the earth is leveled, pounded, and crushed.

### Verse 89:22
**Arabic**: وَجَآءَ رَبُّكَ وَٱلْمَلَكُ صَفًّۭا صَفًّۭا

**English**: And your Lord comes, with the angels, row after row.

### Verse 89:23
**Arabic**: وَجِا۟ىٓءَ يَوْمَئِذٍۭ بِجَهَنَّمَ ۚ يَوْمَئِذٍۢ يَتَذَكَّرُ ٱلْإِنسَٰنُ وَأَنَّىٰ لَهُ ٱلذِّكْرَىٰ

**English**: And on that Day, Hell is brought forward. On that Day, man will remember, but how will remembrance avail him?

### Verse 89:24
**Arabic**: يَقُولُ يَٰلَيْتَنِى قَدَّمْتُ لِحَيَاتِى

**English**: He will say, "If only I had forwarded for my life."

### Verse 89:25
**Arabic**: فَيَوْمَئِذٍۢ لَّا يُعَذِّبُ عَذَابَهُۥٓ أَحَدٌۭ

**English**: On that Day, none will punish as He punishes.

### Verse 89:26
**Arabic**: وَلَا يُوثِقُ وَثَاقَهُۥٓ أَحَدٌۭ

**English**: And none will shackle as He shackles.

### Verse 89:27
**Arabic**: يَٰٓأَيَّتُهَا ٱلنَّفْسُ ٱلْمُطْمَئِنَّةُ

**English**: But as for you, O tranquil soul.

### Verse 89:28
**Arabic**: ٱرْجِعِىٓ إِلَىٰ رَبِّكِ رَاضِيَةًۭ مَّرْضِيَّةًۭ

**English**: Return to your Lord, pleased and accepted.

### Verse 89:29
**Arabic**: فَٱدْخُلِى فِى عِبَٰدِى

**English**: Enter among My servants.

### Verse 89:30
**Arabic**: وَٱدْخُلِى جَنَّتِى

**English**: Enter My Paradise.
