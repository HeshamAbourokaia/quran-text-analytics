# Surah 105: Al-Fil (الفيل)

- **Mushaf number**: 105
- **Place of revelation**: Mecca (Makkiyah)
- **Verse count**: 5
- **Themes**: [Patience and Endurance](../themes/patience.md)
- **Named entities mentioned**: [Allah](../entities/divine_allah.md), [Ar-Rahim (the Most Merciful)](../entities/divine_ar-rahim.md), [Ar-Rahman (the Most Gracious)](../entities/divine_ar-rahman.md), [Rabb (the Lord)](../entities/divine_rabb.md)

- **Revelation order (chronological)**: #19 of 114
- **Revelation phase**: [Early Meccan](../timeline/phase_early-mecca.md) (610-615 CE, Year 1-5 of Prophethood)

## Verses

### Verse 105:0
**Arabic**: بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

**English**: In the name of God, the Gracious, the Merciful.

### Verse 105:1
**Arabic**: أَلَمْ تَرَ كَيْفَ فَعَلَ رَبُّكَ بِأَصْحَٰبِ ٱلْفِيلِ

**English**: Have you not considered how your Lord dealt with the People of the Elephant?

### Verse 105:2
**Arabic**: أَلَمْ يَجْعَلْ كَيْدَهُمْ فِى تَضْلِيلٍۢ

**English**: Did He not make their plan go wrong?

### Verse 105:3
**Arabic**: وَأَرْسَلَ عَلَيْهِمْ طَيْرًا أَبَابِيلَ

**English**: He sent against them swarms of birds.

### Verse 105:4
**Arabic**: تَرْمِيهِم بِحِجَارَةٍۢ مِّن سِجِّيلٍۢ

**English**: Throwing at them rocks of baked clay.

### Verse 105:5
**Arabic**: فَجَعَلَهُمْ كَعَصْفٍۢ مَّأْكُولٍۭ

**English**: Leaving them like chewed-up leaves.
