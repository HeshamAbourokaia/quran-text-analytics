# Surah 90: Al-Balad (البلد)

- **Mushaf number**: 90
- **Place of revelation**: Mecca (Makkiyah)
- **Verse count**: 20
- **Themes**: [Warning](../themes/warning.md), [Patience and Endurance](../themes/patience.md)
- **Named entities mentioned**: [Allah](../entities/divine_allah.md), [Ar-Rahim (the Most Merciful)](../entities/divine_ar-rahim.md), [Ar-Rahman (the Most Gracious)](../entities/divine_ar-rahman.md), [Rabb (the Lord)](../entities/divine_rabb.md)

## Verses

### Verse 90:0
**Arabic**: بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

**English**: In the name of God, the Gracious, the Merciful.

### Verse 90:1
**Arabic**: لَآ أُقْسِمُ بِهَٰذَا ٱلْبَلَدِ

**English**: I swear by this land.

### Verse 90:2
**Arabic**: وَأَنتَ حِلٌّۢ بِهَٰذَا ٱلْبَلَدِ

**English**: And you are a resident of this land.

### Verse 90:3
**Arabic**: وَوَالِدٍۢ وَمَا وَلَدَ

**English**: And by a father and what he fathered.

### Verse 90:4
**Arabic**: لَقَدْ خَلَقْنَا ٱلْإِنسَٰنَ فِى كَبَدٍ

**English**: We created man in distress.

### Verse 90:5
**Arabic**: أَيَحْسَبُ أَن لَّن يَقْدِرَ عَلَيْهِ أَحَدٌۭ

**English**: Does he think that no one has power over him?

### Verse 90:6
**Arabic**: يَقُولُ أَهْلَكْتُ مَالًۭا لُّبَدًا

**English**: He says, "I have used up so much money."

### Verse 90:7
**Arabic**: أَيَحْسَبُ أَن لَّمْ يَرَهُۥٓ أَحَدٌ

**English**: Does he think that no one sees him?

### Verse 90:8
**Arabic**: أَلَمْ نَجْعَل لَّهُۥ عَيْنَيْنِ

**English**: Did We not give him two eyes?

### Verse 90:9
**Arabic**: وَلِسَانًۭا وَشَفَتَيْنِ

**English**: And a tongue, and two lips?

### Verse 90:10
**Arabic**: وَهَدَيْنَٰهُ ٱلنَّجْدَيْنِ

**English**: And We showed him the two ways?

### Verse 90:11
**Arabic**: فَلَا ٱقْتَحَمَ ٱلْعَقَبَةَ

**English**: But he did not brave the ascent.

### Verse 90:12
**Arabic**: وَمَآ أَدْرَىٰكَ مَا ٱلْعَقَبَةُ

**English**: And what will explain to you what the ascent is?

### Verse 90:13
**Arabic**: فَكُّ رَقَبَةٍ

**English**: The freeing of a slave.

### Verse 90:14
**Arabic**: أَوْ إِطْعَٰمٌۭ فِى يَوْمٍۢ ذِى مَسْغَبَةٍۢ

**English**: Or the feeding on a day of hunger.

### Verse 90:15
**Arabic**: يَتِيمًۭا ذَا مَقْرَبَةٍ

**English**: An orphan near of kin.

### Verse 90:16
**Arabic**: أَوْ مِسْكِينًۭا ذَا مَتْرَبَةٍۢ

**English**: Or a destitute in the dust.

### Verse 90:17
**Arabic**: ثُمَّ كَانَ مِنَ ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوا۟ وَتَوَاصَوْا۟ بِٱلصَّبْرِ وَتَوَاصَوْا۟ بِٱلْمَرْحَمَةِ

**English**: Then he becomes of those who believe, and advise one another to patience, and advise one another to kindness.

### Verse 90:18
**Arabic**: أُو۟لَٰٓئِكَ أَصْحَٰبُ ٱلْمَيْمَنَةِ

**English**: These are the people of happiness.

### Verse 90:19
**Arabic**: وَٱلَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا۟ بِـَٔايَٰتِنَا هُمْ أَصْحَٰبُ ٱلْمَشْـَٔمَةِ

**English**: But as for those who defy Our revelations-these are the people of misery.

### Verse 90:20
**Arabic**: عَلَيْهِمْ نَارٌۭ مُّؤْصَدَةٌۢ

**English**: Upon them is a padlocked Fire.
