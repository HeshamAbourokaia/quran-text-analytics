# Surah 69: Al-Haqqah (الحاقة)

- **Mushaf number**: 69
- **Place of revelation**: Mecca (Makkiyah)
- **Verse count**: 52
- **Themes**: [Day of Judgment](../themes/day-of-judgment.md)
- **Named entities mentioned**: [Allah](../entities/divine_allah.md), [Ar-Rahim (the Most Merciful)](../entities/divine_ar-rahim.md), [Ar-Rahman (the Most Gracious)](../entities/divine_ar-rahman.md), [Pharaoh (Firaun)](../entities/figure_firaun.md), [Rabb (the Lord)](../entities/divine_rabb.md)

- **Revelation order (chronological)**: #78 of 114
- **Revelation phase**: [Late Meccan](../timeline/phase_late-mecca.md) (619-622 CE, Year 9-13 of Prophethood)

## Verses

### Verse 69:0
**Arabic**: بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

**English**: In the name of God, the Gracious, the Merciful.

### Verse 69:1
**Arabic**: ٱلْحَآقَّةُ

**English**: The Reality.

### Verse 69:2
**Arabic**: مَا ٱلْحَآقَّةُ

**English**: What is the Reality?

### Verse 69:3
**Arabic**: وَمَآ أَدْرَىٰكَ مَا ٱلْحَآقَّةُ

**English**: What will make you understand what the Reality is?

### Verse 69:4
**Arabic**: كَذَّبَتْ ثَمُودُ وَعَادٌۢ بِٱلْقَارِعَةِ

**English**: Thamood and Aad denied the Catastrophe.

### Verse 69:5
**Arabic**: فَأَمَّا ثَمُودُ فَأُهْلِكُوا۟ بِٱلطَّاغِيَةِ

**English**: As for Thamood, they were annihilated by the Overwhelming.

### Verse 69:6
**Arabic**: وَأَمَّا عَادٌۭ فَأُهْلِكُوا۟ بِرِيحٍۢ صَرْصَرٍ عَاتِيَةٍۢ

**English**: And as for Aad; they were annihilated by a furious, roaring wind.

### Verse 69:7
**Arabic**: سَخَّرَهَا عَلَيْهِمْ سَبْعَ لَيَالٍۢ وَثَمَٰنِيَةَ أَيَّامٍ حُسُومًۭا فَتَرَى ٱلْقَوْمَ فِيهَا صَرْعَىٰ كَأَنَّهُمْ أَعْجَازُ نَخْلٍ خَاوِيَةٍۢ

**English**: He unleashed it upon them for seven nights and eight days, in succession. You could see the people tossed around, as though they were stumps of hollow palm-trees.

### Verse 69:8
**Arabic**: فَهَلْ تَرَىٰ لَهُم مِّنۢ بَاقِيَةٍۢ

**English**: Can you see any remnant of them?

### Verse 69:9
**Arabic**: وَجَآءَ فِرْعَوْنُ وَمَن قَبْلَهُۥ وَٱلْمُؤْتَفِكَٰتُ بِٱلْخَاطِئَةِ

**English**: Then Pharaoh came, and those before him, and the Overturned Cities steeped in sin.

### Verse 69:10
**Arabic**: فَعَصَوْا۟ رَسُولَ رَبِّهِمْ فَأَخَذَهُمْ أَخْذَةًۭ رَّابِيَةً

**English**: But they disobeyed the messenger of their Lord, so He seized them with an overpowering grip.

### Verse 69:11
**Arabic**: إِنَّا لَمَّا طَغَا ٱلْمَآءُ حَمَلْنَٰكُمْ فِى ٱلْجَارِيَةِ

**English**: When the waters overflowed, We carried you in the cruising ship.

### Verse 69:12
**Arabic**: لِنَجْعَلَهَا لَكُمْ تَذْكِرَةًۭ وَتَعِيَهَآ أُذُنٌۭ وَٰعِيَةٌۭ

**English**: To make it a lesson for you-so that retaining ears may retain it.

### Verse 69:13
**Arabic**: فَإِذَا نُفِخَ فِى ٱلصُّورِ نَفْخَةٌۭ وَٰحِدَةٌۭ

**English**: Then, when the Trumpet is sounded a single time.

### Verse 69:14
**Arabic**: وَحُمِلَتِ ٱلْأَرْضُ وَٱلْجِبَالُ فَدُكَّتَا دَكَّةًۭ وَٰحِدَةًۭ

**English**: And the earth and the mountains are lifted up, and crushed, with a single crush.

### Verse 69:15
**Arabic**: فَيَوْمَئِذٍۢ وَقَعَتِ ٱلْوَاقِعَةُ

**English**: On that Day, the Event will come to pass.

### Verse 69:16
**Arabic**: وَٱنشَقَّتِ ٱلسَّمَآءُ فَهِىَ يَوْمَئِذٍۢ وَاهِيَةٌۭ

**English**: And the heaven will crack; so on that Day it will be frail.

### Verse 69:17
**Arabic**: وَٱلْمَلَكُ عَلَىٰٓ أَرْجَآئِهَا ۚ وَيَحْمِلُ عَرْشَ رَبِّكَ فَوْقَهُمْ يَوْمَئِذٍۢ ثَمَٰنِيَةٌۭ

**English**: And the angels will be ranged around its borders, while eight will be carrying the Throne of your Lord above them that Day.

### Verse 69:18
**Arabic**: يَوْمَئِذٍۢ تُعْرَضُونَ لَا تَخْفَىٰ مِنكُمْ خَافِيَةٌۭ

**English**: On that Day you will be exposed, and no secret of yours will remain hidden.

### Verse 69:19
**Arabic**: فَأَمَّا مَنْ أُوتِىَ كِتَٰبَهُۥ بِيَمِينِهِۦ فَيَقُولُ هَآؤُمُ ٱقْرَءُوا۟ كِتَٰبِيَهْ

**English**: As for him who is given his book in his right hand, he will say, "Here, take my book and read it.

### Verse 69:20
**Arabic**: إِنِّى ظَنَنتُ أَنِّى مُلَٰقٍ حِسَابِيَهْ

**English**: I knew I would be held accountable."

### Verse 69:21
**Arabic**: فَهُوَ فِى عِيشَةٍۢ رَّاضِيَةٍۢ

**English**: So he will be in pleasant living.

### Verse 69:22
**Arabic**: فِى جَنَّةٍ عَالِيَةٍۢ

**English**: In a lofty Garden.

### Verse 69:23
**Arabic**: قُطُوفُهَا دَانِيَةٌۭ

**English**: Its pickings are within reach.

### Verse 69:24
**Arabic**: كُلُوا۟ وَٱشْرَبُوا۟ هَنِيٓـًٔۢا بِمَآ أَسْلَفْتُمْ فِى ٱلْأَيَّامِ ٱلْخَالِيَةِ

**English**: "Eat and drink merrily for what you did in the days gone by."

### Verse 69:25
**Arabic**: وَأَمَّا مَنْ أُوتِىَ كِتَٰبَهُۥ بِشِمَالِهِۦ فَيَقُولُ يَٰلَيْتَنِى لَمْ أُوتَ كِتَٰبِيَهْ

**English**: But as for him who is given his book in his left hand, he will say, "I wish I was never given my book.

### Verse 69:26
**Arabic**: وَلَمْ أَدْرِ مَا حِسَابِيَهْ

**English**: And never knew what my account was.

### Verse 69:27
**Arabic**: يَٰلَيْتَهَا كَانَتِ ٱلْقَاضِيَةَ

**English**: If only it was the end.

### Verse 69:28
**Arabic**: مَآ أَغْنَىٰ عَنِّى مَالِيَهْ ۜ

**English**: My money cannot avail me.

### Verse 69:29
**Arabic**: هَلَكَ عَنِّى سُلْطَٰنِيَهْ

**English**: My power has vanished from me."

### Verse 69:30
**Arabic**: خُذُوهُ فَغُلُّوهُ

**English**: "Take him and shackle him.

### Verse 69:31
**Arabic**: ثُمَّ ٱلْجَحِيمَ صَلُّوهُ

**English**: Then scorch him in the Blaze.

### Verse 69:32
**Arabic**: ثُمَّ فِى سِلْسِلَةٍۢ ذَرْعُهَا سَبْعُونَ ذِرَاعًۭا فَٱسْلُكُوهُ

**English**: Then in a chain which length is seventy cubits tie him up.

### Verse 69:33
**Arabic**: إِنَّهُۥ كَانَ لَا يُؤْمِنُ بِٱللَّهِ ٱلْعَظِيمِ

**English**: For he would not believe in God the Great.

### Verse 69:34
**Arabic**: وَلَا يَحُضُّ عَلَىٰ طَعَامِ ٱلْمِسْكِينِ

**English**: Nor would he advocate the feeding of the destitute.

### Verse 69:35
**Arabic**: فَلَيْسَ لَهُ ٱلْيَوْمَ هَٰهُنَا حَمِيمٌۭ

**English**: So he has no friend here today.

### Verse 69:36
**Arabic**: وَلَا طَعَامٌ إِلَّا مِنْ غِسْلِينٍۢ

**English**: And no food except scum.

### Verse 69:37
**Arabic**: لَّا يَأْكُلُهُۥٓ إِلَّا ٱلْخَٰطِـُٔونَ

**English**: Which only the sinners eat."

### Verse 69:38
**Arabic**: فَلَآ أُقْسِمُ بِمَا تُبْصِرُونَ

**English**: Indeed, I swear by what you see.

### Verse 69:39
**Arabic**: وَمَا لَا تُبْصِرُونَ

**English**: And by what you do not see.

### Verse 69:40
**Arabic**: إِنَّهُۥ لَقَوْلُ رَسُولٍۢ كَرِيمٍۢ

**English**: It is the speech of a noble messenger.

### Verse 69:41
**Arabic**: وَمَا هُوَ بِقَوْلِ شَاعِرٍۢ ۚ قَلِيلًۭا مَّا تُؤْمِنُونَ

**English**: And it is not the speech of a poet-little do you believe.

### Verse 69:42
**Arabic**: وَلَا بِقَوْلِ كَاهِنٍۢ ۚ قَلِيلًۭا مَّا تَذَكَّرُونَ

**English**: Nor is it the speech of a soothsayer-little do you take heed.

### Verse 69:43
**Arabic**: تَنزِيلٌۭ مِّن رَّبِّ ٱلْعَٰلَمِينَ

**English**: It is a revelation from the Lord of the Worlds.

### Verse 69:44
**Arabic**: وَلَوْ تَقَوَّلَ عَلَيْنَا بَعْضَ ٱلْأَقَاوِيلِ

**English**: Had he falsely attributed some statements to Us.

### Verse 69:45
**Arabic**: لَأَخَذْنَا مِنْهُ بِٱلْيَمِينِ

**English**: We would have seized him by the right arm.

### Verse 69:46
**Arabic**: ثُمَّ لَقَطَعْنَا مِنْهُ ٱلْوَتِينَ

**English**: Then slashed his lifeline.

### Verse 69:47
**Arabic**: فَمَا مِنكُم مِّنْ أَحَدٍ عَنْهُ حَٰجِزِينَ

**English**: And none of you could have restrained Us from him.

### Verse 69:48
**Arabic**: وَإِنَّهُۥ لَتَذْكِرَةٌۭ لِّلْمُتَّقِينَ

**English**: Surely, it is a message for the righteous.

### Verse 69:49
**Arabic**: وَإِنَّا لَنَعْلَمُ أَنَّ مِنكُم مُّكَذِّبِينَ

**English**: And We know that some of you will reject it.

### Verse 69:50
**Arabic**: وَإِنَّهُۥ لَحَسْرَةٌ عَلَى ٱلْكَٰفِرِينَ

**English**: And it is surely a source of grief for the unbelievers.

### Verse 69:51
**Arabic**: وَإِنَّهُۥ لَحَقُّ ٱلْيَقِينِ

**English**: Yet it is the absolute truth.

### Verse 69:52
**Arabic**: فَسَبِّحْ بِٱسْمِ رَبِّكَ ٱلْعَظِيمِ

**English**: So glorify the name of your Lord, the Magnificent.
