# Surah 93: Ad-Duha (الضحى)

- **Mushaf number**: 93
- **Place of revelation**: Mecca (Makkiyah)
- **Verse count**: 11
- **Themes**: [Ethics and Character](../themes/ethics.md)
- **Named entities mentioned**: [Allah](../entities/divine_allah.md), [Ar-Rahim (the Most Merciful)](../entities/divine_ar-rahim.md), [Ar-Rahman (the Most Gracious)](../entities/divine_ar-rahman.md), [Rabb (the Lord)](../entities/divine_rabb.md)

- **Revelation order (chronological)**: #11 of 114
- **Revelation phase**: [Early Meccan](../timeline/phase_early-mecca.md) (610-615 CE, Year 1-5 of Prophethood)

## Verses

### Verse 93:0
**Arabic**: بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

**English**: In the name of God, the Gracious, the Merciful.

### Verse 93:1
**Arabic**: وَٱلضُّحَىٰ

**English**: By the morning light.

### Verse 93:2
**Arabic**: وَٱلَّيْلِ إِذَا سَجَىٰ

**English**: And the night as it settles.

### Verse 93:3
**Arabic**: مَا وَدَّعَكَ رَبُّكَ وَمَا قَلَىٰ

**English**: Your Lord did not abandon you, nor did He forget.

### Verse 93:4
**Arabic**: وَلَلْءَاخِرَةُ خَيْرٌۭ لَّكَ مِنَ ٱلْأُولَىٰ

**English**: The Hereafter is better for you than the First.

### Verse 93:5
**Arabic**: وَلَسَوْفَ يُعْطِيكَ رَبُّكَ فَتَرْضَىٰٓ

**English**: And your Lord will give you, and you will be satisfied.

### Verse 93:6
**Arabic**: أَلَمْ يَجِدْكَ يَتِيمًۭا فَـَٔاوَىٰ

**English**: Did He not find you orphaned, and sheltered you?

### Verse 93:7
**Arabic**: وَوَجَدَكَ ضَآلًّۭا فَهَدَىٰ

**English**: And found you wandering, and guided you.

### Verse 93:8
**Arabic**: وَوَجَدَكَ عَآئِلًۭا فَأَغْنَىٰ

**English**: And found you in need, and enriched you?

### Verse 93:9
**Arabic**: فَأَمَّا ٱلْيَتِيمَ فَلَا تَقْهَرْ

**English**: Therefore, do not mistreat the orphan.

### Verse 93:10
**Arabic**: وَأَمَّا ٱلسَّآئِلَ فَلَا تَنْهَرْ

**English**: Nor rebuff the seeker.

### Verse 93:11
**Arabic**: وَأَمَّا بِنِعْمَةِ رَبِّكَ فَحَدِّثْ

**English**: But proclaim the blessings of your Lord.
