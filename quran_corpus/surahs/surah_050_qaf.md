# Surah 50: Qaf (ق)

- **Mushaf number**: 50
- **Place of revelation**: Mecca (Makkiyah)
- **Verse count**: 45
- **Themes**: [Faith and Monotheism](../themes/faith-monotheism.md)
- **Named entities mentioned**: [Allah](../entities/divine_allah.md), [Ar-Rahim (the Most Merciful)](../entities/divine_ar-rahim.md), [Ar-Rahman (the Most Gracious)](../entities/divine_ar-rahman.md), [Pharaoh (Firaun)](../entities/figure_firaun.md), [Lot (Lut)](../entities/prophet_lut.md), [Noah (Nuh)](../entities/prophet_nuh.md), [Rabb (the Lord)](../entities/divine_rabb.md)

- **Revelation order (chronological)**: #34 of 114
- **Revelation phase**: [Early Meccan](../timeline/phase_early-mecca.md) (610-615 CE, Year 1-5 of Prophethood)

## Verses

### Verse 50:0
**Arabic**: بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

**English**: In the name of God, the Gracious, the Merciful.

### Verse 50:1
**Arabic**: قٓ ۚ وَٱلْقُرْءَانِ ٱلْمَجِيدِ

**English**: Qaf. By the Glorious Quran.

### Verse 50:2
**Arabic**: بَلْ عَجِبُوٓا۟ أَن جَآءَهُم مُّنذِرٌۭ مِّنْهُمْ فَقَالَ ٱلْكَٰفِرُونَ هَٰذَا شَىْءٌ عَجِيبٌ

**English**: They marveled that a warner has come to them from among them. The disbelievers say, "This is something strange.

### Verse 50:3
**Arabic**: أَءِذَا مِتْنَا وَكُنَّا تُرَابًۭا ۖ ذَٰلِكَ رَجْعٌۢ بَعِيدٌۭ

**English**: When we have died and become dust? This is a farfetched return."

### Verse 50:4
**Arabic**: قَدْ عَلِمْنَا مَا تَنقُصُ ٱلْأَرْضُ مِنْهُمْ ۖ وَعِندَنَا كِتَٰبٌ حَفِيظٌۢ

**English**: We know what the earth consumes of them, and with Us is a comprehensive book.

### Verse 50:5
**Arabic**: بَلْ كَذَّبُوا۟ بِٱلْحَقِّ لَمَّا جَآءَهُمْ فَهُمْ فِىٓ أَمْرٍۢ مَّرِيجٍ

**English**: But they denied the truth when it has come to them, so they are in a confused state.

### Verse 50:6
**Arabic**: أَفَلَمْ يَنظُرُوٓا۟ إِلَى ٱلسَّمَآءِ فَوْقَهُمْ كَيْفَ بَنَيْنَٰهَا وَزَيَّنَّٰهَا وَمَا لَهَا مِن فُرُوجٍۢ

**English**: Have they not observed the sky above them, how We constructed it, and decorated it, and it has no cracks?

### Verse 50:7
**Arabic**: وَٱلْأَرْضَ مَدَدْنَٰهَا وَأَلْقَيْنَا فِيهَا رَوَٰسِىَ وَأَنۢبَتْنَا فِيهَا مِن كُلِّ زَوْجٍۭ بَهِيجٍۢ

**English**: And the earth, how We spread it out, and set on it mountains, and grew in it all kinds of delightful pairs?

### Verse 50:8
**Arabic**: تَبْصِرَةًۭ وَذِكْرَىٰ لِكُلِّ عَبْدٍۢ مُّنِيبٍۢ

**English**: A lesson and a reminder for every penitent worshiper.

### Verse 50:9
**Arabic**: وَنَزَّلْنَا مِنَ ٱلسَّمَآءِ مَآءًۭ مُّبَٰرَكًۭا فَأَنۢبَتْنَا بِهِۦ جَنَّٰتٍۢ وَحَبَّ ٱلْحَصِيدِ

**English**: And We brought down from the sky blessed water, and produced with it gardens and grain to harvest.

### Verse 50:10
**Arabic**: وَٱلنَّخْلَ بَاسِقَٰتٍۢ لَّهَا طَلْعٌۭ نَّضِيدٌۭ

**English**: And the soaring palm trees, with clustered dates.

### Verse 50:11
**Arabic**: رِّزْقًۭا لِّلْعِبَادِ ۖ وَأَحْيَيْنَا بِهِۦ بَلْدَةًۭ مَّيْتًۭا ۚ كَذَٰلِكَ ٱلْخُرُوجُ

**English**: As sustenance for the servants. And We revive thereby a dead town. Likewise is the resurrection.

### Verse 50:12
**Arabic**: كَذَّبَتْ قَبْلَهُمْ قَوْمُ نُوحٍۢ وَأَصْحَٰبُ ٱلرَّسِّ وَثَمُودُ

**English**: Before them the people of Noah denied the truth, and so did the dwellers of Russ, and Thamood.

### Verse 50:13
**Arabic**: وَعَادٌۭ وَفِرْعَوْنُ وَإِخْوَٰنُ لُوطٍۢ

**English**: And Aad, and Pharaoh, and the brethren of Lot.

### Verse 50:14
**Arabic**: وَأَصْحَٰبُ ٱلْأَيْكَةِ وَقَوْمُ تُبَّعٍۢ ۚ كُلٌّۭ كَذَّبَ ٱلرُّسُلَ فَحَقَّ وَعِيدِ

**English**: And the Dwellers of the Woods, and the people of Tubba. They all rejected the messengers, so My threat came true.

### Verse 50:15
**Arabic**: أَفَعَيِينَا بِٱلْخَلْقِ ٱلْأَوَّلِ ۚ بَلْ هُمْ فِى لَبْسٍۢ مِّنْ خَلْقٍۢ جَدِيدٍۢ

**English**: Were We fatigued by the first creation? But they are in doubt of a new creation.

### Verse 50:16
**Arabic**: وَلَقَدْ خَلَقْنَا ٱلْإِنسَٰنَ وَنَعْلَمُ مَا تُوَسْوِسُ بِهِۦ نَفْسُهُۥ ۖ وَنَحْنُ أَقْرَبُ إِلَيْهِ مِنْ حَبْلِ ٱلْوَرِيدِ

**English**: We created the human being, and We know what his soul whispers to him. We are nearer to him than his jugular vein.

### Verse 50:17
**Arabic**: إِذْ يَتَلَقَّى ٱلْمُتَلَقِّيَانِ عَنِ ٱلْيَمِينِ وَعَنِ ٱلشِّمَالِ قَعِيدٌۭ

**English**: As the two receivers receive, seated to the right and to the left.

### Verse 50:18
**Arabic**: مَّا يَلْفِظُ مِن قَوْلٍ إِلَّا لَدَيْهِ رَقِيبٌ عَتِيدٌۭ

**English**: Not a word does he utter, but there is a watcher by him, ready.

### Verse 50:19
**Arabic**: وَجَآءَتْ سَكْرَةُ ٱلْمَوْتِ بِٱلْحَقِّ ۖ ذَٰلِكَ مَا كُنتَ مِنْهُ تَحِيدُ

**English**: The daze of death has come in truth: "This is what you tried to evade."

### Verse 50:20
**Arabic**: وَنُفِخَ فِى ٱلصُّورِ ۚ ذَٰلِكَ يَوْمُ ٱلْوَعِيدِ

**English**: And the Trumpet is blown: "This is the Promised Day."

### Verse 50:21
**Arabic**: وَجَآءَتْ كُلُّ نَفْسٍۢ مَّعَهَا سَآئِقٌۭ وَشَهِيدٌۭ

**English**: And every soul will come forward, accompanied by a driver and a witness.

### Verse 50:22
**Arabic**: لَّقَدْ كُنتَ فِى غَفْلَةٍۢ مِّنْ هَٰذَا فَكَشَفْنَا عَنكَ غِطَآءَكَ فَبَصَرُكَ ٱلْيَوْمَ حَدِيدٌۭ

**English**: "You were in neglect of this, so We lifted your screen from you, and your vision today is keen."

### Verse 50:23
**Arabic**: وَقَالَ قَرِينُهُۥ هَٰذَا مَا لَدَىَّ عَتِيدٌ

**English**: And His escort will say, "This is what I have ready with me."

### Verse 50:24
**Arabic**: أَلْقِيَا فِى جَهَنَّمَ كُلَّ كَفَّارٍ عَنِيدٍۢ

**English**: "Throw into Hell every stubborn disbeliever.

### Verse 50:25
**Arabic**: مَّنَّاعٍۢ لِّلْخَيْرِ مُعْتَدٍۢ مُّرِيبٍ

**English**: Preventer of good, aggressor, doubter.

### Verse 50:26
**Arabic**: ٱلَّذِى جَعَلَ مَعَ ٱللَّهِ إِلَٰهًا ءَاخَرَ فَأَلْقِيَاهُ فِى ٱلْعَذَابِ ٱلشَّدِيدِ

**English**: Who fabricated another god with God; toss him into the intense agony."

### Verse 50:27
**Arabic**: ۞ قَالَ قَرِينُهُۥ رَبَّنَا مَآ أَطْغَيْتُهُۥ وَلَٰكِن كَانَ فِى ضَلَٰلٍۭ بَعِيدٍۢ

**English**: His escort will say, "Our Lord, I did not make him rebel, but he was far astray."

### Verse 50:28
**Arabic**: قَالَ لَا تَخْتَصِمُوا۟ لَدَىَّ وَقَدْ قَدَّمْتُ إِلَيْكُم بِٱلْوَعِيدِ

**English**: He will say, "Do not feud in My presence-I had warned you in advance.

### Verse 50:29
**Arabic**: مَا يُبَدَّلُ ٱلْقَوْلُ لَدَىَّ وَمَآ أَنَا۠ بِظَلَّٰمٍۢ لِّلْعَبِيدِ

**English**: The decree from Me will not be changed, and I am not unjust to the servants."

### Verse 50:30
**Arabic**: يَوْمَ نَقُولُ لِجَهَنَّمَ هَلِ ٱمْتَلَأْتِ وَتَقُولُ هَلْ مِن مَّزِيدٍۢ

**English**: On the Day when We will say to Hell, "Are you full?" And it will say, "Are there any more?"

### Verse 50:31
**Arabic**: وَأُزْلِفَتِ ٱلْجَنَّةُ لِلْمُتَّقِينَ غَيْرَ بَعِيدٍ

**English**: And Paradise will be brought closer to the pious, not far away.

### Verse 50:32
**Arabic**: هَٰذَا مَا تُوعَدُونَ لِكُلِّ أَوَّابٍ حَفِيظٍۢ

**English**: "This is what you were promised-for every careful penitent.

### Verse 50:33
**Arabic**: مَّنْ خَشِىَ ٱلرَّحْمَٰنَ بِٱلْغَيْبِ وَجَآءَ بِقَلْبٍۢ مُّنِيبٍ

**English**: Who inwardly feared the Most Gracious, and came with a repentant heart.

### Verse 50:34
**Arabic**: ٱدْخُلُوهَا بِسَلَٰمٍۢ ۖ ذَٰلِكَ يَوْمُ ٱلْخُلُودِ

**English**: Enter it in peace. This is the Day of Eternity."

### Verse 50:35
**Arabic**: لَهُم مَّا يَشَآءُونَ فِيهَا وَلَدَيْنَا مَزِيدٌۭ

**English**: Therein they will have whatever they desire-and We have even more.

### Verse 50:36
**Arabic**: وَكَمْ أَهْلَكْنَا قَبْلَهُم مِّن قَرْنٍ هُمْ أَشَدُّ مِنْهُم بَطْشًۭا فَنَقَّبُوا۟ فِى ٱلْبِلَٰدِ هَلْ مِن مَّحِيصٍ

**English**: How many generations before them, who were more powerful than they, did We destroy? They explored the lands-was there any escape?

### Verse 50:37
**Arabic**: إِنَّ فِى ذَٰلِكَ لَذِكْرَىٰ لِمَن كَانَ لَهُۥ قَلْبٌ أَوْ أَلْقَى ٱلسَّمْعَ وَهُوَ شَهِيدٌۭ

**English**: In that is a reminder for whoever possesses a heart, or cares to listen and witness.

### Verse 50:38
**Arabic**: وَلَقَدْ خَلَقْنَا ٱلسَّمَٰوَٰتِ وَٱلْأَرْضَ وَمَا بَيْنَهُمَا فِى سِتَّةِ أَيَّامٍۢ وَمَا مَسَّنَا مِن لُّغُوبٍۢ

**English**: We created the heavens and the earth and what is between them in six days, and no fatigue touched Us.

### Verse 50:39
**Arabic**: فَٱصْبِرْ عَلَىٰ مَا يَقُولُونَ وَسَبِّحْ بِحَمْدِ رَبِّكَ قَبْلَ طُلُوعِ ٱلشَّمْسِ وَقَبْلَ ٱلْغُرُوبِ

**English**: So endure what they say, and proclaim the praises of your Lord before the rising of the sun, and before sunset.

### Verse 50:40
**Arabic**: وَمِنَ ٱلَّيْلِ فَسَبِّحْهُ وَأَدْبَٰرَ ٱلسُّجُودِ

**English**: And glorify Him during the night, and at the end of devotions.

### Verse 50:41
**Arabic**: وَٱسْتَمِعْ يَوْمَ يُنَادِ ٱلْمُنَادِ مِن مَّكَانٍۢ قَرِيبٍۢ

**English**: And listen for the Day when the caller calls from a nearby place.

### Verse 50:42
**Arabic**: يَوْمَ يَسْمَعُونَ ٱلصَّيْحَةَ بِٱلْحَقِّ ۚ ذَٰلِكَ يَوْمُ ٱلْخُرُوجِ

**English**: The Day when they will hear the Shout in all truth. That is the Day of Emergence.

### Verse 50:43
**Arabic**: إِنَّا نَحْنُ نُحْىِۦ وَنُمِيتُ وَإِلَيْنَا ٱلْمَصِيرُ

**English**: It is We who control life and death, and to Us is the destination.

### Verse 50:44
**Arabic**: يَوْمَ تَشَقَّقُ ٱلْأَرْضُ عَنْهُمْ سِرَاعًۭا ۚ ذَٰلِكَ حَشْرٌ عَلَيْنَا يَسِيرٌۭ

**English**: The Day when the earth will crack for them at once. That is an easy gathering for Us.

### Verse 50:45
**Arabic**: نَّحْنُ أَعْلَمُ بِمَا يَقُولُونَ ۖ وَمَآ أَنتَ عَلَيْهِم بِجَبَّارٍۢ ۖ فَذَكِّرْ بِٱلْقُرْءَانِ مَن يَخَافُ وَعِيدِ

**English**: We are fully aware of what they say, and you are not a dictator over them. So remind by the Quran whoever fears My warning.
